Viognier around the world

24 editor-picked Viognier producers across 3 regions.

A guide to Viognier worth seeking out, by region. We list the estates, how each region expresses the grape, and the bottles that show it best.

Viognier in Rhone Valley

E. Guigal ★ 5.0

Cote-Rotie AOC, Condrieu AOC, Hermitage AOC, Cotes du Rhone AOCSyrah, Viognier, Marsanne, Roussanne, GrenacheFounded 1946Cote-Rotie

Ampuis-based negociant and domaine celebrated for the single-vineyard Cote-Rotie La La La trilogy from old Syrah vines.

Tasting: Visits and tastings at Chateau d'Ampuis by appointment

Signature wines: guigal-cote-rotie-la-mouline, guigal-cote-rotie-la-landonne, guigal-cote-rotie-la-turque

Tip: The La La La trilogy (La Mouline, La Landonne, La Turque) are allocated; the Chateau d'Ampuis Cote-Rotie is the most accessible flagship.

Chateau d'Ampuis, 69420 Ampuis, France → Book →

M. Chapoutier ★ 4.9

Hermitage AOC, Crozes-Hermitage AOC, Cotes du Rhone AOCSyrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, Grenache, ViognierFounded 1808Demeter CertifiedECOCERTHermitage

Tain-l'Hermitage negociant under biodynamic conversion since the 1990s, producing benchmark Hermitage Syrah and rare Marsanne whites.

Tasting: Open tasting room in Tain-l'Hermitage; cellar tours by appointment

Signature wines: chapoutier-ermitage-le-pavillon, chapoutier-ermitage-de-lore

Tip: The Braille labels on all bottles reflect the family's commitment to accessibility. The tasting room in town is walk-in friendly.

18 Avenue du Docteur Paul Durand, 26600 Tain-l'Hermitage, France → Book →

Domaine Jamet ★ 4.9

Cote-Rotie AOCSyrah, ViognierFounded 1976Cote-Rotie

Benchmark Cote-Rotie domaine producing a single assemblage from multiple Cote Brune and Cote Blonde terraces, known for depth and finesse.

Tasting: By appointment only at the domaine in Ampuis

Signature wines: jamet-cote-rotie

Tip: Jean-Paul and Jean-Luc Jamet blend all their lieux-dits into one estate Cote-Rotie that consistently ranks among the appellation's finest.

Le Vallin, 69420 Ampuis, France →

Rene Rostaing ★ 4.7

Cote-Rotie AOC, Condrieu AOCSyrah, ViognierFounded 1971Cote-Rotie

Ampuis domaine with parcels on La Landonne lieu-dit of the Cote Brune and the Cote Blonde, producing structured, terroir-expressive Cote-Rotie.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: rostaing-cote-rotie-la-landonne, rostaing-cote-rotie-cote-blonde

Tip: La Landonne is the most mineral and iron-tinged cuvee; La Vialliere is a more accessible early-drinker.

35 Route de la Cote, 69420 Ampuis, France →

Domaine Stephane Ogier ★ 4.8

Cote-Rotie AOC, Condrieu AOCSyrah, ViognierFounded 1997ECOCERTCote-Rotie

Modern Cote-Rotie producer crafting single-vineyard cuvees from the Cote Brune and Blonde with precision viticulture.

Tasting: Tastings by appointment at the domaine in Ampuis

Signature wines: ogier-cote-rotie-belle-helene, ogier-cote-rotie-lancement

Tip: The Belle Helene single-vine cuvee is among the most expressive Cote-Rotie bottlings of its generation.

3 Chemin du Bac, 69420 Ampuis, France →

Domaine Bonnefond ★ 4.5

Cote-Rotie AOC, Condrieu AOCSyrah, ViognierFounded 1980Cote-Rotie

Brothers Patrick and Christophe Bonnefond farm Cote-Rotie parcels on the Coteaux de Tupin, producing elegant, floral Syrah and classic Viognier.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: bonnefond-cote-rotie-coteaux-de-tupin

Le Moidon, 69420 Ampuis, France →

Pierre Gaillard ★ 4.5

Cote-Rotie AOC, Saint-Joseph AOC, Condrieu AOCSyrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, ViognierFounded 1987ECOCERTSaint-Joseph

Malleval domaine with vines across Cote-Rotie, Saint-Joseph and Condrieu, known for rich, concentrated Syrah with reliable terroir expression.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: gaillard-cote-rotie-esprit-de-blonde, gaillard-saint-joseph-clos-de-cuminal

42520 Malleval, Loire, France →

Yves Cuilleron ★ 4.7

Condrieu AOC, Saint-Joseph AOC, Cote-Rotie AOCViognier, Syrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1987ECOCERTCondrieu

Leading Condrieu domaine producing multiple single-vineyard Viognier bottlings alongside Cote-Rotie and Saint-Joseph.

Tasting: Open tasting room in Chavanay; appointment recommended

Signature wines: cuilleron-condrieu-les-chaillets, cuilleron-condrieu-la-petite-cote

Tip: Les Chaillets is the classic cuvee; Vertige is from the highest-altitude vineyards.

58 Route de Montchardon, 42410 Chavanay, France → Book →

Georges Vernay ★ 4.8

Condrieu AOC, Cote-Rotie AOCViognier, SyrahFounded 1953ECOCERTCondrieu

The domaine credited with saving Condrieu from near-extinction in the 1970s, now run by Christine Vernay with benchmark single-vineyard Viognier.

Tasting: Tasting room in Condrieu; open Monday to Saturday, appointments preferred

Signature wines: vernay-condrieu-coteau-de-vernon, vernay-condrieu-terrasses-de-lempire

Tip: Coteau de Vernon is the top cuvee from the oldest vines and is the most age-worthy Viognier in the appellation.

1 Route Nationale, 69420 Condrieu, France →

Andre Perret ★ 4.5

Condrieu AOC, Saint-Joseph AOCViognier, Syrah, MarsanneFounded 1984Condrieu

Chavanay domaine producing expressive Condrieu from the Coteau Chery lieu-dit, one of the appellation's most concentrated aromatic terroirs.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine in Chavanay

Signature wines: perret-condrieu-coteau-chery, perret-condrieu-clos-chanson

17 Route de la Taquete, 42410 Chavanay, France →

Domaine Francois Villard ★ 4.5

Condrieu AOC, Saint-Joseph AOC, Cote-Rotie AOCViognier, Syrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1991ECOCERTCondrieu

Saint-Michel-sur-Rhone domaine producing ambitious Northern Rhone whites and reds with low-intervention and site-specific focus.

Tasting: Tastings by appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: villard-condrieu-le-grand-vallon, villard-saint-joseph-le-grand-pompee

18 Petit Route de Romans, 42410 Saint-Michel-sur-Rhone, France →

Domaine Faury ★ 4.3

Condrieu AOC, Saint-Joseph AOCSyrah, Viognier, MarsanneFounded 1979ORGANIC CERTIFIEDSaint-Joseph

Chavanay family domaine across Condrieu and Saint-Joseph, producing clean, precise Viognier and Syrah with organic farming.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: faury-saint-joseph-rouge, faury-condrieu

La Ribaudy, 42410 Chavanay, France →

Tardieu-Laurent ★ 4.5

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOC, Gigondas AOC, Hermitage AOC, Cote-Rotie AOCGrenache, Syrah, Mourvedre, Marsanne, Roussanne, ViognierFounded 1994Chateauneuf-du-Pape

Negociant house sourcing old-vine Rhone fruit across both north and south for barrel-aged cuvees of concentration and depth.

Tasting: By appointment; offices in Lourmarin

Signature wines: tardieu-laurent-chateauneuf-vv, tardieu-laurent-gigondas

Les Grandes Bastides, Route de Cucuron, 84160 Lourmarin, France →

Famille Perrin ★ 4.3

Vinsobres AOC, Cotes du Rhone Villages AOC, Cotes du Rhone AOCGrenache, Syrah, Mourvedre, Grenache Blanc, ViognierFounded 1992Cotes du Rhone

The Perrin family secondary label (Beaucastel owners) producing Cotes du Rhone, Cotes du Rhone Villages and Vinsobres.

Tasting: Tasting room in Orange; open Monday to Saturday

Signature wines: famille-perrin-reserve-rouge, famille-perrin-les-sinards

Tip: La Vieille Ferme is the entry-level range; the Vinsobres Les Cornuds is a genuinely serious wine at a moderate price.

Route de Jonquieres, 84100 Orange, France → Book →

M. Chapoutier Tasting Cellar ★ 4.7

Syrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, Grenache, ViognierWalk-ins welcomeTain-l'Hermitage, Northern Rhône

M. Chapoutier's flagship tasting cellar at Tain-l'Hermitage, showcasing the full range from Crozes-Hermitage to iconic single-parcel Ermitage cuvees. Braille-labelled bottles displayed throughout.

18 avenue du Docteur Paul Durand, 26600 Tain-l'Hermitage, France →

E. Guigal Tasting Room ★ 4.9

Syrah, Viognier, Grenache, MourvèdreAppointment onlyAmpuis, Côte-Rôtie

E. Guigal's historic cellars and tasting room in Ampuis, home of the legendary La Landonne, La Mouline and La Turque single-vineyard Côte-Rôties. Guided cellar tour with vertical tasting available.

1 route de Bordeaux, 69420 Ampuis, France →

Delas Freres Tasting Cellar ★ 4.4

Syrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, ViognierAppointment onlySaint-Jean-de-Muzols, Saint-Joseph

Delas Freres winery and tasting space in Saint-Jean-de-Muzols offering guided visits through cellars with tastings of Saint-Joseph, Cornas and Hermitage single-parcel wines.

ZA de l'Olivet, 07300 Saint-Jean-de-Muzols, France →

Maison des Vins du Rhone Avignon ★ 4.4

Grenache, Syrah, Viognier, Marsanne, RoussanneWalk-ins welcomeCentre historique, Avignon

The Rhone Valley wine trade body's urban tasting bar in Avignon's historic centre. The rotating selection spans the full Rhone Valley from Condrieu to Chateauneuf-du-Pape with over 300 references.

6 rue des Trois Faucons, 84000 Avignon, France →

Domaine Yves Cuilleron Tasting Room ★ 4.6

Viognier, Syrah, Marsanne, RoussanneWalk-ins welcomeChavanay, Saint-Joseph / Condrieu

Yves Cuilleron's modern winery and tasting room in Chavanay at the southern edge of Condrieu appellation. Noted for expressive Condrieu whites and structured Saint-Joseph reds from old-vine Syrah.

58 route de Verlieu, 42410 Chavanay, France →

Domaine Romaneaux-Destezet ★ 4.8

Saint-Joseph AOC and Vin de FranceSyrah, Viognier, Marsanne, Serine (ancient Syrah clone)Founded 1998Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDSaint-Joseph AOC / Vin de France (Ardeche)

Herve Souhaut's micro-domaine in the Ardeche produces low-intervention Syrah from ancient Serine vines and Viognier on steep granite terraces. Cult status among natural-wine sommeliers worldwide.

Tip: Contact Herve directly for cellar-door visits; there is no public tasting room calendar. The estate is intentionally difficult to reach and the production is tiny.

Le Destezet, 07340 Ardeche, France →

Domaine Eric Texier ★ 4.7

Cotes du Rhone AOC and Vin de FranceSyrah, Viognier, Marsanne, GrenacheFounded 1998Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDCotes du Rhone / Breze

Eric Texier makes mineral, low-intervention northern Rhone whites and reds from obscure Ardeche parcels. Former nuclear engineer; cult reference for natural Rhone lovers.

Tip: Texier's wines are allocated through specialist importers. In France, La Cave des Papilles in Paris stocks the full range. The domaine itself does not maintain a public tasting room.

Breze, 07700 Boffres, Ardeche, France →

E. Guigal Cotes du Rhone blanc ★ 4.2

Cotes du Rhone AOCViognier, Grenache Blanc, MarsanneEUR 10-14 retailstill-white

Guigal's Cotes du Rhone blanc is Viognier-dominant, giving it apricot-blossom and stone-fruit aromatics at a fraction of the cost of Condrieu. Drink young for the freshest Viognier expression.

Tip: At under EUR15 this is one of the best-value Viognier-driven white wines in France. Serve chilled with a Provencal fish dish.

Viognier in Sicily

Baglio di Pianetto ★ 4.5

Sicilia DOCNero d'Avola, Syrah, Merlot, Cabernet Sauvignon, Inzolia, Catarratto, ViognierFounded 1997ORGANIC CERTIFIEDSicilia DOC

Founded in 1997 by Count Paolo Marzotto, Baglio di Pianetto sits at 900 metres near Santa Cristina Gela, 25 kilometres from Palermo. The estate blends Sicilian and French winemaking traditions across 106 hectares, with an elegant agrirelais for overnight stays.

Tasting: Private visits and introductory wine courses at the 20-room agrirelais. Monday to Friday 09:00 to 17:00 by appointment.

Signature wines: baglio-pianetto-ramione, baglio-pianetto-ficiligno, baglio-pianetto-viafrancia

Via Francia, 90030 Santa Cristina Gela (PA), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Viognier in Tuscany

Michele Satta ★ 4.6

Bolgheri DOC and Toscana IGTSangiovese, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Teroldego, Vermentino, Viognier27 haFounded 1983Michele Satta and familyWinemaker Michele SattaBolgheri DOC

Bolgheri's enduring artisan: Michele Satta arrived in 1974 and bottled his first wine in 1983. Notable for keeping a 100% Sangiovese (Cavaliere) on the Bordeaux-dominated coast.

Tasting: Cellar tours and tasting flights by appointment; Michele Satta personally hosts on advance booking.

Signature wines: michele-satta-piastraia, michele-satta-i-castagni, michele-satta-cavaliere

Scores: Wine Advocate 95 (2018), Vinous 93 (2018)

Localita Casone Ugolino 23, 57022 Castagneto Carducci, Livorno, Italy → Book →
