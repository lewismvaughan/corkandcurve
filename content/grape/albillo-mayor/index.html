Albillo Mayor around the world

11 editor-picked Albillo Mayor producers across 1 region.

A guide to Albillo Mayor worth seeking out, by region. We list the estates, how each region expresses the grape, and the bottles that show it best.

Albillo Mayor in Ribera del Duero

Dominio del Águila ★ 4.8

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo Mayor, Garnacha TintaFounded 2010ORGANIC CERTIFIEDLa Horra

Jorge Monzón's certified-organic estate at La Aguilera producing Pícaro del Águila rosé, Dominio del Águila and single-parcel Peñas Aladas from pre-phylloxera old vines.

La Aguilera, 09317 La Aguilera, Burgos, Spain →

Bodegas Protos ★ 4.6

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorFounded 1927Peñafiel

Founded in 1927 as a cooperative under Peñafiel Castle, Protos is the historic anchor of the appellation and the region's most-visited winery, housed in a spectacular Richard Rogers-designed cellar opened in 2008.

Tasting: The most-visited winery in Ribera del Duero, with guided tours open to the public since 2009; book via enoturismo@bodegasprotos.com or +34 659 843 463.

Tip: Combine the cellar tour with a visit to the castle-top Museo Provincial del Vino above.

C/ Bodegas Protos 24-28, 47300 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Goyo García Viadero ★ 4.7

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorFounded 2001Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalLa Horra

A natural-wine pioneer in the Burgos sector working with old pre-phylloxera Tinto Fino and Albillo Mayor vines, producing low-intervention wines with minimal sulfur under the Villalbilla and other labels.

Villalbilla de Gumiel, 09314 Villalbilla de Gumiel, Burgos, Spain →

Dominio de Atauta ★ 4.8

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorFounded 2000ORGANIC CERTIFIEDSoria Sub-zone

Specialists in extreme-altitude old-vine Tinto Fino from the Soria sub-zone at Atauta, where pre-phylloxera ungrafted vines at over 1000 metres produce the Llanos del Almendro and single-parcel La Mala cuvées.

Calle Real 21, 42312 Atauta, Soria, Spain →

Comenge ★ 4.5

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Cabernet Sauvignon, Albillo MayorORGANIC CERTIFIEDCuriel de Duero

An organic estate at Curiel de Duero producing Don Miguel Comenge, Familia Comenge and the Biberius entry range, plus an Albillo Mayor white and rosé from their castle-view vineyard overlooking the Duero.

Camino del Castillo s/n, 47316 Curiel de Duero, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodega Matarromera ★ 4.5

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorValbuena de Duero

Carlos Moro's estate at Valbuena de Duero producing Matarromera Crianza, Reserva and Gran Reserva alongside the Melior range, with vineyards between Olivares and Valbuena and a focus on balanced, elegant Tinto Fino.

Carretera Renedo-Pesquera km 30, 47359 Valbuena de Duero, Valladolid, Spain →

Pradorey ★ 4.5

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorLa Horra

A La Horra estate producing the Pradorey Élite, Reserva and the Albillo Mayor white, with modern winemaking in a 21st-century gravity-flow cellar and a hotel for wine-tourism stays.

Carretera de Gumiel km 1, 09311 La Horra, Burgos, Spain →

Bodegas Mas Que Vinos ★ 4.4

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorBiodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalValbuena de Duero

A biodynamic-practicing estate at Valbuena de Duero producing the Ercavio and Alma del Duero ranges from Tinto Fino and Albillo Mayor with a natural wine philosophy, minimal intervention and low added sulphur.

Calle Cabeza Gorda s/n, 47359 Valbuena de Duero, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodegas Emina ★ 4.4

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Cabernet Sauvignon, Albillo MayorPeñafiel

A large Peñafiel estate within the Matarromera group, producing the Emina Crianza, Reserva and single-vineyard Emina Atio, with a Centro de Interpretación Vitivinícola museum and hospitality complex on site.

Carretera de Valbuena km 5, 47315 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodegas Arzuaga Navarro ★ 4.6

Tempranillo, Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Albillo MayorWalk-ins welcomeGolden Mile

Golden Mile estate with hotel, spa, restaurant and wine bar under one roof. Tasting room open to walk-ins for by-the-glass pours from the Arzuaga DO Ribera del Duero range; guided cellar tours include vintage vertical flights on request.

Carretera N-122, Km 325, 47350 Quintanilla de Onesimo, Valladolid, Spain →

Dominio de Es ★ 4.7

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorAtauta, Soria

Bertrand Sourdais, who also makes Antidoto, launched Dominio de Es sourcing fruit from small pre-phylloxera parcelas around Atauta in Soria. The Vinas Viejas de Soria blends co-fermented Tinto Fino with old-vine Albillo Mayor, aged in Burgundy cooperage. Production is tiny and allocation-only. Decanter reviewed the 2017 vintage positively. One of the most distinctive expressions of the Soria sub-zone available outside Spain.

Tip: Available through specialist Spanish importers; check Voyageurs du Vin (France) and Decanter merchant links for international availability.

Atauta, 42345 Soria, Spain →
