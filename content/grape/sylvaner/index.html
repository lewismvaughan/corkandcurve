Sylvaner around the world

33 editor-picked Sylvaner producers across 1 region.

A guide to Sylvaner worth seeking out, by region. We list the estates, how each region expresses the grape, and the bottles that show it best.

Sylvaner in Alsace

Domaine Marcel Deiss ★ 4.9

Alsace AOC, Alsace Grand Cru (Altenberg de Bergheim, Schoenenbourg, Mambourg) and complantation field-blendsRiesling, Gewurztraminer, Pinot Gris, Muscat, Pinot Blanc, Pinot Noir, Sylvaner32 haFounded 1947Deiss family (Jean-Michel and Mathieu Deiss)Demeter CertifiedECOCERTRibeauville-Hunawihr-Bergheim

Bergheim estate that revived the Alsace climat tradition with co-planted Grand Cru field-blends, biodynamic since 1998 and Demeter certified, run by Mathieu Deiss with his father Jean-Michel.

Tasting: Cellar visits and tastings at the Bergheim domaine; bookings via the website.

Signature wines: altenberg-de-bergheim, schoenenbourg, burlenberg

Tip: Deiss bottles many of his Grands Crus as field-blends rather than single varietals; the Altenberg de Bergheim cuvee is a thirteen-variety complantation.
