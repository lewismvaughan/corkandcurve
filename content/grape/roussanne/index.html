Roussanne around the world

31 editor-picked Roussanne producers across 2 regions.

A guide to Roussanne worth seeking out, by region. We list the estates, how each region expresses the grape, and the bottles that show it best.

Roussanne in Priorat

Clos Figueras ★ 4.3

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Syrah, Cabernet Sauvignon, Garnacha Blanca, RoussanneFalset

A boutique producer with parcels across the DOQ Priorat, crafting Garnacha-dominant reds and small volumes of white from Garnacha Blanca and Roussanne.

Tasting: Visits by appointment in Falset.

Signature wines: font-de-la-figuera, clos-figueres

Carrer de la Bassa, s/n, 43730 Falset, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Roussanne in Rhone Valley

E. Guigal ★ 5.0

Cote-Rotie AOC, Condrieu AOC, Hermitage AOC, Cotes du Rhone AOCSyrah, Viognier, Marsanne, Roussanne, GrenacheFounded 1946Cote-Rotie

Ampuis-based negociant and domaine celebrated for the single-vineyard Cote-Rotie La La La trilogy from old Syrah vines.

Tasting: Visits and tastings at Chateau d'Ampuis by appointment

Signature wines: guigal-cote-rotie-la-mouline, guigal-cote-rotie-la-landonne, guigal-cote-rotie-la-turque

Tip: The La La La trilogy (La Mouline, La Landonne, La Turque) are allocated; the Chateau d'Ampuis Cote-Rotie is the most accessible flagship.

Chateau d'Ampuis, 69420 Ampuis, France → Book →

M. Chapoutier ★ 4.9

Hermitage AOC, Crozes-Hermitage AOC, Cotes du Rhone AOCSyrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, Grenache, ViognierFounded 1808Demeter CertifiedECOCERTHermitage

Tain-l'Hermitage negociant under biodynamic conversion since the 1990s, producing benchmark Hermitage Syrah and rare Marsanne whites.

Tasting: Open tasting room in Tain-l'Hermitage; cellar tours by appointment

Signature wines: chapoutier-ermitage-le-pavillon, chapoutier-ermitage-de-lore

Tip: The Braille labels on all bottles reflect the family's commitment to accessibility. The tasting room in town is walk-in friendly.

18 Avenue du Docteur Paul Durand, 26600 Tain-l'Hermitage, France → Book →

Paul Jaboulet Aine ★ 4.8

Hermitage AOC, Crozes-Hermitage AOC, Cotes du Rhone AOCSyrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, GrenacheFounded 1834ECOCERTCrozes-Hermitage

One of the Rhone's oldest negociants, producers of the iconic Hermitage La Chapelle Syrah from the hill's granite flanks in Tain.

Tasting: La Cave by appointment; wine shop open in Tain-l'Hermitage

Signature wines: jaboulet-hermitage-la-chapelle

Tip: La Chapelle from top vintages (1961, 1990, 2010) is among the most age-worthy wines in France.

25 Place du Taurobole, 26600 Tain-l'Hermitage, France → Book →

Pierre Gaillard ★ 4.5

Cote-Rotie AOC, Saint-Joseph AOC, Condrieu AOCSyrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, ViognierFounded 1987ECOCERTSaint-Joseph

Malleval domaine with vines across Cote-Rotie, Saint-Joseph and Condrieu, known for rich, concentrated Syrah with reliable terroir expression.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: gaillard-cote-rotie-esprit-de-blonde, gaillard-saint-joseph-clos-de-cuminal

42520 Malleval, Loire, France →

Yves Cuilleron ★ 4.7

Condrieu AOC, Saint-Joseph AOC, Cote-Rotie AOCViognier, Syrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1987ECOCERTCondrieu

Leading Condrieu domaine producing multiple single-vineyard Viognier bottlings alongside Cote-Rotie and Saint-Joseph.

Tasting: Open tasting room in Chavanay; appointment recommended

Signature wines: cuilleron-condrieu-les-chaillets, cuilleron-condrieu-la-petite-cote

Tip: Les Chaillets is the classic cuvee; Vertige is from the highest-altitude vineyards.

58 Route de Montchardon, 42410 Chavanay, France → Book →

Domaine Francois Villard ★ 4.5

Condrieu AOC, Saint-Joseph AOC, Cote-Rotie AOCViognier, Syrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1991ECOCERTCondrieu

Saint-Michel-sur-Rhone domaine producing ambitious Northern Rhone whites and reds with low-intervention and site-specific focus.

Tasting: Tastings by appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: villard-condrieu-le-grand-vallon, villard-saint-joseph-le-grand-pompee

18 Petit Route de Romans, 42410 Saint-Michel-sur-Rhone, France →

Domaine du Tunnel ★ 4.4

Saint-Peray AOC, Cornas AOCMarsanne, Roussanne, SyrahFounded 1994ORGANIC CERTIFIEDSaint-Peray

Saint-Peray specialist producing still and sparkling Marsanne alongside compelling Cornas from adjacent granite terraces.

Tasting: By appointment at the cellar in Saint-Peray

Signature wines: tunnel-saint-peray-marsanne, tunnel-cornas-pur-noir

20 Rue de la Republique, 07130 Saint-Peray, France →

Domaine Combier ★ 4.4

Crozes-Hermitage AOCSyrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1961Biodynamic PracticingECOCERTCrozes-Hermitage

Organic and biodynamic-practicing Crozes-Hermitage estate with the Clos des Grives single-vineyard Syrah as its standout red.

Tasting: Tasting room open weekdays; Saturday by appointment

Signature wines: combier-crozes-clos-des-grives

Tip: Among the most reliable value-for-quality bottlings in the Northern Rhone; biodynamic farming at accessible prices.

Route de Larnage, 26600 Pont-de-l'Isere, France → Book →

Domaine Alain Graillot ★ 4.6

Crozes-Hermitage AOCSyrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1985Crozes-Hermitage

Alain Graillot elevated the Crozes-Hermitage appellation's reputation for serious Syrah beginning in 1985; La Guiraude is the old-vine prestige cuvee.

Tasting: Visits by appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: graillot-crozes-hermitage-rouge, graillot-crozes-hermitage-la-guiraude

Tip: The straightforward Crozes rouge is one of the best value reds in the Northern Rhone.

Les Chenes Verts, 26600 Pont-de-l'Isere, France →

Domaine Belle ★ 4.4

Crozes-Hermitage AOC, Hermitage AOCSyrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1990ECOCERTCrozes-Hermitage

Larnage Crozes-Hermitage estate with parcels on the Hermitage hill, known for purity of fruit and mineral expression.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine in Larnage

Signature wines: belle-crozes-hermitage-les-pierrelles, belle-crozes-hermitage-cuvee-louis-belle

Le Mas, 26600 Larnage, France →

Domaine Gonon ★ 4.8

Saint-Joseph AOCSyrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1963Biodynamic PracticingECOCERTSaint-Joseph

Mauves reference estate for Saint-Joseph, run by brothers Pierre and Jean Gonon on old-vine granite parcels; one of the most elegant Saint-Josephs made.

Tasting: By appointment only at the estate in Mauves

Signature wines: gonon-saint-joseph-rouge, gonon-saint-joseph-blanc

Tip: Wines sell out quickly. The Saint-Joseph blanc from Marsanne and Roussanne is a rare and beautiful white.

10 Rue des Launays, 07300 Mauves, France →

Domaine des Lises (Maxime Graillot) ★ 4.5

Crozes-Hermitage AOCSyrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 2012ECOCERTCrozes-Hermitage

Maxime Graillot's own domaine in Mercurol. Old-vine Crozes-Hermitage parcels farmed organically with a site-specific focus.

Tasting: By appointment at the domaine

Signature wines: maxime-graillot-crozes-les-lises

Tip: A producer to follow; each vintage shows increasing terroir precision and elegance.

Route de Romans, 26600 Mercurol, France →

Chateau de Beaucastel ★ 5.0

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOCGrenache, Mourvedre, Syrah, Counoise, Cinsault, Roussanne, Grenache Blanc, ClairetteFounded 1549Demeter CertifiedECOCERTChateauneuf-du-Pape

The Perrin family estate at Courthezon, one of the oldest and most biologically farmed CdP properties, using all 13 permitted varieties and producing the benchmark Roussanne Vieilles Vignes blanc.

Tasting: Visits and tastings by appointment at the estate; wine shop on site

Signature wines: beaucastel-chateauneuf-rouge, beaucastel-roussanne-vieilles-vignes, beaucastel-hommage-a-jacques-perrin

Tip: The Hommage a Jacques Perrin is the prestige red; the Roussanne Vieilles Vignes white is the estate's flagship white wine.

Chemin Gironde, 84350 Courthezon, France → Book →

Clos des Papes ★ 4.9

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOCGrenache, Mourvedre, Syrah, Vaccarese, Counoise, Roussanne, ClairetteFounded 1896ORGANIC CERTIFIEDChateauneuf-du-Pape

Paul-Vincent Avril's family estate, one of Chateauneuf-du-Pape's most consistent producers, making densely structured reds from a large parcel mosaic.

Tasting: By appointment at the estate in Chateauneuf-du-Pape

Signature wines: clos-des-papes-rouge, clos-des-papes-blanc

Tip: The red uses Mourvedre more generously than most CdP estates, lending dark-earth, game-meat character.

13 Avenue Saint-Pierre de Luxembourg, 84230 Chateauneuf-du-Pape, France →

Domaine de la Janasse ★ 4.7

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOC, Cotes du Rhone AOCGrenache, Syrah, Mourvedre, Counoise, Clairette, Roussanne, Grenache BlancFounded 1973Chateauneuf-du-Pape

Courthezon family estate producing approachable and reliably excellent Chateauneuf-du-Pape including the old-vine Chaupin Grenache cuvee.

Tasting: Tasting room open Monday to Saturday at the domaine

Signature wines: janasse-chateauneuf-chaupin, janasse-chateauneuf-vieilles-vignes

Tip: Cuvee Chaupin from a single old-vine Grenache parcel is a must-try.

29 Chemin du Moulin, 84350 Courthezon, France → Book →

Clos Saint-Jean ★ 4.6

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOCGrenache, Syrah, Mourvedre, Counoise, Clairette, RoussanneFounded 1955Chateauneuf-du-Pape

Chateauneuf-du-Pape estate run by brothers Pascal and Vincent Maurel, producing concentrated cuvees including the rare Deus ex Machina prestige bottling.

Tasting: Tastings by appointment at the estate

Signature wines: clos-saint-jean-deus-ex-machina, clos-saint-jean-la-combe-des-fous

Grande Rue, 84230 Chateauneuf-du-Pape, France →

Tardieu-Laurent ★ 4.5

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOC, Gigondas AOC, Hermitage AOC, Cote-Rotie AOCGrenache, Syrah, Mourvedre, Marsanne, Roussanne, ViognierFounded 1994Chateauneuf-du-Pape

Negociant house sourcing old-vine Rhone fruit across both north and south for barrel-aged cuvees of concentration and depth.

Tasting: By appointment; offices in Lourmarin

Signature wines: tardieu-laurent-chateauneuf-vv, tardieu-laurent-gigondas

Les Grandes Bastides, Route de Cucuron, 84160 Lourmarin, France →

Chateau La Nerthe ★ 4.7

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOCGrenache, Syrah, Mourvedre, Counoise, Roussanne, Clairette, Grenache BlancFounded 1560ECOCERTChateauneuf-du-Pape

One of the oldest estates in CdP with winemaking cited to the 16th century, producing refined GSM-led reds and whites from organic farming.

Tasting: Open tasting room and shop at the chateau; cellar tours by appointment

Signature wines: nerthe-chateauneuf-rouge, nerthe-cuvee-des-cadettes

Tip: The Cuvee des Cadettes white from very old Roussanne and Clairette vines is a rare and age-worthy white CdP.

4213 Route de Sorgues, 84230 Chateauneuf-du-Pape, France → Book →

Delas Freres ★ 4.5

AOCSyrah, Marsanne, RoussanneFounded 1835Hermitage

Delas Freres, founded 1835 in Tain-l'Hermitage, is a leading Northern Rhone negociant producing benchmark Hermitage rouge, Les Bessards cuvee, and Saint-Joseph.

Tasting: By appointment at the Delas Freres cellars in Tain-l'Hermitage

Signature wines: delas-hermitage-les-bessards, delas-hermitage-domaine-des-tourettes

Tip: The Les Bessards single-parcel Hermitage is the flagship; Saint-Joseph Sainte-Epine is excellent value.

40 Rue Jules Nadi, 26600 Tain-l'Hermitage, France →

M. Chapoutier Tasting Cellar ★ 4.7

Syrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, Grenache, ViognierWalk-ins welcomeTain-l'Hermitage, Northern Rhône

M. Chapoutier's flagship tasting cellar at Tain-l'Hermitage, showcasing the full range from Crozes-Hermitage to iconic single-parcel Ermitage cuvees. Braille-labelled bottles displayed throughout.

18 avenue du Docteur Paul Durand, 26600 Tain-l'Hermitage, France →

Delas Freres Tasting Cellar ★ 4.4

Syrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, ViognierAppointment onlySaint-Jean-de-Muzols, Saint-Joseph

Delas Freres winery and tasting space in Saint-Jean-de-Muzols offering guided visits through cellars with tastings of Saint-Joseph, Cornas and Hermitage single-parcel wines.

ZA de l'Olivet, 07300 Saint-Jean-de-Muzols, France →

Chateau de Beaucastel Tasting Room ★ 4.8

Grenache, Mourvèdre, Syrah, Cinsault, RoussanneAppointment onlyCourthezon, Chateauneuf-du-Pape

The Perrin family estate in Courthezon, one of only two Chateauneuf-du-Pape estates to use all 13 permitted grape varieties. Guided tours of historic cellars with structured tastings of the estate range.

Chemin de Beaucastel, 84350 Courthezon, France →

Maison Brotte Espace Viniculture ★ 4.3

Grenache, Syrah, Mourvèdre, Roussanne, ClairetteWalk-ins welcomeChateauneuf-du-Pape

Maison Brotte's purpose-built visitor centre next to the Pere Anselme Wine Museum. Wide range of Chateauneuf-du-Pape, Gigondas, Vacqueyras and Cotes du Rhone open for daily tasting.

Chemin de la Gironde, 84230 Chateauneuf-du-Pape, France →

Maison des Vins du Rhone Avignon ★ 4.4

Grenache, Syrah, Viognier, Marsanne, RoussanneWalk-ins welcomeCentre historique, Avignon

The Rhone Valley wine trade body's urban tasting bar in Avignon's historic centre. The rotating selection spans the full Rhone Valley from Condrieu to Chateauneuf-du-Pape with over 300 references.

6 rue des Trois Faucons, 84000 Avignon, France →

Clos des Papes Tasting Room ★ 4.8

Grenache, Mourvèdre, Syrah, Vaccarese, RoussanneAppointment onlyChateauneuf-du-Pape village

The Avril family's Clos des Papes in the village of Chateauneuf-du-Pape. Known for consistently scoring among the appellation's highest-rated estates, offering intimate visits to their village chai.

13 avenue Pierre de Luxembourg, 84230 Chateauneuf-du-Pape, France →

Cave de Tain Tasting Centre ★ 4.1

Syrah, Marsanne, Roussanne, GrenacheWalk-ins welcomeTain-l'Hermitage, Northern Rhône

Cave de Tain cooperative's visitor centre and tasting room, one of Northern Rhone's largest producers. Full range from Crozes-Hermitage to Hermitage and Cornas, including the Nobles Rives prestige tier.

22 route de Larnage, 26600 Tain-l'Hermitage, France →

Domaine Yves Cuilleron Tasting Room ★ 4.6

Viognier, Syrah, Marsanne, RoussanneWalk-ins welcomeChavanay, Saint-Joseph / Condrieu

Yves Cuilleron's modern winery and tasting room in Chavanay at the southern edge of Condrieu appellation. Noted for expressive Condrieu whites and structured Saint-Joseph reds from old-vine Syrah.

58 route de Verlieu, 42410 Chavanay, France →

Chateau Mont-Redon Tasting Room ★ 4.3

Grenache, Syrah, Mourvèdre, Cinsault, RoussanneWalk-ins welcomeChateauneuf-du-Pape

One of the oldest and largest estates in Chateauneuf-du-Pape with a welcoming daily tasting room. The Fabre-Diffre family has owned the property since 1923, and the tasting covers both Chateauneuf-du-Pape and Lirac.

Route de l'Orange, 84230 Chateauneuf-du-Pape, France →

Domaine du Tunnel (Stephane Robert) ★ 4.7

Saint-Peray AOC, Cornas AOCMarsanne, Roussanne, SyrahFounded 1998ORGANIC CERTIFIEDSaint-Peray AOC and Cornas AOC

Stephane Robert's tiny domaine produces the finest Saint-Peray sparkling Marsanne and a remarkable Cornas from certified-organic vines. One of the northern Rhone's best-kept secrets.

Tip: The sparkling Saint-Peray Methode Traditionnelle is produced in tiny quantities and is almost impossible to find in export markets. Visit the cellar door in Saint-Peray for direct allocation.

20 rue de la Republique, 07130 Saint-Peray, Ardeche, France →

Domaine La Janasse ★ 4.8

Chateauneuf-du-Pape AOCGrenache, Mourvedre, Syrah, Grenache Blanc, Clairette, RoussanneFounded 1967ORGANIC CERTIFIEDChateauneuf-du-Pape AOC

Aimé and Christophe Sabon's organically-farmed Chateauneuf-du-Pape estate is beloved by local sommeliers for quality that outpunches its price, particularly the old-vine Grenache cuvees.

Tip: The Cuvee Chaupin white (old-vine Grenache Blanc, Clairette, Roussanne) is among the best whites in the appellation. Visit by appointment from Avignon or Courthezon.

27 chemin du Moulin, 84350 Courthezon, Vaucluse, France →
