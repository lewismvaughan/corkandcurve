Wine in Colmar old town, Alsace

12 editor picks across 6 categories

Wine Bars in Colmar old town

L'Un des Sens ★ 4.6

180 by the glassColmar old town

Natural-wine cave a manger on Rue Berthe Molly run by chef Romain Leichtweis and Manon. Around 450 bottles, 180 by-the-glass references and Michelin-trained share plates.

The list: More than 450 natural-leaning wines from across France and Europe, with a deep Alsace by-the-glass list run by chef Romain Leichtweis and Manon, paired with tapas and seasonal share plates

Pours: Alsace Riesling, Pinot Noir d'Alsace, natural Pinot Gris, Cremant d'Alsace

18 Rue Berthe Molly, 68000 Colmar, France →

Le Cercle des Aromes ★ 4.5

200 by the glassColmar old town

Wine bar on Rue Schongauer pouring more than 200 wines by the glass with a deep Alsace selection. Run by sommeliers focused on grower-direct and biodynamic Alsace producers.

The list: More than 200 wines by the glass with a strong Alsace backbone, including Grand Cru and biodynamic growers, paired with sharing plates of cheese, charcuterie and seasonal small plates

Pours: Alsace Grand Cru Riesling, Pinot Gris vendanges tardives, Cremant d'Alsace, Gewurztraminer

1 Rue Schongauer, 68000 Colmar, France →

Cafe des Marchands ★ 4.3

16 by the glassColmar old town

Wine and beer bar in a half-timbered house on pedestrian Rue des Marchands, opposite the Maison Pfister. Quiet terrace with Alsace-focused glass list and French sharing plates.

The list: Wine and craft beer bar with a curated Alsace by-the-glass selection of Riesling, Pinot Gris and Pinot Noir alongside cheese and charcuterie boards on a quiet pedestrian terrace

Pours: Alsace Riesling, Pinot Noir, Cremant d'Alsace

9 Rue des Marchands, 68000 Colmar, France →

Colmar old town

Wine Hotels in Colmar old town

La Maison des Tetes ★ 4.7

EUR 280-650 per nightColmar old town

Five-star Relais et Chateaux hotel inside the 1609 Renaissance Maison des Tetes in Colmar, home to chef Eric Girardin's one-Michelin-star restaurant and a historic Alsatian brasserie.

The list: Restaurant Girardin Michelin-star tasting menu; Brasserie Historique with Alsace by-the-glass; concierge-arranged cellar visits

19 Rue des Tetes, 68000 Colmar, France → Book →

Hotel Quatorze ★ 4.5

EUR 200-380 per nightColmar old town

Boutique 14-key hotel in a former 19th-century pharmacy on Rue des Augustins, steps from Petite Venise and Musee Unterlinden. Curated Alsace wine fridge and concierge tasting bookings.

The list: In-room curated Alsace minibar; concierge bookings at Wolfberger, Hugel and Trimbach cellars

14 Rue des Augustins, 68000 Colmar, France → Book →

Hotel Saint-Martin ★ 4.2

EUR 110-230 per nightColmar old town

Three-star Logis hotel inside a 14th-century Colmar mansion on Grand Rue, central walkable base for Petite Venise and Place de la Cathedrale. Wine boutiques and tasting rooms nearby.

The list: Concierge tastings at La Sommeliere, Vinum and the Musee des Vins d'Alsace; walking distance to Le Cercle des Aromes

38 Grand Rue, 68000 Colmar, France → Book →
