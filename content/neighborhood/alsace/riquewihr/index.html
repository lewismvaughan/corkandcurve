Wine in Riquewihr, Alsace

7 editor picks across 5 categories

Tasting Rooms in Riquewihr

Famille Hugel Tasting Room ★ 4.7

Riesling, Gewurztraminer, Pinot Gris, Pinot NoirWalk-ins welcomeRiquewihr

Twelve generations of Hugel since 1639 on the main street of Riquewihr, with cellar tours through the 17th-century barrel hall and tastings of the Classic, Estate and Grossi Laue ranges.

3 Rue de la 1ere Armee, 68340 Riquewihr, France →

Dopff au Moulin ★ 4.4

Cremant d'Alsace, Riesling, Pinot Blanc, GewurztraminerWalk-ins welcomeRiquewihr

Dopff family since 1574 at the foot of Grand Cru Schoenenbourg, the cellar that pioneered Cremant d'Alsace in 1900. Tour the foudre hall and taste the sparkling and still ranges.

2 Avenue Jacques Preiss, 68340 Riquewihr, France →

Wine Bars in Riquewihr

Maison Zimmer ★ 4.3

10 by the glassRiquewihr

Family producer on the main street of Riquewihr with a 16th-century vaulted cellar, commented tastings of Schoenenbourg Riesling and the Winstub Au Tire-Bouchon next door.

The list: Estate-owned 16th-century cellar tasting bar with three-wine commented tastings of the Zimmer range plus a Winstub Au Tire-Bouchon serving Alsace classics with the same wine list

Pours: Riesling Schoenenbourg, Gewurztraminer, Pinot Gris, Cremant

33 Rue du General de Gaulle, 68340 Riquewihr, France →

Wine Restaurants in Riquewihr

La Table du Gourmet ★ 4.7

One-Michelin-star and Michelin Green Star restaurant by chef Jean-Luc Brendel in a 16th-century half-timbered house, hyper-seasonal vegetable-driven cuisine and biodynamic Alsace cellarRiquewihr

Jean-Luc Brendel's one-Michelin-star (and Green Star) restaurant in a 16th-century half-timbered house in Riquewihr. Vegetable-led tasting menus and biodynamic Alsace pairings.

Awards: Michelin 2025, Michelin Green Star 2025, Gault Millau 2025

5 Rue de la 1ere Armee Francaise, 68340 Riquewihr, France →

Winstub Au Tire-Bouchon ★ 4.2

Zimmer family winstub in central Riquewihr, traditional tartes flambees, fish choucroute and foie gras paired with the estate's Schoenenbourg Riesling and GewurztraminerRiquewihr

Zimmer-family winstub on Riquewihr's main street since the early 1970s. Tartes flambees, fish choucroute and foie gras paired with estate Schoenenbourg Riesling.

Awards: Visit Alsace listing 2025

29 Rue du General de Gaulle, 68340 Riquewihr, France →
