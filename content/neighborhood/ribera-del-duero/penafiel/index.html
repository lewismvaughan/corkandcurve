Vineyards in Penafiel

Bodegas Protos ★ 4.6

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorFounded 1927Peñafiel

Founded in 1927 as a cooperative under Peñafiel Castle, Protos is the historic anchor of the appellation and the region's most-visited winery, housed in a spectacular Richard Rogers-designed cellar opened in 2008.

Tasting: The most-visited winery in Ribera del Duero, with guided tours open to the public since 2009; book via enoturismo@bodegasprotos.com or +34 659 843 463.

Tip: Combine the cellar tour with a visit to the castle-top Museo Provincial del Vino above.

C/ Bodegas Protos 24-28, 47300 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Pago de Carraovejas ★ 4.8

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Cabernet Sauvignon, MerlotFounded 1987Peñafiel

A landmark estate near Peñafiel famous for its Pago de Carraovejas Crianza and Reserva and the single-vineyard El Anejon, with the Michelin-starred Ambivium restaurant drawing visitors from across Castile.

Tasting: Wine tourism and cellar visits by appointment; on-site Ambivium restaurant holds a Michelin star.

Camino Carraovejas s/n, 47316 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodegas Tamaral ★ 4.3

DO Ribera del DueroTinto FinoPeñafiel

A modern estate near Peñafiel crafting value-driven Tinto Fino wines in Crianza, Reserva and Finca la Meda styles, widely available at Spanish restaurants and a good introduction to the appellation.

Carretera N-122, km 310.5, 47315 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Viñas del Jaro ★ 4.3

DO Ribera del DueroTinto FinoPeñafiel

A Peñafiel area estate producing the Jaro and Pago de los Capellanes sibling ranges, known for well-structured Crianza and Reserva at accessible price points from the Valladolid sector.

Ctra. Peñafiel-Valoria km 1, 47316 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Resalte de Peñafiel ★ 4.4

DO Ribera del DueroTinto FinoFounded 1997Peñafiel

A Peñafiel estate producing the Resalte Gran Resalte, Crianza and Viña Resalte lines from estate Tinto Fino vineyards at the foot of Peñafiel castle, with a tasting room open to visitors.

Avenida de la Constitución 10, 47300 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Convento de Oreja ★ 4.3

DO Ribera del DueroTinto FinoPeñafiel

An estate near Peñafiel producing Convento de Oreja Crianza, Reserva and Selección from Tinto Fino, named for the historic convent that once occupied the site and known for their accessible, well-balanced style.

Calle Convento s/n, 47315 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodegas Emina ★ 4.4

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Cabernet Sauvignon, Albillo MayorPeñafiel

A large Peñafiel estate within the Matarromera group, producing the Emina Crianza, Reserva and single-vineyard Emina Atio, with a Centro de Interpretación Vitivinícola museum and hospitality complex on site.

Carretera de Valbuena km 5, 47315 Peñafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Penafiel

Producer-led wine education workshops at Bodegas Protos in Penafiel. Sessions cover sensory analysis, vertical tastings of the Protos Crianza-to-Gran-Reserva range, and the influence of the Richard Rogers architecture on visitor understanding of wine production. Penafiel

Comprehensive wine and terroir education programme at Pago de Carraovejas. Wine Tours in Penafiel

Penafiel Castle and Provincial Wine Museum Guided Tour ★ 4.6

Penafiel

Combined visit to the 15th-century Penafiel Castle and the Provincial Wine Museum housed within it. Museum designed by architect Roberto Valle Gonzalez covers three floors of winemaking, ethnographic and historical exhibits. Ground floor tasting room offers guided samplings of DO Ribera del Duero wines; free shuttle from town centre at weekends.

Museo del Vino, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Pago de Carraovejas 6 Senses Wine and Vineyard Tour ★ 4.8

Penafiel

One of Ribera del Duero's leading wine tourism programmes at the Pago de Carraovejas estate in Penafiel. The 6 Senses experience covers six stages from vineyard to barrel room; the extended version pairs each stage with dishes from the one-Michelin-star Ambivium kitchen. Launched 2013.

Camino de Carraovejas s/n, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Wine Museums in Penafiel

Museo Provincial del Vino de Valladolid ★ 4.7

Penafiel

The foremost wine museum in Ribera del Duero, installed within the 15th-century Penafiel Castle on a strategic hill overlooking three valleys. Designed by architect Roberto Valle Gonzalez across three floors covering winemaking, ethnographic and historical exhibits linked to wine and castle history. The ground-floor tasting room offers guided samplings of DO Ribera del Duero wines. Free shuttle coaches operate to the castle on weekends and holidays.

Museo del Vino, Castillo de Penafiel, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodegas Protos Heritage and Architecture Visitor Centre ★ 4.4

Penafiel

The visitor centre of Bodegas Protos in Penafiel presents the winery's history from its 1927 cooperative origins alongside the story of the Richard Rogers building (inaugurated 2008). Tour of the 14th-century underground aging cellars beneath Penafiel Castle forms the heritage core; the new Rogers building contextualises the architectural evolution of the cooperative. The winery is one of the founding producers of the DO Ribera del Duero.

C/ Bodegas Protos 24-28, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Penafiel

Nightlife in Penafiel

Agora de Protos Wine Bar ★ 4.3

15 by the glassPeñafiel

The wine bar component of the Agora de Protos complex occupies the original 1927 Bodegas Protos bodega in Peñafiel, beneath the hilltop castle. Serves the full Protos range (Roble through Reserva) alongside charcuterie and creative snacks. The only wine bar in Spain with Pritzker Prize-winning architecture (Richard Rogers) overhead. Evening service available; restaurant closes 15:30 but bar remains open for tapas and wine.

The list: Full Bodegas Protos range; wine shop attached

Av. Escalona, 12, 47300 Peñafiel, Valladolid →

Penafiel Castle Wine Museum Evening Bar ★ 4.4

12 by the glassPenafiel

The Museo Provincial del Vino at Penafiel Castle operates a tasting bar on the upper floors with views across the Duero valley and Cerro de la Muela hill. In the summer months, evening extended hours allow visitors to taste Ribera del Duero wines in the museum setting with sunset and dusk castle views. The museum's permanent collection of 13 zones dedicated to Ribera del Duero winemaking creates a context-rich drinking environment unlike any standard wine bar.

The list: DO Ribera del Duero wines served in the museum tasting bar; varied producers

Castillo de Peñafiel, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid →

La Olmilla Traditional Bodega Wine Bar ★ 4.3

8 by the glassPenafiel

After the formal guided cellar tour, La Olmilla's ground-floor bar serves local wines poured from traditional clay jars alongside aperitivo snacks. The setting - a traditional subterranean bodega beneath Peñafiel's historic core - makes this one of the more atmospheric evening drinking spots in the DO. The background ambience of the medieval stone cellar and the sounds of the nearby castle create a uniquely Castilian wine-drinking mood.

The list: DO Ribera del Duero wines in traditional clay service; aperitivo plates

Calle Olmilla, 2, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid →

Bodegas Protos Evening Cellar Tour ★ 4.4

3 by the glassPeñafiel

Bodegas Protos offers evening cellar tours and wine tastings beneath the Peñafiel castle, combining the Richard Rogers-designed contemporary winery with the underground historical cave system. Visits include the underground ageing galleries (constant cool temperature), the cooperage, and the Roger's building barrel hall. Evening slots allow sunset views of the castle from the winery terrace before descending into the caves for the tasting.

The list: Protos Crianza, Reserva, and Gran Reserva tasting; 2-3 wines with tour

Calle Bodegas Protos 24-28, 47300 Peñafiel, Valladolid →

La Olmilla Underground Cellar Evening Tasting ★ 4.4

3 by the glassPenafiel

La Olmilla offers scheduled evening guided visits to its traditional underground bodega beneath the streets of Peñafiel, where wine is tasted from traditional clay jars in the cool cave environment. The cellar was originally excavated by the town's ancestors for wine storage and preservation; the evening session recreates this historical practice. Sessions run throughout the year with the highest demand in summer and harvest months. Book directly or via the Peñafiel tourist office.

The list: DO Ribera del Duero wines served from clay jars; local producer selection

Calle Olmilla, 2, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid →
