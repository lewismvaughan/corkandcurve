Vineyards in Durnstein

Domäne Wachau ★ 4.6

Wachau DACGrüner Veltliner, Riesling440 haFounded 1938ORGANIC CERTIFIEDDürnstein

Wachau's largest estate, representing around 250 vintner families across 440 hectares. Founded 1938 as Winzergenossenschaft Wachau, renamed Domäne Wachau in 2008. Roman Horvath MW oversees the range alongside Heinz Frischengruber.

Tasting: Vinothek in Dürnstein open to visitors; tastings and direct sales. The Kellerschlössel Baroque cellar palace hosts tours.

Signature wines: domaene-wachau-achleiten-gruner-smaragd, domaene-wachau-kellerberg-riesling-smaragd, domaene-wachau-terrasen-federspiel-gruner

Tip: The Achleiten and Kellerberg Smaragd bottlings compete directly with the great single-estate wines at a more accessible price.

Dürnstein 107, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria → Book →

Weingut Konrad ★ 4.0

Wachau DACGrüner Veltliner, Riesling7 haFounded 1930Dürnstein

Dürnstein estate below the famous blue Baroque church tower and castle ruins. Works Loibenberg and Hollerin parcels producing Grüner Veltliner and Riesling.

Tasting: Direct cellar sales; by appointment.

Tip: Buy a bottle and walk up to the castle ruins for a Wachau picnic with an exceptional Danube view.

Dürnstein 58, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria →

Distilleries in Durnstein

Domäne Wachau - Wachauer Marillenbrand ★ 4.5

Founded 1938Dürnstein

The estate distillery of Domäne Wachau produces a noble, clear Marillenbrand distilled from sweet ripe Wachau apricots grown on the estate's south-bank orchards. The apricot brandy is available at the Vinothek shop alongside the estate wines.

Tip: The Marillenbrand can be purchased directly from the Vinothek shop at cellar-door prices; the apricot brandy pairs with a vineyard picnic from the estate's Wine.Vineyard.Picnic programme.

Domäne Wachau Nr. 107, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria →

The Dürnstein retail outlet of Bailoni, the first Wachau apricot distillery. Stocking the full Bailoni range of apricot liqueurs, brandies and frizzante products produced at the Krems-Stein distillery. Conveniently located in central Dürnstein for wine tourists.

One of two Dürnstein retail outlets of Wieser Wachau, the Wösendorf farm distillery. Sells the full range of Wieser apricot spirits, preserves, single-origin coffee and natural cosmetics. Convenient walk-in retail during the Dürnstein visitor season.

Dietary in Durnstein

Wachau DACGruner Veltliner, RieslingORGANIC CERTIFIEDDurnstein

The Domane Wachau cooperative has been progressively converting member vineyard plots to certified organic management and producing specific cuvees from these plots under organic certification. Current certification body and specific cuvees should be confirmed on the estate website.

Domane Wachau (vegan winemaking on selected cuvees) ★ 4.1

Wachau DACGruner Veltliner, RieslingDurnstein

Domane Wachau documents vegan-compatible fining agents (bentonite clay rather than animal-derived products) on selected cuvees. Specific bottles carrying the vegan indication are identified on the estate website. Verify the current cuvee list directly.
