Tasting Rooms in Johannisberg

Vinothek Schloss Johannisberg ★ 4.7

RieslingWalk-ins welcomeJohannisberg, Geisenheim

Hilltop Vinothek of Schloss Johannisberg above the Rhine in Geisenheim, where Riesling was first documented as a varietal name on the 1720 estate. Tastings of the estate's Riesling range with views over the slopes.
