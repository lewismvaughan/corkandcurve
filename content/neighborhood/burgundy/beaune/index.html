Wine Festivals in Beaune

Hospices de Beaune Wine Auction ★ 4.9

Beaune

The world's most famous charity wine auction, held in the Halles de Beaune on the third Sunday of November since 1859. Barrels of the latest Hospices cuvees from Cote de Beaune and Cote de Nuits parcels sell to fund the Beaune hospital.

Tip: The public Festivinales tastings around the town run the three days before the Sunday sale; book Beaune accommodation a year ahead as the Trois Glorieuses weekend fills the whole Cote d'Or.
