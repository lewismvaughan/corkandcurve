Tasting Rooms in Pinhao, Cima Corgo

Quinta do Bomfim ★ 4.7

Touriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaAppointment onlyPinhão, Cima Corgo

Dow's home estate on the riverbank at Pinhão, farmed by the Symington family for five generations. The 1896 lodge tour ends with Douro DOC, Port or Old Tawny flights over the river.

Quinta do Bomfim, 5085-060 Pinhão, Portugal →

Quinta das Carvalhas ★ 4.6

Touriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta FranciscaAppointment onlyPinhão, Cima Corgo

Real Companhia Velha's flagship estate facing Pinhão, the largest single quinta in the Douro. Tastings span the white, Vinhas Velhas red, LBV and 20-Year Tawny with cheese and cold cuts.

Quinta das Carvalhas, 5085-034 Pinhão, Portugal →

Hidden Gems in Pinhao, Cima Corgo

Quinta de la Rosa ★ 4.4

Douro DOCTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta RorizFounded 1988Bergqvist familyPinhao, Cima Corgo

A family-owned working estate on the Douro near Pinhao, making fine reds and Ports under winemaker Jorge Moreira, with a relaxed wine hotel and riverside dining.

Tip: Stay overnight to taste at the cellar after the day-trippers leave; the riverside terrace at dusk is one of the best in the valley.

EM590, 5085-241 Gouvaes do Douro, Pinhao, Portugal →
