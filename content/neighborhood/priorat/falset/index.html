Wine Festivals in Falset

Fira del Vi de Falset ★ 4.7

Falset

The Fira del Vi de Falset is the main annual wine event for the DOQ Priorat and DO Montsant designations, held each year around the first weekend of May in Falset. Around 60 producers pour their current releases across the fairground in Falset, with a separate professional tasting day at Castell de Falset. The fair has run annually for over 30 editions and draws around 30,000 visitors each year.

Tip: Advance tickets sell out. Buy on firadelvi.org before arriving. The professional Monday session at the castle requires trade credentials.

Festa del Vi Novell ★ 4.1

Falset

Each November, Falset and surrounding villages celebrate the release of vi novell, the new wine from the just-completed harvest. The Catalan equivalent of a new-wine celebration, vi novell is poured at cooperative cellars and the wine bars of the Carrer de la Bassa in Falset for a limited window. Cooperatives including Falset-Marca and Vinicola del Priorat open their doors to receive the new harvest.

Tip: Vi novell is available for only a few weeks after the harvest. The cooperatives in Falset are the best source.

Tast Maridatge Priorat ★ 4.4

Falset

The Tast Maridatge Priorat is a food and wine pairing event held each autumn in Falset that pairs current-release DOQ Priorat wines with Catalan cuisine prepared by local restaurateurs. The format matches specific wines to dishes from the region, with producers on hand to discuss their wines. The event emphasises the traditional Catalan food culture of the comarca alongside the wines.

Tip: Ticket numbers are limited. Book through Priorat Enoturisme in advance. Dishes include romesco-based preparations and calcots when in season.

Inter-Priorat Annual Trade Tasting ★ 4.3

Falset

The Consell Regulador de la DOQ Priorat organises an annual trade tasting each spring, presenting the full range of DOQ Priorat producers to importers, sommeliers, and journalists. The tasting showcases wines across the Vi de Vila village classification tiers. Access is restricted to trade and press with accreditation. It is the central showcase for the DOQ to present its wines to the international wine trade.

Tip: Trade attendance requires advance accreditation through the Consell Regulador. Contact doqpriorat.org well ahead of the event date.

Dietary in Falset

Mas Martinet Viticultors ★ 4.8

Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDFalset

Mas Martinet, run by Sara Perez, has been certified organic (CCPAE) since 2006 and practises agroecological farming integrating biodynamic, permaculture, and organic principles. Sara Perez took over winemaking from her father Josep Lluis Perez and was among the earliest organic adopters in the DOQ. The estate produces Cami Pessebre, Clos Martinet, and Martinet Bru from Falset.

Tip: Mas Martinet was among the first estates in Priorat to certify organic. Sara Perez's agroecological approach integrates biodiversity corridors and soil health monitoring beyond standard certification.

Carretera de Gratallops s/n, 43730 Falset, Tarragona, Spain →

Mas Martinet Viticultors ★ 4.5

Falset

Mas Martinet Bru is listed by The Natural Wine Company (UK) as a natural-leaning wine. Sara Perez uses indigenous yeast, minimal added sulfur, and no fining agents across the estate range. The agroecological approach and the Bru entry wine appear on natural wine platforms despite the estate not formally using the natural wine designation.

Tip: Mas Martinet Bru is the most widely stocked Mas Martinet wine on natural wine retailer platforms. The estate uses indigenous yeast and low sulfur but does not formally use the 'natural wine' designation.

Carretera de Gratallops s/n, 43730 Falset, Tarragona, Spain →

Mas Martinet Viticultors ★ 4.5

Falset
