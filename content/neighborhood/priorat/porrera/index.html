Wine in Porrera, Priorat

20 editor picks across 4 categories

Vineyards in Porrera

Ferrer Bobet ★ 4.7

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Cabernet Sauvignon, SyrahFounded 2005Sergi Ferrer-Salat and Raul BobetWinemaker Raul BobetPorrera

Founded in 2005 by pharmaceutical entrepreneur Sergi Ferrer-Salat and former Torres technical director Raul Bobet, Ferrer Bobet focuses on old-vine Garnacha and Cariñena from the mountain terrain between Falset and Porrera.

Tasting: Barrels and Aromas (1.5 hrs, 35 euros) or From Vine to Glass (2.5 hrs, 50 euros); book via website.

Signature wines: ferrer-bobet-vinyes-velles, ferrer-bobet-selecci-especial

Carretera de Falset a Porrera (T-740) Km 6.5, 43730 Falset, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Celler Vall Llach ★ 4.8

DOQ PrioratCariñena, Garnacha, Merlot, SyrahFounded 1997Enric Costa and Lluis LlachPorrera

Founded in the early 1990s by singer Lluis Llach and his partner Enric Costa in Porrera. Mas de la Rosa holds Gran Vinya Classificada status, the highest tier in DOQ Priorat. The estate is known for Cariñena-dominant blends.

Tasting: Guided visits by appointment at the Porrera cellar.

Signature wines: mas-de-la-rosa, idus-de-vall-llach, embruix-de-vall-llach

Carrer Pont, 9, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Marco Abella ★ 4.5

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Garnacha BlancaFounded 2005David Marco and Olivia BayesPorrera

The Marco family has cultivated vineyards in Porrera since the 15th century; David Marco and Olivia Bayes built the modern winery in 2005. Produces seven red and two white cuvees in DOQ Priorat.

Tasting: Enoturisme experiences available; contact enoturisme@marcoabella.com.

Signature wines: mas-mallola, loidana, olbia

Carretera Porrera a Cornudella del Montsant, Km. 0.7, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Sangenis i Vaque ★ 4.4

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Cabernet Sauvignon, SyrahFounded 1978Sangenis and Vaque familyPorrera

A family winery founded in 1978 by Pere Sangenis and Coxita Vaque, based on the village square of Porrera. Produces 25,000 to 30,000 bottles annually from 15 hectares of DOQ Priorat vineyards.

Tasting: Visits by arrangement at the village cellar in Porrera.

Signature wines: sangenis-i-vaque-coster-de-les-vinyes, sangenis-i-vaque-el-serral, sangenis-i-vaque-porrera-vi-de-vila

Placa Catalunya, 3, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Cims de Porrera ★ 4.5

DOQ PrioratCariñena, GarnachaFounded 1996Porrera

Established in 1996 to recover old-vine Cariñena and Garnacha from Porrera's steep llicorella slopes. Led since 2004 by Adria and Marc Perez together with around 20 local growers from the Porrera cooperative.

Tasting: Tastings and visits by arrangement with the cooperative.

Signature wines: cims-de-porrera-classic, cims-de-porrera-vi-de-vila

Ctra. de Torroja s/n, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Celler Cal Pla ★ 4.5

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Syrah, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1814Joan SangenisWinemaker Joan SangenisPorrera

The seventh generation of the Sangenis family at Porrera, with estate history dating to 1814. The modern Celler Cal Pla facility, built in 2017, vinifies Garnacha and Cariñena from the family's Mas d'en Compte vineyard.

Tasting: Visits by appointment at the Porrera cellar.

Signature wines: celler-cal-pla-planots, celler-cal-pla-nou-naix

Prat de la Riba, 1, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Celler Priorat Natur ★ 4.2

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Garnacha BlancaORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalPorrera

A natural wine producer in Porrera working with certified-organic old-vine Garnacha and Cariñena within the DOQ Priorat.

Tasting: Contact for cellar visits in Porrera.

Signature wines: priorat-natur-vi-de-paratge

Carrer de la Placa, 4, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Mas de la Rosa (Vall Llach Gran Vinya Classificada) ★ 4.9

DOQ Priorat Gran Vinya ClassificadaCariñena, GarnachaEnric Costa and Lluis LlachPorrera

The Mas de la Rosa single vineyard in Porrera, vinified by Celler Vall Llach, holds Gran Vinya Classificada status. One of only three Gran Vinya Classificada vineyards in DOQ Priorat.

Tasting: Viewable through Celler Vall Llach visits.

Signature wines: mas-de-la-rosa

Carrer Pont, 9, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Tasting: Contact for cellar visits.

Signature wines: del-pont-vi-de-paratge

Carrer del Pont, 3, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Ester Nin and Carles Ortiz produce low-intervention wines from organically farmed old Cariñena and Garnacha vines in Porrera, using indigenous yeasts and minimal additions.

Hidden Gems in Porrera

Nin-Ortiz ★ 4.7

DOQ PrioratCarinena, GarnachaFounded 2003Ester Nin and Carles OrtizWinemaker Ester NinORGANIC CERTIFIEDPorrera

Micro-production estate in Porrera from Ester Nin, former viticulturist at Clos Erasmus, producing old-vine Carinena wines of singular precision from organically farmed century-old vines.

Tip: Nin-Ortiz produces tiny quantities and direct cellar visits are rare. Seek the wines through specialist natural wine importers in Spain or the UK.

Carrer Major 2, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →

Ferrer Bobet ★ 4.5

DOQ PrioratCarinena, GarnachaFounded 2001ORGANIC CERTIFIEDPorrera

Small organic estate in Porrera producing concentrated old-vine Carinena and Garnacha wines admired by local sommeliers but rarely on mainstream tourist itineraries.

Tip: Ferrer Bobet wines are not easy to find outside Spain. The Seleccio Especial is the prestige bottling; the regular Porrera Carinena is the locals' recommendation.

Carrer Hospital 9, 43737 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →

Clos Figueras ★ 4.4

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Carinena, Syrah, Cabernet SauvignonFounded 1997ORGANIC CERTIFIEDPorrera

Small organic estate in Porrera producing Garnacha and Carinena wines from steep licorella terraces. Little-known beyond specialist circles despite consistent quality from organically farmed vines.

Tip: Clos Figueras wines are stocked by a handful of specialist importers in the US and UK. The Porrera Vi de Vila bottling is the one to seek.

Partida Figueras s/n, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →

Nightlife in Porrera

Bar el Portal de Porrera ★ 4.2

10 by the glassPorrera

The village bar in Porrera, the historic Carinena village of the DOQ, serves wines from local producers including Vall Llach and Cims de Porrera by the glass on weekend evenings.

The list: Porrera Vi de Vila wines from local producers, served by the glass with Catalan snacks

Tip: Porrera's village bar is the place to drink Carinena-dominant wines from the local vintage at cellar-door prices.

Carrer Major s/n, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →

Vall Llach Terrace Evening ★ 4.5

10 by the glassPorrera

The terrace of Vall Llach's tasting room in Porrera village is open for evening wine service during the summer season, offering current estate releases against the backdrop of the village's licorella slopes at dusk.

The list: Vall Llach estate wines by the glass on the tasting room terrace at dusk, overlooking the Porrera terraces

Tip: Vall Llach's tasting room terrace in Porrera at dusk, with glasses of Embruix or Idus against the silhouette of the Carinena terraces, is one of the most evocative evening experiences in the DOQ.

Carrer Major 23, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →

Dietary in Porrera

Nin-Ortiz ★ 4.6

NaturalPorrera

Ester Nin and Carles Ortiz produce Nin-Ortiz wines from organically farmed century-old Carinena in Porrera with minimal cellar intervention. Indigenous yeast fermentation, no fining or filtration, and very low sulfur additions characterise the winemaking approach.

Tip: Nin-Ortiz wines are available through specialist natural wine merchants. Production is very small and wines sell out quickly. Contact the winery or a specialist importer directly.

Carrer Major 2, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →

Cims de Porrera ★ 4.4

Porrera

Cims de Porrera, founded by Josep Lluis Perez to preserve century-old Carinena and Garnacha vines in Porrera, is listed as a natural wine producer by MYSA Natural Wine with vegan winemaking practices. The estate uses organic farming and minimal-intervention winemaking without animal-derived fining agents.

Tip: Cims de Porrera Vi de Villa Porrera is listed by specialist vegan wine retailers as suitable for vegans. The producer's organic and minimal-intervention approach supports this.

Carrer Major s/n, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →

Nin-Ortiz ★ 4.5

Porrera

Nin-Ortiz's minimal-intervention approach includes very low or no sulfur additions at bottling. Ester Nin's old-vine Carinena wines from Porrera are fermented with indigenous yeast and bottled with minimal sulfite protection, relying on organic-farmed fruit health for stability.

Tip: Nin-Ortiz wines are low in added sulfite and should be stored carefully. The wines are designed for relatively early drinking unless cellared in stable conditions.

Carrer Major 2, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →
