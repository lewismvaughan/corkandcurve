Wine Experiences in Jerez centro

Fiesta de la Vendimia de Jerez ★ 4.9

Jerez centro

The annual Jerez harvest festival held in early September centres on the Pisada de la Uva ceremony: barefoot treading of the first Palomino Fino grapes in the traditional lagares at the Gonzalez Byass bodega. Open-air events, flamenco performances and free sherry tastings run across the historic centre for several days.

Plaza de la Alameda Vieja, 11402 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain →

Pisada de la Uva Grape Treading Ceremony ★ 4.8

Jerez centro

The ancient barefoot grape-treading ritual performed at the Gonzalez Byass bodega at the start of the Vendimia. Participants in traditional Jota costumes tread Palomino Fino grapes in the lagar to produce the first must of the harvest. A highlight of the Jerez sherry calendar and an internationally attended ritual.

Calle Manuel Maria Gonzalez 12, 11403 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain →

Harvest Participation at Gonzalez Byass ★ 4.8

Jerez centro

A limited harvest participation programme at Gonzalez Byass during the September Vendimia, in which guests join the bodega team in the Macharnudo vineyard for the Palomino Fino pick. The day concludes with a must tasting and bodega dinner.

Calle Manuel Maria Gonzalez 12, 11403 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain →
