Wine Schools in Chiroubles

Ateliers Oenologiques au Domaine Christophe Savoye ★ 4.3

EUR 50 per personChiroubles

Producer-led oenology workshops in the Chiroubles cru. Christophe Savoye guides groups of 2 to 8 through an initiation to tasting, grape varieties, terroir, and food pairings in 90 minutes, concluding with a traditional Beaujolais snack and optional old-vintage tastings.

279 Rue des Ecoles, 69115 Chiroubles, France →
