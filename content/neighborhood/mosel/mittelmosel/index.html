Wine in Mittelmosel, Mosel

13 editor picks across 1 categories

Vineyards in Mittelmosel

Weingut Clemens Busch ★ 4.7

VDP.Grosse Lage MoselRieslingBusch family (Clemens and Florian Busch)Winemaker Clemens BuschBiodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDMittelmosel

A biodynamic pioneer in Punderich working the single Marienburg slope as separate blue, red and grey-slate parcels. Organic since 1984 and certified biodynamic since 2015; characterful, often skin-influenced dry Riesling.

Tasting: Tastings and sales by appointment at the Punderich estate.

Kirchstrasse 37, 56862 Punderich, Germany → Book →

Weingut Schloss Lieser ★ 4.7

VDP.Grosse Lage MoselRieslingHaag family (Thomas Haag)Winemaker Thomas HaagMittelmosel

Thomas Haag's estate at the castle village of Lieser, with parcels in the Brauneberger Juffer, Wehlener Sonnenuhr and Niederberg Helden. Multiple-time German winery of the year for its precise Rieslings.

Tasting: Vinothek tastings and sales at the estate in Lieser; visits by arrangement.

Am Markt 1-5, 54470 Lieser, Germany → Book →

Weingut Immich-Batterieberg ★ 4.6

Mosel PradikatsweinRieslingGernot Kollmann and partnersWinemaker Gernot KollmannMittelmosel

One of the oldest estates on the Mosel, revived by Gernot Kollmann around the old-vine Enkircher sites of Batterieberg, Steffensberg and Ellergrub. Dry, spontaneously fermented, low-intervention Riesling.

Tasting: Tastings and sales by appointment at the estate in Enkirch.

Im Alten Tal 2, 56850 Enkirch, Germany → Book →

Weingut Vollenweider ★ 4.5

Mosel PradikatsweinRieslingFounded 2000Moritz Hoffmann (estate founded by Daniel Vollenweider)Winemaker Moritz HoffmannMittelmosel

A boutique estate founded by Swiss-born Daniel Vollenweider and centred on the steep old-vine Wolfer Goldgrube, now led by Moritz Hoffmann. Concentrated, classically fruity Mosel Riesling from the Traben-Trarbach area.

Tasting: Small estate; tastings and sales by appointment in Traben-Trarbach.

Wolfer Weg 53, 56841 Traben-Trarbach, Germany → Book →

Weingut Weiser-Kunstler ★ 4.6

Mosel PradikatsweinRieslingFounded 2005Konstantin Weiser and Alexandra KunstlerWinemaker Konstantin WeiserMittelmosel

A sought-after small grower working steep, organically farmed parcels in the Enkircher Ellergrub and Trabener Gaispfad. Detailed, low-intervention Riesling in dry and fruity styles.

Tasting: Small estate; tastings and sales by appointment in Traben-Trarbach.

Wilhelmstrasse 11, 56841 Traben-Trarbach, Germany → Book →

Weingut Max Ferd. Richter ★ 4.4

Mosel PradikatsweinRiesling, Muller-ThurgauFounded 1680Richter family (Constantin Richter)Winemaker Constantin RichterMittelmosel

A ninth-generation estate at Mulheim with parcels across the Mittelmosel including the Brauneberger Juffer and a Mulheimer Helenenkloster site noted for Eiswein. Wide range of fruity and dry Riesling.

Tasting: Tastings and sales at the Mulheim estate; visits by arrangement.

Hauptstrasse 85, 54486 Mulheim, Germany → Book →

Weingut Nik Weis - St. Urbans-Hof ★ 4.6

VDP.Grosse Lage MoselRieslingFounded 1947Weis family (Nik Weis)Winemaker Nik WeisMittelmosel

A leading Leiwen estate with sites on both the Mosel and Saar, including the Leiwener Laurentiuslay, Ockfener Bockstein and Wiltinger Schlangengraben. Pure, classically styled Riesling for a global market.

Tasting: Tastings and sales at the Leiwen estate; visits by appointment.

Urbanusstrasse 16, 54340 Leiwen, Germany → Book →

Weingut Grans-Fassian ★ 4.5

VDP.Grosse Lage MoselRiesling, Weissburgunder (Pinot Blanc)Grans family (Gerhard Grans)Winemaker Gerhard GransMittelmosel

A Leiwen estate with parcels in the Leiwener Laurentiuslay, Trittenheimer Apotheke and Piesporter Goldtropfchen. Precise dry and fruity Riesling plus a little Weissburgunder.

Tasting: Tastings and sales at the Leiwen estate; visits by arrangement.

Romerstrasse 28, 54340 Leiwen, Germany → Book →

Weingut Carl Loewen ★ 4.6

VDP.Grosse Lage MoselRieslingLoewen family (Karl Josef and Christopher Loewen)Winemaker Christopher LoewenMittelmosel

A Leiwen estate celebrated for the ungrafted old-vine Maximin Herrenberg and the Laurentiuslay. Christopher Loewen makes detailed dry and fruity Rieslings, including the 1896 Riesling from pre-phylloxera vines.

Tasting: Tastings and sales at the Leiwen estate; visits by arrangement.

Matthiasstrasse 30, 54340 Leiwen, Germany → Book →

Weingut A.J. Adam ★ 4.6

VDP.Grosse Lage MoselRieslingAdam family (Andreas Adam)Winemaker Andreas AdamMittelmosel

A small, sought-after grower who revived the steep Dhroner Hofberg. Andreas Adam makes concentrated dry and fruity Rieslings that have become collector favourites.

Tasting: Small estate; tastings and sales by appointment in Neumagen-Dhron.

Metschert 14, 54347 Neumagen-Dhron, Germany → Book →

Weingut Loersch ★ 4.4

Mosel PradikatsweinRieslingLoersch family (Alexander Loersch)Winemaker Alexander LoerschMittelmosel

A young, ambitious Leiwen grower working old-vine parcels in the Trittenheimer Apotheke and Leiwener Laurentiuslay. Precise, mostly dry Rieslings from steep slate.

Tasting: Small estate; tastings and sales by appointment in Leiwen.

Tannenweg 11, 54340 Leiwen, Germany → Book →

Weingut Stein ★ 4.4

Mosel PradikatsweinRieslingStein family (Ulrich and Peter Stein)Winemaker Ulrich SteinNaturalMittelmosel

Ulli Stein's individualist estate around St. Aldegund and Alf, known for low-intervention, sometimes provocative Rieslings from steep, near-abandoned slate sites and a hilltop tasting house.

Tasting: Tastings at Haus Waldfrieden above Alf and at the Bullay estate; by arrangement.

Brautrockstrasse 40, 56859 Bullay, Germany → Book →

Weingut Daniel Twardowski ★ 4.5

Mosel PradikatsweinSpatburgunder (Pinot Noir)Daniel TwardowskiWinemaker Daniel TwardowskiMittelmosel

A cult micro-estate proving the slate Mosel can make serious Pinot Noir. Daniel Twardowski's Pinot Noix is grown on Devonian slate and made in a Burgundian, low-yield style.

Tasting: Very small estate; visits and tastings strictly by appointment.

Folzerweg 20, 54347 Neumagen-Dhron, Germany → Book →
