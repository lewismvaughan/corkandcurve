COS Pithos (amphora wines) ★ 4.8

COS Pithos wines are fermented and aged in terracotta amphorae, reviving pre-industrial Sicilian vinification. The Pithos Bianco from Grecanico fermented on skins and the Pithos Rosso from Nero d'Avola are among Italy's most influential natural wines.

Vineyard in Sicily · Cerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG· IGP Terre Siciliane Verified May 26, 2026

Benanti Pietrarizzo Contrada ★ 4.6

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello CappuccioFounded 1734Etna DOC

The Pietrarizzo contrada on the north slope of Etna at 800 metres produces Benanti's most mineral and savoury Nerello Mascalese. The Benanti family has farmed this plot since the 18th century, making it one of the oldest continuously cultivated contrade on the volcano.

Tasting: Visits available as part of the Benanti estate experience. Book via benanti.it.

Signature wines: benanti-pietrarizzo

Duca di Salaparuta (Corvo) ★ 4.3

Sicilia DOCNero d'Avola, Catarratto, Grillo, Inzolia, PerriconeFounded 1824Sicilia DOC

Founded in 1824 by the Duke of Salaparuta, Duca di Salaparuta (Corvo) is one of Sicily's oldest commercial wine estates. Their Duca Enrico is a landmark 100% Nero d'Avola and the Bianca di Valguarnera a prestigious Inzolia white. Now owned by Illva Saronno.

Tasting: Visits and tastings at the historic Casteldaccia winery. Contact via the Duca di Salaparuta website.

Signature wines: duca-di-salaparuta-bianca-di-valguarnera, duca-enrico

Via Nazionale SS 113, 90040 Casteldaccia (PA), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Sicilia DOCNero d'Avola, Syrah, Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Catarratto, Chardonnay, GrilloFounded 1968Sicilia DOC

Tenuta Rapitala at Camporeale in the Palermo province was established by a Franco-Sicilian partnership in 1968. The estate produces a broad range of Sicilian varietal and blended wines across 170 hectares, with the Grand Cru their prestigious top bottling.

Tasting: Visits and tastings at the Camporeale estate. Contact via the Rapitala website.
