Carricante in Sicily

28 editor-picked estates growing Carricante in Sicily.

Where to taste Carricante in Sicily: the estates that grow it, how the region expresses the grape, and how to book a visit.

Carricante estates in Sicily

Planeta ★ 4.7

Menfi DOC, Etna DOC, Noto DOC, Sicilia DOCNero d'Avola, Syrah, Merlot, Cabernet Franc, Grecanico, Chardonnay, Grillo, Carricante, Nocera, Nerello MascaleseFounded 1994Menfi DOC

One of Sicily's most influential estates, Planeta operates six wineries across the island from Menfi to Etna, Noto and Milazzo, showcasing the diversity of Sicilian terroir through native and international varieties.

Tasting: Three estate cellar doors: Ulmo at Menfi, Buonivini at Noto, and Sciaranuova on Etna. Tasting experiences bookable on the Planeta estate website.

Signature wines: planeta-santa-cecilia, planeta-burdese, planeta-cometa

Contrada Dispensa, 92013 Menfi (AG), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Benanti Viticoltori ★ 4.8

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, CatarrattoFounded 1734Etna DOC

One of Etna's pioneering estates, Benanti has been producing wine on the volcanic slopes since 1734. Their single-contrada whites from Milo are benchmarks for Etna Bianco Superiore, and their Nerello reds helped launch the modern Etna renaissance.

Tasting: Tuesday through Sunday by reservation. Three tasting programmes including Get to Know Etna, Contrade Selection and Library Vintages. Tours of the historic palmento and vineyard.

Signature wines: benanti-pietramarina, benanti-rovittello, benanti-serra-della-contessa

Via Giuseppe Garibaldi 361, 95029 Viagrande (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Tenuta delle Terre Nere ★ 4.8

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, CatarrattoFounded 2002Etna DOC

Founded in 2002 by Marc de Grazia on Etna's north slope at Randazzo, Tenuta delle Terre Nere was instrumental in establishing the single-contrada bottlings that made Etna internationally famous. Old-vine Nerello from plots like Calderara Sottana and Guardiola set the benchmark.

Tasting: By appointment, Monday to Friday. Contact via visite@tenutaterrenere.com.

Signature wines: terre-nere-calderara-sottana, terre-nere-guardiola, terre-nere-feudo-di-mezzo

Contrada Calderara sn, 95036 Randazzo (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Graci ★ 4.7

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, CatarrattoFounded 2004Etna DOC

Alberto Aiello Graci has been farming old-vine Nerello Mascalese and Carricante on Etna's north slope since 2004, producing single-contrada wines including the benchmark Arcuria Etna Rosso from 52 acres of ungrafted vines.

Tasting: Visits by appointment at the Passopisciaro estate. Contact via info@graci.eu.

Signature wines: graci-arcuria, graci-barbabecchi, graci-etna-bianco

Contrada Feudo di Mezzo, SP7iii, Passopisciaro, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Pietradolce ★ 4.7

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, CatarrattoFounded 2005Etna DOC

Founded in 2005 by Michele Faro at Solicchiata on Etna's north slope, Pietradolce works with old-vine Nerello Mascalese and Carricante across several contrade, producing one of the island's most age-worthy single-vineyard Biancos in the Archineri.

Tasting: Visits and tastings at the Solicchiata winery on Etna's north slope. Contact via the Pietradolce website.

Signature wines: pietradolce-archineri, pietradolce-etna-rosso, pietradolce-barbagalli

SS120 174, Contrada Rampante, Solicchiata, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Girolamo Russo ★ 4.8

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Carricante, Catarratto, Inzolia, GrecanicoFounded 2005ORGANIC CERTIFIEDEtna DOC

Giuseppe Russo took over the family's old Nerello Mascalese vineyards in 2005 after his father Girolamo died. Fifteen hectares of organically farmed vines between 650 and 780 metres on Etna's north slope produce sought-after single-vineyard reds including San Lorenzo and a Rina.

Tasting: Vineyard visits and tastings with local cold cuts, cheeses and homemade bread. Contact info@girolamorusso.it or call 0942 983142.

Signature wines: russo-san-lorenzo, russo-a-rina, russo-feudo

Via Regina Margherita 78, 95012 Passopisciaro (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Cottanera ★ 4.5

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, Merlot, Syrah, MondeuseFounded 1994Etna DOC

Cottanera is a family estate on Etna's north slope producing both native Nerello-based Etna DOC wines and international varieties grown at high altitude. The boutique agriturismo Dimora Cottanera makes it a wine-tourism anchor on the Alcantara Valley rim.

Tasting: Monday to Saturday 9:00 to 17:00, booking essential. Guided vineyard, cellar and tasting experiences.

Signature wines: cottanera-barbazzale-bianco, cottanera-fatagione, cottanera-calderara

Strada Provinciale 89, Contrada Iannazzo, 95030 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Murgo ★ 4.5

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, CatarrattoFounded 1860Etna DOC

A historic estate on Etna's eastern slopes, Murgo introduced Sicily's most acclaimed traditional-method sparkling wine in 1990 using Nerello Mascalese. The 30-hectare property has been in the Scammacca del Murgo family since 1860.

Tasting: Wine tastings available Monday to Saturday 11:30 to 14:00. Restaurant and accommodation on site.

Signature wines: murgo-brut-metodo-classico, murgo-etna-rosso, murgo-etna-bianco

Via Zafferana 13, 95010 Santa Venerina (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Frank Cornelissen ★ 4.9

Etna DOC, IGP Terre SicilianeNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Catarratto, Minella, CarricanteFounded 2001Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalEtna DOC

Belgian natural-wine pioneer Frank Cornelissen established his estate on Etna's north slope in 2001, working without sulphur at all. His Magma cuvee from ungrafted pre-phylloxera Nerello vines above 1,000 metres is among Sicily's most sought-after cult wines.

Tasting: Five tasting experiences from the Classic (5 wines, EU40) to the Magma private (6 crus including Grand Vin Magma, EU1,400 for up to 6 people). E-bike vineyard tours available. Book via pr@frankcornelissen.it.

Signature wines: cornelissen-magma, cornelissen-munjebel-rosso, cornelissen-munjebel-bianco

Via Canonico Zumbo 1, Passopisciaro, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Gulfi ★ 4.7

Eloro DOC, Sicilia DOCNero d'Avola, Nerello Cappuccio, Albanello, CarricanteORGANIC CERTIFIEDEloro DOC

Gulfi owns some of the oldest Nero d'Avola parcels in the Pachino area, including pre-phylloxera crus at Maccaraj, Bufaleffj, Baronj and Sanlorenzo. The estate's four single-vineyard bottlings set the benchmark for terroir-expressive Nero d'Avola.

Tasting: Visits and tastings at the Noto estate. Contact info@gulfi.it.

Signature wines: gulfi-nerojbleo, gulfi-nerobuffaleffj, gulfi-nerobaronj

Contrada Maccari, Strada Prov. Noto-Pachino Km 13.5, 96017 Noto (SR), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Tenuta di Fessina ★ 4.6

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, CarricanteORGANIC CERTIFIEDEtna DOC

Silvia Maestrelli's Tenuta di Fessina in Rovittello at 670 metres on Etna's north slope offers one of the island's most accessible high-quality tasting experiences. The organic estate produces refined Nerello Mascalese and Carricante across its Etna DOC range.

Tasting: Open every day including holidays. Tasting reception at 10:30, 13:30 and 15:30. Contact fessina@tenutadifessina.com or call +39 345 7230176.

Signature wines: fessina-erse, fessina-musmeci, fessina-a-puddara

Contrada Rovittello, Via Nazionale SS120 22, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Palmento Costanzo ★ 4.6

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, CatarrattoFounded 2011ORGANIC CERTIFIEDEtna DOC

Founded in 2011 by Mimmo and Valeria Costanzo, Palmento Costanzo farms approximately ten hectares organically on Etna's north slope in Contrada Santo Spirito. They produce contrada-specific wines with manual cultivation and a fully organic approach.

Tasting: Visite e Degustazioni bookable via the estate website. Contact info@palmentocostanzo.com or call +39 0942 983239.

Signature wines: palmento-costanzo-mofete-bianco, palmento-costanzo-mofete-rosso, palmento-costanzo-di-me

Contrada Santo Spirito, Passopisciaro, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Tornatore ★ 4.4

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, CatarrattoFounded 1865Etna DOC

The Tornatore family has farmed on Etna since 1865, with a large single-vineyard block in the Castiglione di Sicilia hills. Their estate is often cited as Etna DOC's largest single property, producing a full range of DOC wines from Nerello and Carricante.

Tasting: Guided tours and tastings at the estate, described as the largest single vineyard in Etna DOC. Contact +39 337 1087964.

Signature wines: tornatore-etna-bianco, tornatore-etna-rosso, tornatore-trimarchisa

Via Pietramarina 8, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Tenute Nicosia ★ 4.4

Etna DOC, Cerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG, Sicilia DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, Nero d'Avola, Frappato, CatarrattoORGANIC CERTIFIEDEtna DOC

Tenute Nicosia operates vineyards on Etna and in Vittoria, producing organic wines across a broad range including Etna DOC and Cerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG. Their Trecastagni winery on the south-east slope of Etna is a key wine-tourism destination.

Tasting: Guided tours and tastings by reservation at the Trecastagni winery. Multiple tasting packages including Contrade dell'Etna. Contact info@cantinenicosia.it.

Signature wines: nicosia-etna-rosso-fondo-filara, nicosia-etna-bianco, nicosia-monte-gorna

Via Luigi Capuana 65, 95039 Trecastagni (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Tenuta Tascante (Tasca d'Almerita on Etna) ★ 4.6

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, CarricanteFounded 2010Etna DOC

Tasca d'Almerita's Etna estate in Castiglione di Sicilia, established around 2010, produces contrada-expressive Nerello Mascalese reds and Carricante whites within the Etna DOC. Ghiaia Nera is the benchmark cuvee of the estate.

Tasting: Visits bookable via the Tasca d'Almerita website. Estate is located in Contrada Rampante on Etna's north slope.

Signature wines: tascante-ghiaia-nera, tascante-il-picciolo

Contrada Rampante, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Planeta Sciaranuova (Etna) ★ 4.5

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, CarricanteFounded 2008Etna DOC

Planeta's Etna estate in Solicchiata, established in 2008, produces Nerello Mascalese and Carricante within the Etna DOC. The Sciaranuova winery is one of the island's most architecturally striking cellar doors.

Tasting: Tasting experience at the Sciaranuova winery. Bookable via the Planeta estate website.

Signature wines: planeta-nerello-etna, planeta-carricante-etna

Contrada Sciaranuova, Solicchiata, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Alta Mora by Cusumano (Etna) ★ 4.4

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, CarricanteFounded 2013Etna DOC

Cusumano's Etna label Alta Mora is produced from the Verzella contrada on the north slope of Etna. The range focuses on Nerello Mascalese for reds and Carricante for whites, with contrada bottlings among the island's most accessible Etna expressions.

Tasting: Etna estate tastings bookable via the Cusumano website.

Signature wines: alta-mora-etna-rosso, alta-mora-etna-bianco

Contrada Verzella, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Firriato Cavanera Etnea ★ 4.4

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, CarricanteEtna DOC

Firriato's Etna estate in the Verzella contrada includes the Cavanera resort, one of the most complete wine-tourism experiences on Etna, with accommodation, restaurant, cellar visits and the Gaudensius traditional-method sparkling wine from Nerello Mascalese.

Tasting: Wine resort and restaurant on Etna's north slope. Visits and stays bookable via Firriato.

Signature wines: firriato-gaudensius-etna-brut, firriato-cavanera-ripa-di-moraci

Contrada Verzella, Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Vini Gambino ★ 4.4

Nerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, CarricanteAppointment onlyLinguaglossa, Etna Nord

Family winery on Etna's north slope at 700 m altitude offering sommelier-led tastings paired with Sicilian delicacies; award-winning Tifeo range.

Tip: Combine with a Gambino lunch for a full 3-hour experience.

Contrada Petto Dragone, 95015 Linguaglossa (CT), Sicily →

Benanti Viticoltori ★ 4.7

Nerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, CarricanteAppointment onlyViagrande, Etna Sud-Est

1734-heritage estate with historic palmento and vineyard walk on Monte Serra; the only winery producing wines from all four Etna slopes using 100% native grapes with autochthonous yeasts.

Tip: Open Tue-Sun by reservation; 15 km from Catania, 40 km from Taormina.

Via Giuseppe Garibaldi 361, 95029 Viagrande (CT), Sicily →

Cottanera ★ 4.6

Nerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Carricante, MondeuseAppointment onlyCastiglione di Sicilia, Etna Nord

100-hectare Etna estate at 700 m offering contrade-focused tastings with cellared vintages; lunch packages include traditional Etna recipes paired across the range.

Tip: Book the Le Contrade lunch to taste separately vinified single-vineyard Nerello Mascalese.

Strada Provinciale 89, Contrada Iannazzo, 95030 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily →

Barone di Villagrande ★ 4.7

Carricante, Nerello MascaleseAppointment onlyMilo, Etna Est

Sicily's oldest Etna DOC producer since 1727; historic cellar tours with Etna cuisine pairings and access to a four-room wine resort with infinity pool.

Tip: The Contrada Villagrande single-vineyard Carricante is poured exclusively on-site.

Via del Bosco 25, 95010 Milo (CT), Sicily →

Tenute Nicosia ★ 4.4

Nerello Mascalese, Carricante, Nero d'AvolaAppointment onlyTrecastagni, Etna Sud

Organic Etna winery offering contrade immersions, underground cellar tours and a sparkling-wine focus; ancient Monte Gorna palmento adds heritage depth.

Tip: The sparkling Etna Rosato Brut Methode Classique tasting is a Nicosia signature.

Via L. Capuana 65, 95039 Trecastagni (CT), Sicily →

Planeta Sciaranuova ★ 4.6

Nerello Mascalese, CarricanteAppointment onlyRandazzo, Etna Nord

Planeta's Etna estate inside the Etna Park, with a carefully restored cellar and the Teatro in Vigna natural amphitheatre; exclusive wine shop and panoramic vineyard views of the volcano.

Tip: The Sciaranuova Festival in summer hosts concerts in the vineyard amphitheatre.

Contrada Sciaranuova, 95036 Randazzo (CT), Sicily →

Frank Cornelissen ★ 4.9

Etna DOC / Terre Siciliane IGTNerello Mascalese, Carricante, MinellaFounded 2001Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDSolicchiata, Etna DOC

Belgian-born Frank Cornelissen makes radical no-added-sulfur Nerello Mascalese from pre-phylloxera alberello vines in the Solicchiata and Calderara contrade on the north face of Etna.

Tip: Cornelissen does not operate a conventional tasting room; allocation visits are by appointment and through importer relationships. Availability of top cuvees is tightly limited.

Contrada Barbabecchi, 95030 Solicchiata, Etna, Italy →

Calabretta ★ 4.7

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, CarricanteFounded 1900ORGANIC CERTIFIEDCalderara, Etna DOC

Century-old family estate in the Calderara contrada, making organic, minimal-intervention Etna Rosso from pre-phylloxera alberello vines. One of Etna's quietest and most authentic producers.

Tip: Calabretta rarely advertises tastings; visits are arranged by contacting the family directly. The wines appear on natural wine lists in Catania and Palermo before reaching international import markets.

Contrada Calderara, 95030 Randazzo, Etna, Italy →

Gulfi ★ 4.8

Sicilia DOC / Etna DOCNero d'Avola, Frappato, Carricante, Nerello MascaleseFounded 1996ORGANIC CERTIFIEDChiaramonte Gulfi, Ragusa province

Organic Nero d'Avola specialist in Chiaramonte Gulfi, producing single-vineyard cru bottlings from altitude plantings that show the variety's full age-worthy complexity.

Tip: The single-vineyard Nero d'Avola hierarchy is a lesson in what altitude and vineyard aspect can do to the variety. The Nerojbleo is the most accessible entry to the range.

Contrada Patria, 97010 Chiaramonte Gulfi, Ragusa, Italy →

Benanti Etna Bianco DOC ★ 4.5

Etna Bianco DOCCarricante, CatarrattoEUR 18-22 retail

Benanti's estate-level Etna Bianco is the benchmark for the Carricante variety at entry price. Volcanic minerality, green citrus, fennel and a saline finish. Made from pre-phylloxera alberello vines on the eastern Etna slopes, this is the most accessible and affordable Benanti white.

Tip: At EUR 18-22, the Benanti Etna Bianco is the most price-efficient way to taste the volcanic character of Carricante on its own appellation, without paying premium-contrada pricing.
