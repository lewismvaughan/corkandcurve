Tenuta Santa Maria Valverde ★ 4.6

Boutique winery in Marano di Valpolicella with farm-stay accommodation in the rural house. The estate operates the Bike Station Valpolicella, offering e-bike tours (2 h, 5 km) through the vineyards with Amarone tasting. 17th-century cellar for wine refinement. Rated 4.8/5 on TripAdvisor (124 reviews).

Relais La Magioca ★ 4.5

Negrar, Valpolicella Classica

Small boutique relais in Negrar di Valpolicella, one of the five communes of the Classico zone. Estate-produced Valpolicella Classico and Amarone. Cellar tastings by arrangement. Agriturismo-style dining with local Veronese produce paired with estate wines.

The list: Valpolicella Classica DOC and Amarone DOCG estate wines; boutique relais in Negrar sub-zone; cellar tastings available; agriturismo-style estate dining

Tip: Negrar is the easternmost of the five Valpolicella Classico communes - the wines here tend toward elegance rather than the richer style of Fumane and Marano.

Via Moron 3, 37024 Negrar di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

La Foresteria Serego Alighieri ★ 4.7

Gargagnago, Valpolicella Classica

The Serego Alighieri family (descendants of Dante Alighieri) has farmed this Valpolicella estate since 1353. La Foresteria guest house at Gargagnago offers estate stays with access to Masi-managed wine tastings of the Serego Alighieri range including Vaio Armaron Amarone. Linked to the new Monteleone21 open winery.

The list: Serego Alighieri estate wines by Masi (Dante Alighieri's heirs); Vaio Armaron Amarone; Possessioni range; private cellar tours; Monteleone21 open winery nearby

Tip: The historical connection to Dante Alighieri's family and the 700-year vine lineage make this the most literature-linked wine stay in Veneto.

Via Stazione 2, 37020 Gargagnago di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

Byblos Art Hotel Villa Amista ★ 4.8

Valpolicella Classica

Design hotel in a 15th-century Palladian-style aristocratic villa in the Valpolicella hills. The Wine Cellar Bar houses 1,600+ labels for tastings. The Amista Restaurant holds 1 Michelin Star across 6 consecutive years. ESPACE Byblos spa. Part of Small Luxury Hotels of the World.

The list: 15th-century Wine Cellar Bar with 1,600+ selected labels; Michelin-starred Amista Restaurant (6 consecutive stars); Saor traditional restaurant; wine tastings in historic cellar

Tip: The Wine Cellar Bar is open to non-guests for tasting - no hotel booking required.

Via Cedrare 78, 37029 Corrubbio di San Pietro in Cariano (VR), Italy →

Villa Cordevigo Wine Relais ★ 4.8

Bardolino, Cavaion Veronese

5-star Wine Relais in the Cavaion Veronese hills surrounded by vineyards and olive groves. Estate wines from the Villabella vineyard anchor the Oseleta Michelin-starred restaurant's list. Welcome aperitif, tasting menus, and wine and history packages for guests.

The list: Estate wines from Vigneti Villabella (Garda DOC, Valpolicella DOC); Oseleta 1 Michelin Star restaurant; welcome aperitif; 4-course tasting menu with estate wine pairings

Tip: The wine and history tasting package (estate wines + guided cellar narrative) is the most educational offering and can be booked without an overnight stay.

Localita Cordevigo snc, 37010 Cavaion Veronese (VR), Italy →

Massimago Wine Relais ★ 4.6

Mezzane di Sotto, Valpolicella

Boutique organic wine relais in the Mezzane Valley, 20 minutes from Verona. Six rooms and two apartments with pool and spa exclusively for guests. Estate winery produces organic Amarone and Valpolicella; vineyard walks, cellar tastings, and a seasonal restaurant are all included.

The list: Massimago organic estate wines; Amarone, Ripasso, Valpolicella; vineyard walk and cellar visit for guests; cooking school; yoga; on-site restaurant with home-grown produce

Tip: The combined cooking school + wine pairing session (available as an add-on) pairs Veneto classic dishes with estate wines - highly rated by guests.

Via Giare 21, 37030 Mezzane di Sotto (VR), Italy →

Hotel Villa Giona ★ 4.4

Valpolicella Classica

4-star hotel in a 15th-century villa in San Pietro in Cariano at the heart of Valpolicella Classica. Built by the Giona family of Veronese Jewish origin and expanded in the 16th century. Ideal base for estate visits across Valpolicella, Amarone, and Soave wine zones. 30-minute drive to Lake Garda.

The list: 4-star hotel in the heart of Valpolicella; 10 km from Verona; vineyard-country setting; positioned for estate visits across Valpolicella Classico zone

Tip: The hotel's position at the centre of the Valpolicella Classico DOC zone makes it the best strategic base for visiting multiple producers in a day.

Via Vito Pasetto 8, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano (VR), Italy →
