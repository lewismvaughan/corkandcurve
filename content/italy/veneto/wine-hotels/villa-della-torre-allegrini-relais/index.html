Villa della Torre Allegrini Wine and Art Relais ★ 4.8

Renaissance palace designed 1440s-1562, purchased by Marilisa Allegrini in 2008. Serves as Allegrini's luxury B&B and event venue. 11 rooms in the historic palace amid Fumane di Valpolicella vineyards. Private wine events featuring Allegrini Amarone and La Poja monovarietal Corvina.

Wine hotel in Veneto · Fumane, Valpolicella Classica Verified May 26, 2026

Tenuta Le Cave Wine Relais ★ 4.6

Tregnago, Valpolicella

Organic and biodynamic wine relais on a panoramic terrace between Valpolicella and Soave. Estate produces organic Valpolicella, Ripasso, and Amarone. Wine lovers packages include guided vineyard walks, cellar visits, and discovery tastings of organic wines. Pool and spa available.

The list: Organic and biodynamic estate wines (Valpolicella, Ripasso, Amarone); wine lovers packages; vineyard walking paths; organic production

Tip: Tenuta Le Cave's biodynamic Amarone is one of the rarest expressions of the appellation - the cellar visit is included in the Wine Lovers package.

Via Pagnaghe 5, 37039 Tregnago (VR), Italy →

Alice Relais nelle Vigne ★ 4.6

Carpesica, Vittorio Veneto, Prosecco hills

Vineyard relais of Le Vigne di Alice winery on a hilltop in Carpesica between Conegliano and Vittorio Veneto in the UNESCO Prosecco hills. 19th-century farmhouse with themed Alice in Wonderland rooms. Estate organic Prosecco DOCG tastings; 60 km north of Venice.

The list: Le Vigne di Alice estate Prosecco DOCG; Bellenda 1986 tastings; 19th-century farmhouse surrounded by vineyards; themed Alice in Wonderland rooms

Tip: The Doro Nature brut-zero Prosecco from Le Vigne di Alice is among the most serious expressions of the DOCG - ask for it at breakfast.

Via Giardino 94, Localita Carpesica, 31029 Vittorio Veneto (TV), Italy →

Romantik Relais d'Arfanta ★ 4.7

Tarzo, Treviso Province, Prosecco hills

Elegantly restored country-house hotel in the UNESCO Prosecco hills between Conegliano and Valdobbiadene. 7 rooms (3 suites, 4 standard) in a peaceful vine-surrounded estate with pool, garden, and spa. Own Prosecco DOCG and olive oil at breakfast; attached winery available for tastings. Tel +39 0438 933861.

The list: Own-production Prosecco DOCG; bottle and olive oil at breakfast; attached winery; along the Prosecco wine road; 15 min from Conegliano, 60 min from Venice

Tip: The attached winery offers horizontal tastings of the estate's cru Prosecco bottles - arrange a session at the front desk on arrival.

Localita Pecol d'Arfanta 20, 31020 Tarzo (TV), Italy →

Agriturismo Vedova ★ 4.5

Valdobbiadene, Prosecco hills

Country-chic agriturismo in Valdobbiadene with interiors designed by architect Tobia Scarpa. Surrounded by the UNESCO-listed Prosecco hills. Own-estate Prosecco Superiore DOCG; regional Venetian cuisine. Highly rated for value and charm. Tel available via agriturismovedova.it.

The list: Estate Prosecco Superiore DOCG wines; country-chic agriturismo surrounded by UNESCO-listed Prosecco hills; regional Venetian cuisine with estate wines

Tip: The Scarpa-designed interiors are notable for a farmhouse - the blend of agricultural heritage and contemporary design is intentional.

Via Follo 9, 31049 Valdobbiadene (TV), Italy →

Tenuta Santa Maria Valverde ★ 4.6

Marano di Valpolicella

Boutique winery in Marano di Valpolicella with farm-stay accommodation in the rural house. The estate operates the Bike Station Valpolicella, offering e-bike tours (2 h, 5 km) through the vineyards with Amarone tasting. 17th-century cellar for wine refinement. Rated 4.8/5 on TripAdvisor (124 reviews).

The list: Boutique winery; Amarone, Valpolicella, Ripasso; e-bike vineyard tours with wine tasting; 17th-century cellar; rural house stays

Tip: The e-bike + tasting combination (approx. EUR65) is the most immersive Valpolicella Classico estate experience available at this price point.

Localita Gazzo 4, 37020 Marano di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

Relais La Magioca ★ 4.5

Negrar, Valpolicella Classica

Small boutique relais in Negrar di Valpolicella, one of the five communes of the Classico zone. Estate-produced Valpolicella Classico and Amarone. Cellar tastings by arrangement. Agriturismo-style dining with local Veronese produce paired with estate wines.

The list: Valpolicella Classica DOC and Amarone DOCG estate wines; boutique relais in Negrar sub-zone; cellar tastings available; agriturismo-style estate dining

Tip: Negrar is the easternmost of the five Valpolicella Classico communes - the wines here tend toward elegance rather than the richer style of Fumane and Marano.

Via Moron 3, 37024 Negrar di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →
