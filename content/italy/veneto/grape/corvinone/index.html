Corvinone in Veneto

26 editor-picked estates growing Corvinone in Veneto.

Where to taste Corvinone in Veneto: the estates that grow it, how the region expresses the grape, and how to book a visit.

Corvinone estates in Veneto

Giuseppe Quintarelli ★ 5.0

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, Molinara, Oseleta, Cabernet Sauvignon, NebbioloFounded 1924Quintarelli family (Fiorenza Quintarelli and family)Winemaker Quintarelli family teamValpolicella Classico

The legendary Negrar estate founded in 1924, now run by Giuseppe's daughter Fiorenza. Quintarelli's Amarone and Recioto are benchmark expressions of dried-grape Valpolicella winemaking, aged for years before release.

Tasting: By appointment only; tastings at the historic cellar in Negrar. Very limited availability.

Signature wines: quintarelli-amarone-classico, quintarelli-alzero, quintarelli-recioto-classico

Via Cerè 1, 37020 Negrar di Valpolicella, Verona, Italy → Book →

Dal Forno Romano ★ 5.0

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Oseleta, MolinaraFounded 1983Romano Dal Forno and familyWinemaker Romano Dal Forno and Marco Dal FornoValpolicella

Founded by Romano Dal Forno in 1983 in the Illasi valley, outside the Classico zone. Tiny production, extremely low yields and very long maceration produce arguably the most powerful Amarone in the region.

Tasting: By appointment only; private estate visit at Cellore d'Illasi.

Signature wines: dal-forno-amarone-valpolicella, dal-forno-valpolicella-superiore

Via Lodoletta 1, 37030 Cellore d'Illasi, Verona, Italy → Book →

Allegrini ★ 4.8

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, OseletaFounded 1854Allegrini family (Marilisa Allegrini, president)Winemaker Allegrini winemaking teamValpolicella Classico

Multi-generation family estate at Fumane at the heart of Valpolicella Classico. Allegrini pioneered single-vineyard bottlings in the zone and is widely credited with modernising Amarone's international profile in the 1980s under Giovanni Allegrini.

Tasting: Tasting visits at the Fumane estate and Villa della Torre; bookable via the estate website.

Signature wines: allegrini-amarone-classico, allegrini-la-poja, allegrini-palazzo-della-torre

Via Giare 9-11, 37022 Fumane, Verona, Italy → Book →

Tommasi Viticoltori ★ 4.5

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraFounded 1902Tommasi familyWinemaker Giancarlo TommasiValpolicella Classico

Family estate in San Pietro in Cariano producing Amarone, Valpolicella and Ripasso across multiple vineyard parcels in the Classico zone since 1902. Fourth-generation winemaker Giancarlo oversees production.

Tasting: Wine tasting and cellar visits at the San Pietro in Cariano estate; bookable online.

Signature wines: tommasi-amarone-classico, tommasi-valpolicella-ripasso, tommasi-amarone-ca-florian

Via Ronchetto 2, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Speri Viticoltori ★ 4.7

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraFounded 1874Speri familyWinemaker Luca SperiValpolicella Classico

Six-generation family estate on the Monte Masua and Sant'Urbano hillsides of Fumane. Among the most traditional producers in the Classico zone; Luca Speri is the current winemaker.

Tasting: Cellar visits and seated tastings by appointment at San Pietro in Cariano.

Signature wines: speri-amarone-classico, speri-amarone-monte-sant-urbano

Via Fontana 14, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Tedeschi Vini ★ 4.7

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, RondinellaFounded 1824Tedeschi familyWinemaker Lorenzo TedeschiValpolicella Classico

Founded in 1824, Tedeschi is one of the oldest family producers in Valpolicella Classico. The single-vineyard Amarone Capitel Monte Olmi is its flagship; sixth-generation Lorenzo Tedeschi manages the estate.

Tasting: Visits and tastings by appointment; group tours of the historic cellar available.

Signature wines: tedeschi-amarone-capitel-monte-olmi, tedeschi-valpolicella-capitel-dei-nicalo

Via G. Verdi 4, 37020 San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Tenuta Sant'Antonio ★ 4.6

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, OseletaFounded 1989Castagnedi familyWinemaker Armando CastagnediValpolicella

The Castagnedi brothers established Tenuta Sant'Antonio in 1989 in the Mezzane valley east of the Classico zone. Their Campo dei Gigli Amarone from Oseleta and Corvina is a benchmark for the sub-zone style.

Tasting: Estate tasting visits by appointment at Mezzane di Sotto; full range of Valpolicella and Amarone poured.

Signature wines: tenuta-santantonio-amarone-campo-dei-gigli, tenuta-santantonio-valpolicella-superiore

Via Ceriani 23, 37030 Mezzane di Sotto, Verona, Italy → Book →

Lorenzo Begali ★ 4.7

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, RondinellaFounded 1986Begali family (Lorenzo and Giordana Begali)Winemaker Lorenzo BegaliValpolicella Classico

Small family estate at San Pietro in Cariano producing Amarone and Recioto from very old Corvina vines on the Ca'Bianca cru. Consistently high quality with a traditional approach to drying and long cellaring.

Tasting: Tastings and cellar tours by appointment; intimate family atmosphere.

Signature wines: begali-amarone-monte-ca-bianca, begali-recioto-classico

Via Cengia 10, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Zyme ★ 4.7

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCG and Veronese IGTCorvina, Corvinone, Oseleta, Rondinella, Cabernet SauvignonFounded 2001Celestino GaspariWinemaker Celestino GaspariValpolicella Classico

Founded in 2001 by former Quintarelli cellarmaster Celestino Gaspari. Zyme spans tradition and innovation: the Amarone Kairos uses classic drying methods, while the Harlequin IGT blends 15 native and international varieties.

Tasting: Private tastings by appointment at the San Pietro in Cariano cellar.

Signature wines: zyme-amarone-kairos, zyme-harlequin-veronese-igt

Via Ca' del Pipa 1, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Brigaldara ★ 4.6

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, OseletaFounded 1986Cesari familyValpolicella Classico

A Cesari family estate in the heart of Valpolicella Classico, known for precise, age-worthy Amarone and a careful approach to appassimento in the estate's traditional fruttai.

Tasting: Estate visits and tastings by appointment at San Pietro in Cariano.

Signature wines: brigaldara-amarone-classico, brigaldara-il-vegro-amarone

Via Brigaldara 20, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Ca' La Bionda ★ 4.5

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraFounded 1902Castellani familyWinemaker Alessandro CastellaniValpolicella Classico

Family-run estate at the top of the Marano valley producing structured, traditional Amarone and Valpolicella from old vineyards on volcanic soils.

Tasting: Visits and tastings by appointment on the Marano hillside estate.

Signature wines: ca-la-bionda-amarone-classico, ca-la-bionda-valpolicella-classico

Via Bionda 4, 37020 Marano di Valpolicella, Verona, Italy → Book →

Cantina Valpolicella Negrar ★ 4.3

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, RondinellaFounded 1933Cooperative, 230 member familiesWinemaker Cooperative winemaking teamValpolicella Classico

Founded in 1933, this cooperative represents 230 family growers at the heart of Valpolicella Classico. The Domaso single-vineyard Amarone from a 14-hectare parcel on the Maternigo hill is the flagship. Open cellar door with no appointment needed.

Tasting: Shop and tasting room open to visitors in Negrar; walk-in friendly.

Signature wines: cantina-negrar-amarone-classico, cantina-negrar-domaso

Via Ca' Salgari 2, 37024 Negrar di Valpolicella, Verona, Italy → Book →

Nicolis Angelo e Figli ★ 4.5

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraFounded 1951Nicolis familyWinemaker Nicolis familyValpolicella Classico

The Nicolis family has farmed the Villa Girardi estate in San Pietro in Cariano since 1951. The Ambrosan single-vineyard Amarone from the oldest family vines is their prestige bottling.

Tasting: Wine visits and tastings at San Pietro in Cariano by appointment.

Signature wines: nicolis-amarone-classico, nicolis-ambrosan-amarone

Via Villa Girardi 29, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Le Salette ★ 4.6

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, RondinellaFounded 1986Franco ScamperleWinemaker Monica ScamperleValpolicella Classico

A high-altitude Fumane estate farmed by the Scamperle family since 1986 producing Amarone Pergole Vece from century-old Corvina vines at 450 metres elevation. Monica Scamperle is one of Valpolicella's few female winemakers.

Tasting: Cellar visits and tastings by appointment in Fumane.

Signature wines: le-salette-amarone-pergole-vece, le-salette-valpolicella-classico

Via Pio Brugnoli 11, 37022 Fumane, Verona, Italy → Book →

Santa Sofia ★ 4.4

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Rondinella, CorvinoneFounded 1811Begnoni familyWinemaker Begnoni familyValpolicella Classico

Operating from the 16th-century Villa Santa Sofia in Pedemonte, this producer has been bottling Valpolicella wines since 1811. The Begnoni family combination of history and good-value Amarone attracts steady wine tourism.

Tasting: Villa Santa Sofia open for visits and tastings; the historic loggia attributed to Palladio is part of the tour.

Signature wines: santa-sofia-amarone-classico, santa-sofia-valpolicella-classico

Via Ca' Dede 61, 37020 Pedemonte di San Pietro in Cariano, Verona, Italy → Book →

Valentina Cubi ★ 4.5

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, OseletaFounded 1969Valentina CubiWinemaker Valentina CubiORGANIC CERTIFIEDValpolicella Classico

Valentina Cubi's certified organic estate on the Fumane hillsides produces small-production Amarone and Valpolicella with a focus on indigenous varieties and minimal intervention in both vineyard and cellar.

Tasting: Visits and tastings by appointment at the Fumane estate.

Signature wines: valentina-cubi-amarone-morar, valentina-cubi-valpolicella-classico

Via Casterne 60, 37022 Fumane, Verona, Italy → Book →

Allegrini Estate Wine Shop and Tasting Room ★ 4.7

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, OseletaWalk-ins welcomeFumane, Valpolicella Classica

The Allegrini estate wine shop in Fumane is always open for informal tastings of Valpolicella Classico, Ripasso, Amarone della Valpolicella, Recioto di Valpolicella, and the innovative La Poja monovarietal Corvina IGT. Over 100 ha of family vineyards. Tel +39 045 6832011.

Tip: La Poja is a single-vineyard monovarietal Corvina available only at the estate and a handful of Verona restaurants - the walk-in shop is the easiest access.

Via Giare 5, 37022 Fumane di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

Cantine Bertani Tasting Room ★ 4.7

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraAppointment onlyGrezzana, Valpolicella

Bertani's renewed historic tasting spaces preserve 45 vintages of Amarone della Valpolicella Classico alongside new contemporary design elements. The Library Experience (EUR200) accesses back-vintages from 1958; the standard Valpolicella experience (EUR70) covers current and recent releases. Book via bertani.net/esperienze.

Tip: The Library Experience is among the most significant Amarone vertical tastings available anywhere in Italy - plan 3 hours.

Via Asiago 1, 37023 Grezzana (VR), Italy →

Zyme Tasting Room ★ 4.7

Corvina, Corvinone, Oseleta, Merlot, SyrahAppointment onlySan Pietro in Cariano, Valpolicella Classica

Celestino Gaspari's Zyme winery is carved into a 15th-century sandstone quarry. The tasting room inside the quarry maintains constant temperature and presents 6+ Zyme wines including single-vineyard Valpolicella, Ripasso, Amarone, and blended IGT expressions. Highly rated; book one week ahead.

Tip: The Kairos blend (15 grape varieties) is Gaspari's philosophical statement about Valpolicella terroir - the most unusual pour in the tasting.

Via Ca del Pipa 1, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano (VR), Italy →

Masi Monteleone21 Wine Bar and Tasting ★ 4.8

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraWalk-ins welcomeSant'Ambrogio di Valpolicella

Opened 2025, Monteleone21 is Masi's new 'open cellar' paradigm in Valpolicella Classica. The Wine Bar 'Vino e Cucina' is walk-in accessible. Structured tours include the fruttaio appassimento loft, Possessioni Serego Alighieri cellars, and hillside vineyards. Most complete public-access Amarone production experience in the region.

Tip: Arrive before 11:00 for structured tours; the Wine Bar opens at 12:00 for informal tastings with food pairings.

Via Monteleone 26, 37015 Sant'Ambrogio di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

Massimago Cantina Tasting Room ★ 4.5

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MerlotAppointment onlyMezzane di Sotto, Valpolicella

Massimago's estate tasting in the Mezzane Valley combines a walk through certified organic vineyards with a visit to the historic Amarone ageing cellar. The estate produces certified organic Valpolicella, Ripasso, and Amarone. Mezzane is an emerging sub-zone rarely covered by standard Veneto wine tours.

Tip: The organic certification and Mezzane sub-zone origin are the differentiators here - ask about the estate's transition to biodynamics.

Via Giare 21, 37030 Mezzane di Sotto (VR), Italy →

Villa Cordevigo Wine Relais Cellar Tasting ★ 4.6

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, GarganegaAppointment onlyBardolino, Cavaion Veronese

The wine cellar tasting at Villa Cordevigo presents estate wines from Vigneti Villabella (Garda DOC and Valpolicella DOC). The wine and history experience package is curated for both hotel guests and day visitors. Set in the Cavaion Veronese hills with views over vineyards and olive groves.

Tip: The wine and history package pairs cellar tasting with a guided narrative of the villa and estate - book this format rather than standalone tasting for full context.

Localita Cordevigo snc, 37010 Cavaion Veronese (VR), Italy →

Tenuta Le Cave Organic Cellar Tasting ★ 4.5

Corvina, Corvinone, RondinellaAppointment onlyTregnago, Valpolicella

Tenuta Le Cave's organic and biodynamic cellar tasting presents Valpolicella, Ripasso, and Amarone from estate vineyards in Tregnago. The Wine Lovers package is the recommended format - combining vineyard walk, cellar visit, and discovery tasting of the full estate range. Biodynamic Amarone is the anchor.

Tip: Tregnago sits between Valpolicella and Soave - ask about the Lessinia influence on the estate's vineyard soils, which distinguishes the Le Cave style.

Via Pagnaghe 5, 37039 Tregnago (VR), Italy →

Daniele Piccinin ★ 4.5

Valpolicella Classico DOC / Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina Veronese, Corvinone, Rondinella, OseletaValpolicella Classico, Fumane

A tiny estate in Fumane making organic Valpolicella and Amarone by hand with no added sulfites in the cellar. Piccinin's wines are structured and earthy, beloved by the Italian natural-wine community.

Tip: The Valpolicella Classico is the entry point; ask at Verona wine bars like Osteria del Bugiardo whether they stock a current vintage.

Via Coste 2, 37022 Fumane, Italy →

Monte dall'Ora ★ 4.6

Valpolicella Classico Superiore DOC / Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina Veronese, Corvinone, Rondinella, Oseleta, MolinaraDemeter CertifiedValpolicella Classico, San Pietro in Cariano

One of Valpolicella's few Demeter-certified biodynamic estates, run by Carlo and Alessandra Venturini in Castelrotto. Long-maceration Valpolicella and Amarone with no fining or filtering; the wines age particularly well.

Tip: The Saseti Valpolicella Classico Superiore is the accessible entry wine; visit the estate in Castelrotto for a hillside tasting with Alessandra.

Loc. Castelrotto, Via Monte dall'Ora 5, 37024 San Pietro in Cariano, Italy →

Corte Sant'Alda Valpolicella Classico DOC ★ 4.2

Valpolicella Classico DOCCorvina Veronese, Corvinone, Rondinella, Molinaraunder-EUR-25still-red

The entry Valpolicella Classico from Marinella Camerani's Demeter-certified biodynamic estate in Mezzane di Sotto. Light and precise, showing fresh cherry, herbs and the estate's characteristic biodynamic vitality. Among the few certified biodynamic Valpolicella Classico wines available under EUR 20.

Tip: An ideal gateway wine for natural-wine drinkers discovering Valpolicella; shows how the appellation can produce light, precise reds from biodynamic hillside farming.
