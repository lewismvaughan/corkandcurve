Tasting Rooms in Veneto

Allegrini Estate Wine Shop and Tasting Room ★ 4.7

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, OseletaWalk-ins welcomeFumane, Valpolicella Classica

The Allegrini estate wine shop in Fumane is always open for informal tastings of Valpolicella Classico, Ripasso, Amarone della Valpolicella, Recioto di Valpolicella, and the innovative La Poja monovarietal Corvina IGT. Over 100 ha of family vineyards. Tel +39 045 6832011.

Tip: La Poja is a single-vineyard monovarietal Corvina available only at the estate and a handful of Verona restaurants - the walk-in shop is the easiest access.

Via Giare 5, 37022 Fumane di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

Cantina del Castello Tasting Room ★ 4.5

Garganega, Trebbiano di SoaveWalk-ins welcomeSoave, Verona Province

In the heart of the medieval village of Soave at the foot of Scaliger Castle, Cantina del Castello has produced Soave Classico in the 13th-century Palazzo dei Conti Sambonifacio since the 1960s. The cellar is open Mon-Fri 8:00-18:30 and Sat 10:00-17:00 for tastings and retail.

Tip: Ask for the Pressoni single-vineyard cru - the volcanic basalt terroir character is unmistakable and shows what separates Soave Classico from the broader DOC.

Vicolo Corte Pittora 5, 37038 Soave (VR), Italy →

Cantine Bertani Tasting Room ★ 4.7

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraAppointment onlyGrezzana, Valpolicella

Bertani's renewed historic tasting spaces preserve 45 vintages of Amarone della Valpolicella Classico alongside new contemporary design elements. The Library Experience (EUR200) accesses back-vintages from 1958; the standard Valpolicella experience (EUR70) covers current and recent releases. Book via bertani.net/esperienze.

Tip: The Library Experience is among the most significant Amarone vertical tastings available anywhere in Italy - plan 3 hours.

Via Asiago 1, 37023 Grezzana (VR), Italy →

Zyme Tasting Room ★ 4.7

Corvina, Corvinone, Oseleta, Merlot, SyrahAppointment onlySan Pietro in Cariano, Valpolicella Classica

Celestino Gaspari's Zyme winery is carved into a 15th-century sandstone quarry. The tasting room inside the quarry maintains constant temperature and presents 6+ Zyme wines including single-vineyard Valpolicella, Ripasso, Amarone, and blended IGT expressions. Highly rated; book one week ahead.

Tip: The Kairos blend (15 grape varieties) is Gaspari's philosophical statement about Valpolicella terroir - the most unusual pour in the tasting.

Via Ca del Pipa 1, 37029 San Pietro in Cariano (VR), Italy →

Masi Monteleone21 Wine Bar and Tasting ★ 4.8

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MolinaraWalk-ins welcomeSant'Ambrogio di Valpolicella

Opened 2025, Monteleone21 is Masi's new 'open cellar' paradigm in Valpolicella Classica. The Wine Bar 'Vino e Cucina' is walk-in accessible. Structured tours include the fruttaio appassimento loft, Possessioni Serego Alighieri cellars, and hillside vineyards. Most complete public-access Amarone production experience in the region.

Tip: Arrive before 11:00 for structured tours; the Wine Bar opens at 12:00 for informal tastings with food pairings.

Via Monteleone 26, 37015 Sant'Ambrogio di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

Consorzio Prosecco Superiore DOCG - Valdobbiadene Tasting Centre ★ 4.5

Glera, Perera, Bianchetta TrevigianaAppointment onlySolighetto, Valdobbiadene

The official Consorzio Conegliano Valdobbiadene Prosecco Superiore DOCG tasting centre at the 18th-century Villa Brandolini in Solighetto. Best reference for understanding the appellation - Rive single-vineyard Proseccos, Cartizze DOCG, and Colfondo styles represented. Contact the consortium office at info@coneglianovaldobbiadene.it.

Tip: Ask specifically for a Rive horizontal tasting - comparing three single-vineyard Proseccos from different communes reveals the terroir variation most clearly.

Via Brandolini 39, 31020 Solighetto di Pieve di Soligo (TV), Italy →

Tenuta Santa Maria Valverde Tasting Room ★ 4.6

Corvina, Rondinella, OseletaAppointment onlyMarano di Valpolicella

Boutique winery in Marano di Valpolicella with a 17th-century cellar used for wine refinement. Tasting room accessible via the Bike Station Valpolicella e-bike experience or standalone by appointment. Produces Valpolicella Classico, Ripasso, and Amarone from estate vineyards in the Marano sub-zone.

Tip: The 17th-century stone cellar maintains ideal ageing conditions for the Amarone - ask to see the oldest barrels in the back room during the tasting.

Localita Gazzo 4, 37020 Marano di Valpolicella (VR), Italy →

Poli Grappateca Tasting Bar Bassano ★ 4.5

Grappa di Glera (Prosecco), Grappa di Moscato, Grappa di AmaroneWalk-ins welcomePonte Vecchio, Bassano del Grappa

The walk-in tasting bar and retail space at the Poli Grappa Museum, daily 9:00-19:30. The EUR3.50 guided tasting pours five different Poli grappas covering white, flavoured, and aged expressions. The adjacent museum with 1,500+ miniature bottles is free to visit without tasting.

Tip: The Sarpa di Poli (Prosecco pomace grappa) and the Amorosa (Moscato pomace) show the range of Veneto grape-spirit character at low cost.

Via Gamba 6, 36061 Bassano del Grappa (VI), Italy →

Massimago Cantina Tasting Room ★ 4.5

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, MerlotAppointment onlyMezzane di Sotto, Valpolicella

Massimago's estate tasting in the Mezzane Valley combines a walk through certified organic vineyards with a visit to the historic Amarone ageing cellar. The estate produces certified organic Valpolicella, Ripasso, and Amarone. Mezzane is an emerging sub-zone rarely covered by standard Veneto wine tours.

Tip: The organic certification and Mezzane sub-zone origin are the differentiators here - ask about the estate's transition to biodynamics.

Via Giare 21, 37030 Mezzane di Sotto (VR), Italy →

Villa Cordevigo Wine Relais Cellar Tasting ★ 4.6

Corvina, Corvinone, Rondinella, GarganegaAppointment onlyBardolino, Cavaion Veronese

The wine cellar tasting at Villa Cordevigo presents estate wines from Vigneti Villabella (Garda DOC and Valpolicella DOC). The wine and history experience package is curated for both hotel guests and day visitors. Set in the Cavaion Veronese hills with views over vineyards and olive groves.

Tip: The wine and history package pairs cellar tasting with a guided narrative of the villa and estate - book this format rather than standalone tasting for full context.

Localita Cordevigo snc, 37010 Cavaion Veronese (VR), Italy →

Grapperia Nardini - Tasting and Tour ★ 4.8

Grappa di Glera, Grappa Riserva, Aqua VitaeAppointment onlyBassano del Grappa

The Grapperia Nardini sul Ponte is the tasting and retail hub of Italy's oldest distillery (est. 1779) on the foot of the historic Ponte degli Alpini. Guided tours through 18th-century production rooms end with a grappa flight. Tue-Sat only. The principal grappa tasting room in the Veneto.

Tip: Arrive early - morning tours show the production rooms in best light and the guide is more expansive with smaller groups.

Ponte Vecchio 2, 36061 Bassano del Grappa (VI), Italy →

Distilleria Andrea Da Ponte Tasting Room ★ 4.5

Grappa di Glera (Prosecco), Aged Grappa, Fruit SpiritsAppointment onlyCorbanese di Tarzo, UNESCO Prosecco hills

Three-tier tasting room experience at Distilleria Andrea Da Ponte in the Conegliano Valdobbiadene UNESCO hills. Tasting Tour (30 min), History and Nature Tour (1h15), and Founder's Tour (1h30) with library spirits. Wheelchair accessible; free parking. Book via visite@daponte.it.

Tip: The Founder's Tour opens grappa vintages from the Da Ponte historical archive - unique access not available in standard tour formats.

Via Primo Maggio 1, 31020 Corbanese di Tarzo (TV), Italy →

Tenuta Le Cave Organic Cellar Tasting ★ 4.5

Corvina, Corvinone, RondinellaAppointment onlyTregnago, Valpolicella

Tenuta Le Cave's organic and biodynamic cellar tasting presents Valpolicella, Ripasso, and Amarone from estate vineyards in Tregnago. The Wine Lovers package is the recommended format - combining vineyard walk, cellar visit, and discovery tasting of the full estate range. Biodynamic Amarone is the anchor.

Tip: Tregnago sits between Valpolicella and Soave - ask about the Lessinia influence on the estate's vineyard soils, which distinguishes the Le Cave style.

Via Pagnaghe 5, 37039 Tregnago (VR), Italy →

Vigneti Villabella Tasting Room ★ 4.6

Corvina, Garganega, Bardolino grapesAppointment onlyBardolino, Cavaion Veronese

Vigneti Villabella is the estate winery of Villa Cordevigo Wine Relais, producing Garda DOC and Valpolicella DOC wines from the surrounding vineyards and olive groves. Cellar tastings for hotel guests and day visitors. The estate also houses the five-star Villa Cordevigo Wine Relais and the one-Michelin-star Oseleta restaurant.

Tip: Request the full Villabella flight including the Bardolino Chiaretto rosato - underrated and highly food-friendly with the estate's kitchen.

Localita Cordevigo snc, 37010 Cavaion Veronese (VR), Italy →

Alice Relais - Le Vigne di Alice Prosecco Tasting ★ 4.5

GleraAppointment onlyCarpesica, Vittorio Veneto, UNESCO Prosecco hills

The Le Vigne di Alice winery tasting room at Alice Relais nelle Vigne in Carpesica. The estate produces artisanal, organic Prosecco DOCG - dry Brut and Brut Nature styles made with no herbicides. Doro Nature is the signature. Estate-grown Glera from the UNESCO Prosecco hills. Tel +39 0438 920818.

Tip: The Doro Nature brut-zero is the furthest stylistically from commercial Prosecco - worth seeking out for serious sparkling-wine tasters.

Via Giardino 94, Localita Carpesica, 31029 Vittorio Veneto (TV), Italy →

Tasting Rooms in Veneto, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Veneto for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Veneto is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Veneto estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Veneto cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Veneto tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Veneto?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Veneto rewards trust.
