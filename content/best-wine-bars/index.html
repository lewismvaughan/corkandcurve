Best Wine Bars Worldwide

50 editor-picked rooms across every Cork & Curve region.

Updated May 2026

50 best wine bars worth the trip across every Cork & Curve region. Editor-ranked. natural and classic wine bars worldwide.

Au Bon Manger ★ 4.7 · Champagne

Reims centre · 7 rue Courmeaux, 51100 Reims, France

A neighbourhood bar, cave and kitchen on a quiet Reims street, run by Aline and Eric Serva. The focus is small-scale, organic and biodynamic champagne with local.

Locale Firenze ★ 4.7 · Tuscany

Centro Storico, Florence · Via delle Seggiole 12, 50122 Firenze, Italy

Inside the 13th-century Palazzo Concini with multiple floors including a Renaissance courtyard. Ranked 22 on World's 50 Best Bars 2025; sommelier-led pairings alongside cocktail bar programme.

Vino Vero ★ 4.7 · Veneto

Cannaregio, Venice · Fondamenta Misericordia 2497, 30121 Venezia (Cannaregio), Italy

Opened 2014 by Matteo and Mara Bartoli, Vino Vero was the first natural-wine-only bar in Venice. Over 600 labels from artisanal producers; creative cicchetti with unusual ingredients. Canal-side position on Fondamenta della Misericordia.

Tip: Check the chalk-board for the daily col fondo Prosecco pour - usually a different producer each week.

Antica Bottega del Vino ★ 4.7 · Veneto

Centro storico, Verona · Vicolo Scudo di Francia 3, 37121 Verona (VR), Italy

Verona institution combining restaurant, wine bar, and wine cellar. Wine Spectator Grand Award recipient since 2004; cellar deep in Italian and international labels. Daily from 11:00 to 01:00; serious wine list with knowledgeable sommelier team.

Tip: The private cellar tour (ask in advance) shows historic vintages going back to the 1940s.

Le Chapon Fin Bar a Vins ★ 4.6 · Bordeaux

Triangle d'Or, Bordeaux · 5 rue Montesquieu, 33000 Bordeaux, France

The historic Chapon Fin brasserie's ground-floor wine bar, one of Bordeaux's most celebrated cellars with 600 references. Flights across all major Bordeaux appellations; full food menu available.

Loiseau des Vignes Wine Bar ★ 4.6 · Burgundy

Beaune town centre · 31 Rue Maufoux, 21200 Beaune, France

The by-the-glass programme at the Michelin-starred Loiseau des Vignes near the Hospices, among the first in Europe to pour 70-plus wines by the glass, curated by MOF sommelier Eric Goettelmann.

Le Wine Bar by Le Vintage ★ 4.6 · Champagne

Place du Forum, Reims · 16 Place du Forum, 51100 Reims, France

The bar of the Le Vintage wine shop beside the Place du Forum in Reims, run by the Papavero brothers, with a vast list of grower and house champagnes.

Enoteca Pitti Gola e Cantina ★ 4.6 · Tuscany

Oltrarno, Florence · Piazza Pitti 16, 50125 Firenze, Italy

Wine shop and bar facing the Pitti Palace, focused on small-production handcrafted Tuscan wines. Tasting menus engineered around the bottle list; dinner service runs to 22:30.

Enoteca Al Volto ★ 4.6 · Veneto

San Marco, Venice · Calle Cavalli 4081, 30124 Venezia (San Marco), Italy

Founded in 1936 near the Rialto Bridge, Al Volto pours from a list of over 1,000 bottles with a strong Veneto backbone. Wooden-panelled interior plastered with wine labels; traditional cicchetti served alongside regional selections.

Tip: Open 10:00-16:00 and 18:00-22:00; arrive early evening to find a spot at the bar before the tourist rush.

Estro Vino e Cucina ★ 4.6 · Veneto

Dorsoduro, Venice · Calle Crosera, Dorsoduro 3778, 30123 Venezia, Italy

Opened 2014 by brothers Alberto and Dario Spezzamonte at the Dorsoduro / San Polo boundary. MICHELIN Guide Good Cooking designation 2025. Over 700 ecologically sustainable wines from small European producers; cicchetti and full dinner menu.

Tip: Closed Tuesdays; open Mon and Wed-Sat 11:00-midnight.

Millevini Enoteca ★ 4.6 · Veneto

Rialto, San Marco, Venice · Ramo del Fontego dei Tedeschi, San Marco 5362, 30124 Venezia, Italy

Founded 2001 near the Rialto by certified sommelier Lorenzo Menegus. Every bottle is personally tasted and selected by Lorenzo. Strong Veneto and Friuli backbone; also carries grappa, rum, whisky, gin, and balsamic. One of Venice's most acclaimed wine shops.

Tip: Tell Lorenzo your flavour profile and budget - his personal recommendations consistently overdeliver.

Prova ★ 4.6 · Douro Valley

Ribeira, Porto · Rua de Ferreira Borges 86, 4050-252 Porto, Portugal

Founded by sommelier Diogo Amado near Palacio da Bolsa, Prova is a calm jazz-lit room built around a wide-ranging Portuguese by-the-glass list and guided flights.

Tastavin Vinos y Pinchos ★ 4.6 · Rioja

Calle San Juan, Logrono · Calle de San Juan 25, 26001 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain

Small Calle San Juan wine bar using Coravin to pour rare bottles by the glass; a Rioja-deep list matched to refined pintxos.

Cafe Lavinal ★ 4.5 · Bordeaux

Bages, Pauillac · Place Desquet, 33250 Bages, France

Cazes family-run village bar and bistro in the beautifully restored Bages hamlet, offering Lynch-Bages and Medoc pours alongside traditional Girondine food; popular lunch stop on Medoc vineyard tours.

La Dilettante ★ 4.5 · Burgundy

Beaune · 11 Rue du Faubourg Bretonniere, 21200 Beaune, France

Cosy bodega-style wine bar just outside Beaune's ring road, founded by 'Lolo' formerly of Caves Madeleine. Burgundy-led natural wine list poured beside seasonal small plates.

Le Coq Rouge ★ 4.5 · Champagne

Reims centre · 67 rue Chanzy, 51100 Reims, France

A lively champagne and wine bar on rue Chanzy near Reims Cathedral, serving small plates with cuvees from growers and houses. A favourite of visiting winemakers.

Sacre Bistro ★ 4.5 · Champagne

Epernay centre · 2-4 Place Auban Moet, 51200 Epernay, France

A youthful bistro on the Place Auban-Moet in Epernay with a serious champagne-by-the-glass and natural-wine list. Local, seasonal, often organic plates accompany.

C. Comme Bar a Champagne ★ 4.5 · Champagne

Epernay centre · 8 rue Gambetta, 51200 Epernay, France

The bar side of C. Comme on rue Gambetta in Epernay, pouring dozens of grower champagnes by the glass. Cheese and charcuterie boards make it a full evening stop.

Il Santino ★ 4.5 · Tuscany

Santo Spirito, Florence · Via di Santo Spirito 60R, 50125 Firenze, Italy

Tiny Santo Spirito wine bar in a former cellar, sister venue to Il Santo Bevitore next door. Tuscan-favoured list with small artisan producers; standing-room counter and a handful of bar stools.

Enoteca Tognoni ★ 4.5 · Tuscany

Bolgheri · Via Lauretta 5, 57020 Bolgheri, Italy

The village wine bar of Bolgheri, established 1965 just off the Cypress Avenue. Full Bolgheri DOC roster on bottle and a long-standing relationship with the founding producers of the appellation.

Cantina Do Mori ★ 4.5 · Veneto

Rialto, San Polo, Venice · Sestiere San Polo 429, 30125 Venezia, Italy

Venice's oldest bacaro, established 1462 and hidden in an alley near the Rialto. Double entrance from two narrow calle; an institution for ombre (small wine pours) with traditional cicchetti mini-sandwiches. Mon-Sat 8:00-19:30.

Tip: Go at 11:00 when the freshest cicchetti are laid out; Sunday is closed.

La Cantina ★ 4.5 · Veneto

Cannaregio, Venice · Cannaregio 3689, Campo San Felice, 30121 Venezia, Italy

Hidden corner of Campo San Felice in Cannaregio; La Cantina prepares all cicchetti to order using the highest-quality local ingredients. Cosy interior, outdoor campo seats, and staff who greet regulars as old friends. Phone +39 041 522 8258.

Tip: The tartare cicchetti changes daily depending on what arrived at the Rialto fish market that morning.

Osteria del Bugiardo ★ 4.5 · Veneto

Verona centro storico · Corso Porta Borsari 17/A, 37121 Verona (VR), Italy

Wine bar and osteria on Verona's ancient Corso Porta Borsari owned by Buglioni winery. Exclusive Buglioni list at affable prices; simple cicchetti, charcuterie, and cheeses. Sun-Thu 11:00-midnight, Fri-Sat 11:00-01:00.

Tip: The Amarone by the glass is a rare bargain - Buglioni keeps house pricing far below comparable Amarone bars in Verona.

Enoteca Dal Zovo ★ 4.5 · Veneto

Borgo Trento, Verona · Via IV Novembre 12/A, 37126 Verona (VR), Italy

Established 1958, now in its third generation with Mattia Dal Zovo. Situated across the Adige from Verona's historic centre on fashionable Via IV Novembre. Ask to see the cellar and the sommelier will guide selections from the deep Veneto and Italian list.

Tip: The cellar visit is free for serious wine-seekers - just ask Mattia.

I Rusteghi ★ 4.5 · Veneto

San Marco, Venice · Corte del Tentor, San Marco 5513, 30124 Venezia, Italy

Hidden in a quiet courtyard just behind the Rialto in San Marco, I Rusteghi is a sommelier-run bacaro where the wine list takes priority. Strong Veneto and Friuli backbone with thoughtful cicchetti pairings. A locals' favourite for serious ombre.

Tip: The courtyard is easy to miss - look for the small sign at the mouth of Calle del Tentor.

Vinologia ★ 4.5 · Douro Valley

Ribeira, Porto · Rua de São João 28-30, 4050-552 Porto, Portugal

Also styled La Maison des Porto, this snug Ribeira room specialises in Port from independent grower houses, with knowledgeable staff guiding tastings of small-production bottlings.

Capela Incomum ★ 4.5 · Douro Valley

Centro, Porto · Travessa do Carregal 77-79-81, 4050-167 Porto, Portugal

A wine bar set inside a former chapel near the Carregal gardens, pairing a Douro-leaning Portuguese list with Iberian tapas under vaulted stone ceilings.

Castas e Pratos Wine Bar ★ 4.5 · Douro Valley

Peso da Régua · Rua José Vasques Osório, 5050-280 Peso da Régua, Portugal

The wine-bar counter of the Castas e Pratos warehouse in Régua, drawing on a 600-bottle cellar weighted to Douro and Port for by-the-glass pours overlooking the rail tracks.

Wine Fandango ★ 4.5 · Rioja

El Espolon, Logrono · Calle Vara de Rey 5, 26003 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain

Wine bar and kitchen in an Espolon palace from two Rioja-trade brothers; deep by-the-glass board, flights and a vermouth-led aperitivo.

L'Oiseau Vins ★ 4.4 · Bordeaux

Capucins, Bordeaux · 12 rue des Faussets, 33000 Bordeaux, France

Compact wine bar near Marche des Capucins run by a sommelier couple; rotating 80-bottle list emphasises satellite appellations and biodynamic producers often overlooked by mainstream cellars.

Cepage Wine Bar ★ 4.4 · Bordeaux

Saint-Emilion village · 13 rue Guadet, 33330 Saint-Emilion, France

Village wine bar in the heart of Saint-Emilion with a Right Bank-focused list and regular producer vertical tastings. Terrace seating with views over the medieval village.

Le Bistrot Bourguignon ★ 4.4 · Burgundy

Beaune town centre · 8 Rue Monge, 21200 Beaune, France

Long-running wine bar on Rue Monge pouring more than 20 Burgundies by the glass. Owner Christophe Meux trained at La Cote d'Or; the list favours biodynamic and traditional growers.

Le Comptoir des Tontons ★ 4.4 · Burgundy

Beaune · 22 Rue du Faubourg Madeleine, 21200 Beaune, France

Faubourg Madeleine bistro run by Romain Escoffier where Beaune's winemakers come to drink. Organic-leaning Burgundy list by the glass beside locally-sourced share plates.

The Glue Pot ★ 4.4 · Champagne

Place Drouet d'Erlon, Reims · 49 place Drouet d'Erlon, 51100 Reims, France

A long-running spot on the Place Drouet d'Erlon in Reims that grew from an English pub into a champagne and wine lounge. Boutique grower cuvees and a winemaker.

Bar Brut ★ 4.4 · Champagne

Avenue de Champagne, Epernay · 25T Avenue de Champagne, 51200 Epernay, France

A grower-champagne bar on the Avenue de Champagne in Epernay, pouring small-producer cuvees by the glass. A relaxed stop between the avenue house cellars.

Les Grands Vins de France ★ 4.4 · Champagne

Epernay centre · 6 rue Flodoard, 51200 Epernay, France

A grower-champagne cave and bar in central Epernay carrying more than 50 independent producers. A weekly five-cuvee tasting is poured by the glass at 7.50 euros.

Weinstube Spitzhaeuschen ★ 4.4 · Mosel

Bernkastel old town · Karlstrasse 13, 54470 Bernkastel-Kues, Germany

Wine tavern inside a leaning 1416 half-timbered landmark off the Marktplatz, run as a Weinstube by the Schmitz-Herges family since 1970. Over 50 regional Rieslings and reds in cramped, characterful rooms.

Weinstube Kesselstatt ★ 4.4 · Mosel

Trier cathedral quarter · Liebfrauenstrasse 10, 54290 Trier, Germany

Wine tavern of the 675-year-old Kesselstatt estate beside Trier's Dom and Liebfrauen cathedral. Historic vaulted rooms and a garden terrace pour the estate's Mosel and Saar Rieslings.

Vincafe ★ 4.4 · Piedmont

Alba · Via Vittorio Emanuele 12, 12051 Alba CN, Italy

Wine bar and cafe on Alba's main pedestrian street pouring a rotating Langhe selection by the glass. Counter seating with truffle and cheese plates designed to anchor the Nebbiolo-led list.

Bar Cavour ★ 4.4 · Piedmont

Piazza Carignano, Turin · Piazza Carignano 2, 10123 Torino TO, Italy

The evening bar of the historic Del Cambio restaurant, set above Piazza Carignano. Vermouth-driven aperitivo and a Piedmont-strong wine list in a sophisticated, dimly-lit room.

Uve Rooms and Wine Bar ★ 4.4 · Piedmont

La Morra, Barolo · Via Umberto I 13, 12064 La Morra CN, Italy

Wine bar in the former 18th-century San Sebastiano convent in La Morra, with a Barolo-led by-the-glass list drawn from local cru producers. Pairing plates of Langhe classics in a vaulted room.

Casa del Vino ★ 4.4 · Tuscany

San Lorenzo, Florence · Via dell'Ariento 16R, 50123 Firenze, Italy

Behind the San Lorenzo market under a colonnade, this 19th-century enoteca is run by the Migliorini family in its fourth generation. Wine by the glass alongside crostini and made-to-order panini.

Enoteca Grotta del Brunello ★ 4.4 · Tuscany

Brunello di Montalcino · Via Mazzini 24, 53024 Montalcino, Italy

Run by Marco Capitoni since 1985 in the heart of Montalcino, with vaulted cellar tasting rooms below the shop. Pairings of Brunello with sliced pici, cinghiale and pecorino di Pienza.

Fuori Porta ★ 4.4 · Tuscany

San Niccolo, Florence · Via del Monte alle Croci 10R, 50125 Firenze, Italy

Long-running San Niccolo enoteca with a 650-label cellar leaning hard on Tuscan classics. Crostoni and pasta plates designed to anchor the by-the-glass menu; terrace below the medieval gate.

Cantine del Vino Gia Schiavi ★ 4.4 · Veneto

Dorsoduro, Venice · Fondamenta Nani 992, 30123 Venezia (Dorsoduro), Italy

Known as 'Al Bottegon', this historic Dorsoduro bacaro dates to 1800. Standing-only wine bar and enoteca with 500+ labels from Italian producers. Sandwich cicchetti prepared to order at lunch. A neighbourhood institution beloved by locals and wine lovers.

Tip: Arrive at 12:30 for the best sandwich selection before the lunch crowd depletes the board.

Enoteca del Soave ★ 4.4 · Veneto

Soave, Verona Province · Via Roma 19, 37038 Soave (VR), Italy

Founded 1980 in the medieval village of Soave; returned to direct management by Cantina del Castello in April 2024. The best point-of-access for Soave and Valpolicella wines by the glass, with private room available for groups upstairs. Contact enotecasoave@gmail.com.

Tip: Order the Lessini Durello metodo classico - this Veneto sparkling made from Durella grapes is one of the region's best-kept secrets.

Enoteca Baraldi ★ 4.4 · Veneto

Centro storico, Verona · Via Roma 10/C, 37121 Verona (VR), Italy

Family-owned enoteca in Verona's historic centre led by owner Bruno Baraldi, known for his organic and sustainably farmed selections with deep local knowledge. Also offers wine tastings through the Winedering platform. A retail and tasting hybrid.

Tip: Ask Bruno about biodynamic Valpolicella producers - he sources directly from smaller estates not widely distributed.

Cantina del Castello ★ 4.4 · Veneto

Soave, Verona Province · Vicolo Corte Pittora 5, 37038 Soave (VR), Italy

Located at the foot of Soave Castle in the 13th-century Palazzo dei Conti Sambonifacio, Cantina del Castello has produced Soave Classico since the 1960s. Also operates the Enoteca del Soave retail outlet. Direct estate tastings in the historic cellar.

Tip: The Pressoni single-vineyard Soave shows the volcanic basalt character that distinguishes the Classico zone.

Wine Quay Bar ★ 4.4 · Douro Valley

Ribeira, Porto · Rua Cais da Estiva 111, 4050-080 Porto, Portugal

A Ribeira riverfront wine bar overlooking the Dom Luis I bridge, with one wall built from a 17th-century aqueduct and a Portuguese list paired to small plates.

Arco das Verdades ★ 4.4 · Douro Valley

Ribeira, Porto · Escada das Verdades 13, 4050-622 Porto, Portugal

A small terraced wine bar above the Ribeira with large windows framing the Luis I bridge and the river, focused on the wines of northern Portugal.
