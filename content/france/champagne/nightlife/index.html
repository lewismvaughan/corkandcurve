Wine Bars Late in Champagne

Le Wine Bar by Le Vintage ★ 4.6

30 by the glassPlace du Forum (Reims)

Family-run wine bar on the Place du Forum with a deep grower-Champagne list and a late, convivial room. A Reims institution for terroir-driven bubbles.

The list: Around 500 references of wine, Champagne and spirits, with a strong line in grower and biodynamic Champagne alongside the big houses

Tip: Brothers Nicolas and Pierre-Louis Papavero lean into grower fizz; ask them to open a biodynamic Champagne by the glass.

16 Place du Forum, 51100 Reims, France →

La Vinocave ★ 4.3

20 by the glassReims centre

Wine shop and tasting bar open since 1985, pouring small-domaine Champagnes with food into the evening near the Reims centre.

The list: A wide selection of small-domaine Champagnes and still wines for tasting on site and to take away, running since 1985

Tip: Long-running cave a manger; the team pairs small-grower Champagnes with cheese and charcuterie into the evening.

43 Rue de Chativesle, 51100 Reims, France →

Le Bocal ★ 4.4

12 by the glassBoulingrin (Reims)

Tiny seafood spot behind a fishmonger near the Boulingrin market, pairing oysters and shellfish with Champagne by the glass.

The list: Compact list of Champagne and white wine built to match the seafood, with grower bubbles by the glass

Tip: Behind a fishmonger near the Halles du Boulingrin; the oyster-and-Champagne pairing is the order to make.

27 Rue de Mars, 51100 Reims, France →

La Cave a Champagne ★ 4.2

15 by the glassEpernay centre

Traditional Epernay restaurant and Champagne bar on Rue Gambetta, pouring growers and houses by the glass into the evening.

The list: Champagne-focused list spanning growers and houses, poured by the glass alongside a traditional Champenois menu

Tip: A reliable Epernay table for Champagne by the glass with regional cooking; sit late after the avenue houses close.

16 Rue Gambetta, 51200 Epernay, France →

Le Bistrot du Forum ★ 4.0

10 by the glassPlace du Forum (Reims)

Neighbourhood bistro on the Place du Forum in Reims pouring Champagne by the glass with regional cooking into the evening.

The list: Bistro wine list with Champagne by the glass alongside French regional bottles, a relaxed evening room on the Forum

Tip: A low-key Forum bistro for a glass of Champagne with classic French plates away from the tourist crush.

6 Place du Forum, 51100 Reims, France →

Fortified Specialists in Champagne

Distillerie Jean Goyard ★ 4.3

3 by the glassAy-Champagne

The oldest working distillery in Champagne, at Ay, where Ratafia, Fine and Marc de Champagne are made and tasted in the boutique.

The list: Tastings of the three Champenois spirits, Ratafia Champenois, Fine de Champagne and Marc de Champagne, at the oldest working distillery in the region

Tip: Take the guided distillery tour at Ay and finish with the commented tasting of Ratafia, Fine and Marc, the Champagne digestifs.

52 Rue Rene Lalique, 51160 Ay-Champagne, France →

Nightlife in Champagne, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Champagne for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Champagne is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Champagne estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Champagne cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Champagne tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Champagne?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Champagne rewards trust.
