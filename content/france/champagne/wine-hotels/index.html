A guide to wine hotels in Champagne.

Wine Hotels in Champagne

Royal Champagne Hotel & Spa ★ 4.8

EUR 450-1200 per nightChampillon, Montagne de Reims

A 19th-century coaching inn reborn as a 5-star hilltop retreat above the Marne vineyards at Champillon, with an infinity pool, spa and a Michelin-starred table.

The list: Champagne pairing dinners; tasting flights of Cote des Blancs and Vallee de la Marne growers; guided house visits arranged from the concierge

9 rue de la Republique, 51160 Champillon, France → Book →

Domaine Les Crayeres ★ 4.8

EUR 400-1500 per nightParc de Champagne, Reims

A Pommery-built chateau set in a 7-hectare park in Reims, run as a 5-star Relais and Chateaux property. Home to the two-Michelin-star Le Parc, brasserie Le Jardin.

The list: Champagne-pairing tasting menus at Le Parc; an extensive grower and grande marque list; bar flights of vintage cuvees

64 boulevard Henry Vasnier, 51100 Reims, France → Book →

Hostellerie La Briqueterie & Spa ★ 4.6

EUR 320-900 per nightVinay, near Epernay

A Relais and Chateaux hotel ringed by vineyards and gardens at Vinay, ten minutes from Epernay. Heated indoor pool and spa alongside the Michelin-starred Alcove.

The list: Champagne pairing menus at Alcove; cellar list strong on Cote des Blancs; tastings and house visits arranged for guests

4 route de Sezanne, 51530 Vinay, France → Book →

Hotel Restaurant Les Avises ★ 4.7

EUR 220-450 per nightAvize, Cote des Blancs

Opened in 2011 by Corinne and Anselme Selosse of Domaine Jacques Selosse, this 10-room hotel sits among the grand cru Chardonnay of Avize. A single tasting menu.

The list: Jacques Selosse grower-champagne flights; single set menu built for Cote des Blancs Chardonnay; estate visits for resident guests

59 rue de Cramant, 51190 Avize, France → Book →

Hotel Jean Moet ★ 4.4

EUR 130-280 per nightEpernay centre

A 4-star hotel in a restored Epernay townhouse steps from the Avenue de Champagne. Wine bar and bistro on site, with the train station and the major house cellars.

The list: Champagne bar pouring grower and house cuvees by the glass; cellar-visit bookings arranged at reception

7 rue Jean Moet, 51200 Epernay, France → Book →

La Villa Eugene ★ 4.5

EUR 200-500 per nightAvenue de Champagne, Epernay

The only 5-star hotel on the Avenue de Champagne, in a 19th-century mansion once owned by the Mercier family. Fifteen rooms look over the avenue or the landscaped.

The list: Champagne aperitifs in the salon and garden; concierge-arranged tastings and cellar visits along the avenue

84 avenue de Champagne, 51200 Epernay, France → Book →

La Caserne Chanzy Hotel & Spa ★ 4.6

EUR 200-450 per nightCathedral quarter, Reims

A 5-star Autograph Collection hotel beside Reims Cathedral, opened 2019 in a converted Art Deco building. Spa, restaurant and a champagne-focused bar a short walk.

The list: Champagne bar list spanning grandes marques and growers; tasting events; cellar-visit concierge service

18 rue Tronsson Ducoudray, 51100 Reims, France → Book →

Le Clos Corbier ★ 4.4

EUR 130-260 per nightMareuil-sur-Ay, Vallee de la Marne

An 1850s Champagne family house turned guest house at Mareuil-sur-Ay, ringed by UNESCO-listed vineyards. The owners run cellar visits, tastings and a small table.

The list: Cellar visits and tastings of the two family champagne brands; sabering and blind-tasting workshops led by in-house guides

5 boulevard Nicolas Francois Billecart, 51160 Mareuil-sur-Ay, France → Book →

Parva Domus ★ 4.4

EUR 110-200 per nightAvenue de Champagne, Epernay

A five-room bed and breakfast run by the Rimaire family on the Avenue de Champagne in Epernay, within walking distance of Moet & Chandon and Mercier.

The list: Host-led champagne introductions; recommendations and bookings for the avenue house cellars

27 avenue de Champagne, 51200 Epernay, France → Book →

Grand Hotel des Templiers ★ 4.3

EUR 160-320 per nightReims centre

A 4-star town-house hotel in central Reims, ten minutes on foot from the cathedral, with an indoor heated pool and sauna. A quiet base for visiting the city house.

The list: Champagne service in the lounge; staff bookings for the nearby Saint-Nicaise house cellars

22 rue des Templiers, 51100 Reims, France → Book →

Hotel de la Cloche ★ 4.2

EUR 80-160 per nightEpernay centre

A central Epernay hotel five minutes from the train station and the Moet & Chandon cellars. On-site restaurant serving French regional cooking, with the Avenue de.

The list: Champagne by the glass at the bar; restaurant list of local houses and growers; cellar-visit suggestions at reception

3-5 Place Mendes France, 51200 Epernay, France → Book →

La Villa Saint Pierre ★ 4.1

EUR 60-120 per nightEpernay centre

A simple 14-room hotel in central Epernay, within walking distance of the Avenue de Champagne and the Moet & Chandon cellars. A value base for travellers touring.

The list: Concierge bookings for Avenue de Champagne cellar visits; champagne recommendations from the hosts

1 rue Jeanne d'Arc, 51200 Epernay, France → Book →

Wine Hotels in Champagne, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Champagne for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Champagne is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Champagne estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Champagne cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Champagne tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Champagne?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Champagne rewards trust.
