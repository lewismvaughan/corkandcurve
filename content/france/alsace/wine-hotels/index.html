A guide to wine hotels in Alsace.

Wine Hotels in Alsace

Hotel Le Chambard ★ 4.8

EUR 320-700 per nightKaysersberg

Five-star Relais et Chateaux hotel in 18th-century Kaysersberg, home of Olivier Nasti's two-Michelin-star Table d'Olivier Nasti, an infinity pool spa and a winstub on the Route des Vins.

The list: Two-Michelin-star Table d'Olivier Nasti pairings; winstub with Alsace by-the-glass; concierge cellar visits along the Route des Vins

9-13 Rue du General de Gaulle, 68240 Kaysersberg, France → Book →

La Maison des Tetes ★ 4.7

EUR 280-650 per nightColmar old town

Five-star Relais et Chateaux hotel inside the 1609 Renaissance Maison des Tetes in Colmar, home to chef Eric Girardin's one-Michelin-star restaurant and a historic Alsatian brasserie.

The list: Restaurant Girardin Michelin-star tasting menu; Brasserie Historique with Alsace by-the-glass; concierge-arranged cellar visits

19 Rue des Tetes, 68000 Colmar, France → Book →

Hotel Quatorze ★ 4.5

EUR 200-380 per nightColmar old town

Boutique 14-key hotel in a former 19th-century pharmacy on Rue des Augustins, steps from Petite Venise and Musee Unterlinden. Curated Alsace wine fridge and concierge tasting bookings.

The list: In-room curated Alsace minibar; concierge bookings at Wolfberger, Hugel and Trimbach cellars

14 Rue des Augustins, 68000 Colmar, France → Book →

Hotel La Cour du Bailli Suites and Spa ★ 4.4

EUR 180-380 per nightBergheim

Suite hotel on Bergheim's Grand Rue in a 16th-century property with a spa, restaurant and winstub. Hand-walking distance to the Altenberg Grand Cru and Domaine Marcel Deiss cellar.

The list: Hotel restaurant and winstub pour grower Bergheim Alsace; concierge-arranged tastings at Domaine Marcel Deiss and Sylvie Spielmann

57 Grand Rue, 68750 Bergheim, France → Book →

Hotel a la Cour d'Alsace by HappyCulture ★ 4.5

EUR 180-420 per nightObernai

Four-star HappyCulture hotel and spa in medieval Obernai, set in 17th-century mansions with a winstub, gastronomic restaurant Le Jardin des Remparts and a deep Alsace cellar.

The list: Le Jardin des Remparts and winstub Cour d'Alsace pour grower Alsace lists; sommelier-led tastings inside the cellar

3 Rue de Gail, 67210 Obernai, France → Book →

Domaine du Moulin Hotel and Spa ★ 4.4

EUR 150-350 per nightEnsisheim

Four-star hotel-spa in Ensisheim 20 minutes from Colmar and Mulhouse, with the Villa du Meunier restaurant and a deep Alsace cellar, plus easy access to southern Alsace Grand Crus.

The list: La Villa du Meunier restaurant features Alsace and French growers; concierge bookings to Schlumberger and Wolfberger

44 Rue de la Premiere Armee, 68190 Ensisheim, France → Book →

Hostellerie La Cheneaudiere ★ 4.7

EUR 300-700 per nightVallee de la Bruche

Five-star Relais et Chateaux hotel-spa in the Vosges foothills 45 minutes from Strasbourg, home of Jean-Paul Acker's one-Michelin-star Le Feuillage restaurant and a 2,500 sqm spa.

The list: Le Feuillage Michelin pairings; Le Chene bistro with Alsace by-the-glass; concierge cellar visits in the Vallee de la Bruche and the Route des Vins

3 Rue Vieux Moulin, 67420 Colroy-la-Roche, France → Book →

Le Schaeferhof ★ 4.7

EUR 250-450 per nightMurbach

Maison d'hotes inside an 18th-century sheepfold of the Prince-Abbots of Murbach, romantic park and kitchen garden at the foot of the Grand Ballon and Schlumberger Grand Crus.

The list: Host-curated Alsace wine cellar and tastings; walks to Schlumberger Saering, Kessler and Spiegel Grand Crus

6 Rue de Guebwiller, 68530 Murbach, France → Book →

Hostellerie du Chateau ★ 4.5

EUR 130-240 per nightEguisheim

Charm hotel in the centre of Eguisheim 5 km from Colmar, a half-timbered town house steps from Wolfberger and Leon Beyer tasting cellars. Family-run with hand-painted Alsatian rooms.

The list: Hand-walking distance to Wolfberger, Leon Beyer and Emile Beyer; in-house wine fridge of grower Alsace selections

2 Rue du Chateau, 68420 Eguisheim, France → Book →

Hotel Le Marechal ★ 4.4

EUR 200-400 per nightPetite Venise Colmar

16th-century half-timbered mansion on Place des Six Montagnes Noires in Petite Venise, terrace bistro and a deep cellar of Alsace bottles, ten minutes walk from Musee Unterlinden.

The list: Hotel bistro pours an Alsace-led list; concierge bookings along the Route des Vins

4-6 Place des Six Montagnes Noires, 68000 Colmar, France → Book →

Hotel Saint-Martin ★ 4.2

EUR 110-230 per nightColmar old town

Three-star Logis hotel inside a 14th-century Colmar mansion on Grand Rue, central walkable base for Petite Venise and Place de la Cathedrale. Wine boutiques and tasting rooms nearby.

The list: Concierge tastings at La Sommeliere, Vinum and the Musee des Vins d'Alsace; walking distance to Le Cercle des Aromes

38 Grand Rue, 68000 Colmar, France → Book →

Le Rosenmeer ★ 4.3

EUR 120-250 per nightRosheim

Maetz family hotel-restaurant since 1850 facing Rosheim station on the Mont Sainte-Odile foothills, an Alsace base for visiting Mittelbergheim Zotzenberg and Andlau Kastelberg crus.

The list: Hotel restaurant Le Rosenmeer-Hubert Maetz pours an Alsace-led list; concierge bookings at Mittelbergheim and Andlau cellars

45 Avenue de la Gare, 67560 Rosheim, France → Book →

Hostellerie Saint-Florent ★ 4.2

EUR 90-160 per nightOberhaslach

Family-run hotel-restaurant in Oberhaslach near Molsheim, Vosges foothill setting with traditional Alsace cuisine and a regional wine cellar of grower Klevner and Sylvaner.

The list: Hotel restaurant pours grower Molsheim and Andlau wines; concierge bookings at Domaine Pfister and Loew

28 Rue du Nideck, 67280 Oberhaslach, France → Book →

Chateau d'Isenbourg and Spa ★ 4.7

EUR 280-650 per nightRouffach

Five-star Grandes Etapes hotel in a hilltop chateau above Rouffach, set in five hectares of vineyard with panoramic views to the Vosges and Black Forest. Restaurant and cellar visits.

The list: Chateau cellar of 700 references with deep Alsace; on-site vineyard tour and tasting; sommelier-led pairings at the gastronomic restaurant

9-11 Rue de Pfaffenheim, 68250 Rouffach, France → Book →

Hostellerie des Chateaux and Spa ★ 4.5

EUR 220-480 per nightOttrott

Four-star spa hotel near Obernai on the Route des Vins with a 2,500 sqm spa, Le Gastronomique restaurant and Le Comptoir du Chateau bistrot in the Vosges foothills.

The list: Two restaurants with grower Alsace cellar; concierge cellar visits at Pinot Noir specialists Ottrott Rouge d'Ottrott vineyards

11 Rue des Chateaux, 67530 Ottrott, France → Book →

Le Clos des Sources Hotel and Spa ★ 4.3

EUR 160-320 per nightThannenkirch

Three-star spa hotel in the wooded village of Thannenkirch between Strasbourg and Colmar, 10 km from the Route des Vins, with an indoor pool, sauna and hammam.

The list: Cellar weighted to grower Alsace; concierge bookings at Ribeauville, Bergheim and Rorschwihr cellars

2 Rue de Rodern, 68590 Thannenkirch, France → Book →

Wine Hotels in Alsace, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Alsace for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Alsace is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Alsace estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Alsace cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Alsace tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Alsace?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Alsace rewards trust.
