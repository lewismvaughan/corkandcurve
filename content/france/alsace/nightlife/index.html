Fortified Specialists in Alsace

Distillerie Massenez (Eau-de-vie de fruits) ★ 4.3

Vallee de la Bruche

A historic Alsatian fruit-spirit distillery with tasting flights of kirsch, mirabelle and framboise eaux-de-vie, the Alsatian after-dinner pour.

The list: A historic Alsatian eau-de-vie distillery with tasting flights of kirsch, mirabelle, framboise, poire Williams and rarer fruit spirits, the Alsatian after-dinner pour

Tip: Eau-de-vie is the digestif of Alsace; ask for a flight of kirsch, framboise and mirabelle to see the family's flagship fruit distillations.

13 Route du Vin, 67220 Villé, France →

Distillerie G.E. Massenez and Miclo, Lapoutroie ★ 4.2

Lapoutroie

A Lapoutroie eau-de-vie distillery, the historic heart of Alsatian fruit-spirit production, with kirsch, mirabelle, framboise and modern whisky alongside.

The list: A long-running Lapoutroie eau-de-vie distillery, with kirsch from local cherry orchards alongside whisky and gin in the modern range

Tip: Lapoutroie is the historic centre of Alsatian eau-de-vie; pair a Miclo visit with the village's farmhouse-cheese makers for a non-wine half-day.

311 La Gayire, 68650 Lapoutroie, France →

Lucien Albrecht (Marc d'Alsace) ★ 4.1

Orschwihr

An Orschwihr house with Cremant alongside Marc d'Alsace de Gewurztraminer, the region's distinctive pomace eau-de-vie distilled from Gewurztraminer skins.

The list: An Orschwihr house known for Cremant and pomace-distilled Marc d'Alsace de Gewurztraminer, a regional grape-pomace eau-de-vie

Tip: Marc d'Alsace de Gewurztraminer is the region's signature pomace spirit; ask to taste it beside the dry Cremant for the two faces of the same fruit.

9 Grand Rue, 68500 Orschwihr, France →

Late Tastings in Alsace

Cave Historique des Hospices de Strasbourg evening tastings ★ 4.6

Strasbourg

The 1395 hospital cellar of Strasbourg, home of the 1472 cask and a 30-estate Hospices bottling collection, the city's signature historic tasting.

The list: The 1395 cellar of the Hospices de Strasbourg, hosting tastings of more than 30 Alsatian estates' wines under the Hospices label, with the 1472 cask on display

Tip: Closed Sundays; the 1472 cask is the centrepiece of the cellar, and the bottling collection covers major Alsace estates.

1 Place de l'Hopital, 67091 Strasbourg, France →

The CIVA's interprofessional house in Colmar, where tasting flights cover the full Alsace appellation hierarchy in one room.

The list: The interprofessional CIVA building in Colmar hosts year-round tastings of Alsatian appellations across the wine route, useful for a one-stop introductory flight

Tip: The CIVA's tasting bar in Colmar pours appellation flights from across Alsace, a useful one-stop sampler before driving the wine route.

12 Avenue de la Foire aux Vins, 68000 Colmar, France →

Wolfberger Cremant cellar, Eguisheim ★ 4.1

Eguisheim

The Wolfberger cooperative's Eguisheim cellar, pouring its full Cremant d'Alsace range from entry-level Brut through to vintage cuvees.

The list: Wolfberger is a major Cremant d'Alsace producer, with a dedicated tasting room at Eguisheim pouring its full traditional-method sparkling range

Tip: Wolfberger's Cremant tasting flight covers a single house's full range from blanc de blancs to blanc de noirs in one sitting.

Bestheim Cremant cellar, Bennwihr ★ 4.0

Bennwihr

Bestheim's Bennwihr cellar, a major Cremant d'Alsace house, with tasting flights of Prestige and vintage traditional-method sparkling.

The list: Bestheim is a major Cremant d'Alsace house and its Bennwihr cellar runs scheduled tastings of the Prestige and vintage Cremant lineup

Tip: Bestheim's Prestige Cremant is a strong value within the AOC; taste the vintage cuvee alongside it to see what extra lees ageing does to the wine.

Lucien Albrecht Cremant cellar, Orschwihr ★ 4.1

Orschwihr

The Orschwihr cellar of Lucien Albrecht, a historic Cremant d'Alsace house tied to the 1976 founding of the AOC, with tasting flights from Brut to Brut Rose and Pinot Gris cuvees.

The list: Lucien Albrecht in Orschwihr is a historic Cremant d'Alsace house tied to the AOC's 1976 founding, with cellar tastings of Brut, Brut Rose and Pinot Gris cuvees

Tip: Lucien Albrecht was active in the 1976 founding of the Cremant d'Alsace AOC; the cellar visit puts the regional sparkling story in context.

9 Grand Rue, 68500 Orschwihr, France →

Nightlife in Alsace, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Alsace for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Alsace is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Alsace estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Alsace cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Alsace tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Alsace?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Alsace rewards trust.
