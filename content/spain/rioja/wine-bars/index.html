Best wine bars in Rioja

Wine Fandango ★ 4.5

20 by the glassEl Espolon, Logrono

Wine bar and kitchen in an Espolon palace from two Rioja-trade brothers; deep by-the-glass board, flights and a vermouth-led aperitivo.

The list: List of more than 100 wines, many from Rioja in both modern and classic styles; around 20 rotating by-the-glass pours, plus wine flights and porron service

Pours: Rioja Tempranillo crianza, Rioja Reserva, Rioja Blanco Viura

Calle Vara de Rey 5, 26003 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain →

Tastavin Vinos y Pinchos ★ 4.6

60 by the glassCalle San Juan, Logrono

Small Calle San Juan wine bar using Coravin to pour rare bottles by the glass; a Rioja-deep list matched to refined pintxos.

The list: More than 100 references with over 60 Spanish wines kept fresh on Coravin, focused on Rioja with by-the-glass tastings alongside Michelin-level tapas

Pours: Rioja Reserva, single-vineyard Rioja, Rioja Blanco

Calle de San Juan 25, 26001 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain →

Bar Soriano ★ 4.4

8 by the glassCalle Laurel, Logrono

Calle Laurel institution since 1972 known for one perfect grilled-mushroom pintxo, washed down with glasses of Rioja at the counter.

The list: Classic Calle Laurel pintxo bar pouring young and crianza Rioja by the glass to match its single mushroom pintxo, served since 1972

Pours: Rioja joven, Rioja crianza

Travesia de Laurel 2, 26001 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain →

Bar Blanco y Negro ★ 4.4

8 by the glassCalle Laurel, Logrono

Late-19th-century Calle Laurel bar famed for the matrimonio pintxo, pouring rounds of Rioja in the heart of the elephant trail.

The list: Centenary Calle Laurel bar with Rioja by the glass served alongside its famous matrimonio pintxo of anchovy and boqueron

Pours: Rioja joven, Rioja crianza, Rioja Blanco

Trav. Laurel, 1 BAJO, 26001, Logrono, La Rioja, Spain →

La Taberna del Tio Blas ★ 4.3

10 by the glassCalle Laurel, Logrono

Modern Calle Laurel taberna pouring Rioja by the glass against inventive pintxos like goat cheese with berries and cod fritters.

The list: Creative Calle Laurel pintxo bar pairing Rioja by the glass with modern bites; allergy-aware menu and a regularly changing pour list

Pours: Rioja joven, Rioja crianza, Rioja Blanco Viura

Calle Laurel 1, 26001 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain →

Bar Angel ★ 4.3

12 by the glassCalle Laurel, Logrono

Calle Laurel bar run by the founding family since 1960, pouring Rioja from 20-plus wineries alongside its signature wild-mushroom pintxo.

The list: Family Calle Laurel bar founded 1960 pouring wines from more than 20 wineries by the glass to match its setas (wild mushroom) pintxo

Pours: Rioja joven, Rioja crianza

Calle Laurel 12, 26001 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain →

Vinoteca San Agustin ★ 4.3

15 by the glassCasco Antiguo, Logrono

Old-town vinoteca and tasting bar with 200-plus references, pouring Rioja and vermouth by the glass alongside cheese and Spanish conservas.

The list: More than 200 references of wine, cava, sparkling and vermouth with by-the-glass tastings and guided pairings in the old town

Pours: Rioja Reserva, Rioja Blanco, Cava

PLAZA SAN AGUSTIN n4, 26001 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain →

The One Wine ★ 4.4

24 by the glassLaguardia, Rioja Alavesa

Bright Plaza Mayor wine bar in walled Laguardia pouring 250-plus rotating terroir wines by the glass in its Uncorking Room.

The list: Plaza Mayor wine shop and tasting room with more than 250 terroir-driven wines, an Uncorking Room for by-the-glass tastings from around EUR 3

Pours: Rioja Alavesa Tempranillo, single-vineyard Rioja, Rioja Blanco

Plaza Mayor, 01300 Laguardia, Alava, Spain →

Los Canos ★ 4.4

12 by the glassLa Herradura, Haro

Bar in a 16th-century palace on Haro's lively La Herradura, pouring a curated Rioja-by-the-glass list with pinchos at the counter or terrace.

The list: Short but sharp Rioja by-the-glass list in a 16th-century palace on Haro's La Herradura, poured with pinchos and raciones at the bar or terrace

Pours: Rioja crianza, Rioja Reserva, Rioja Blanco

Plaza San Martin 5, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain →

Wine Bars in Rioja, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Rioja for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Rioja is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Rioja estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Rioja cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Rioja tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Rioja?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Rioja rewards trust.
