Garnacha in Rioja

41 editor-picked estates growing Garnacha in Rioja.

Where to taste Garnacha in Rioja: the estates that grow it, how the region expresses the grape, and how to book a visit.

Garnacha estates in Rioja

Marques de Murrieta ★ 4.8

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Mazuelo, Graciano, Garnacha, ViuraFounded 1852Vicente Cebrian-Sagarriga familyRioja Alta

A founding house of modern Rioja established in 1852, based at the Ygay estate near Logrono and renowned for its long-aged Castillo Ygay Gran Reserva and the Capellania white.

Tasting: Tours and tastings of the Ygay estate and castle by reservation, including the flagship Castillo Ygay Gran Reserva.

Finca Ygay, Carretera Logrono-Zaragoza km 5, 26006 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

CVNE ★ 4.8

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, Mazuelo, ViuraFounded 1879Real de Asua familyBarrio de la Estacion

Compania Vinicola del Norte de Espana, founded in 1879 in Haro and still family-owned, makes the Cune, Imperial Gran Reserva and Monopole white ranges from the Barrio de la Estacion.

Tasting: Tours of the historic Haro cellars, including a building attributed to the Eiffel workshop, with tastings of Cune, Imperial and Monopole; by reservation.

Barrio de la Estacion, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

R. Lopez de Heredia Vina Tondonia ★ 4.9

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, Mazuelo, Viura, Malvasia RiojanaFounded 1877Lopez de Heredia familyBarrio de la Estacion

The most traditional house in Rioja, founded in 1877 in Haro and still run by the founding family, famous for decade-long barrel ageing of Vina Tondonia reds, whites and rosados.

Tasting: Tours of the historic cellars and the Zaha Hadid-designed pavilion shop with tastings of Vina Tondonia and Vina Bosconia; by reservation.

Avenida de Vizcaya 3, Barrio de la Estacion, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Muga ★ 4.7

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, Mazuelo, Viura, Malvasia RiojanaFounded 1932Muga familyBarrio de la Estacion

A family house founded in 1932 in the Barrio de la Estacion at Haro, one of the last bodegas with its own cooperage, making Prado Enea Gran Reserva and the modern Torre Muga and Aro.

Tasting: Guided cellar tours showing the in-house cooperage, with tastings of Prado Enea, Torre Muga and Aro; also a wine bar and balloon rides.

Barrio de la Estacion, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Roda ★ 4.7

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, GracianoFounded 1987Mariano and Carmen Daniela Bujanda Rotaeche familyBarrio de la Estacion

A modern Haro estate founded in 1987 in the Barrio de la Estacion, focused on old-vine Tempranillo selections in its Roda, Roda I and the prestige Cirsion.

Tasting: Tours of the gravity-flow cellar and barrel halls with tastings of Roda, Roda I and Cirsion, plus a wine bar; by reservation.

Avenida de Vizcaya 5, Barrio de la Estacion, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Gomez Cruzado ★ 4.5

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Viura, Tempranillo BlancoFounded 1886Gonzalez Pacheco and Garcia familiesBarrio de la Estacion

A small Barrio de la Estacion house at Haro dating to 1886, making classic Crianza, Reserva and Gran Reserva alongside a terroir range of single-village bottlings.

Tasting: Boutique-winery tours and tastings in the Barrio de la Estacion, including the terroir-selection wines Pancrudo and Cerro las Cuevas; by reservation.

Avenida de Vizcaya 6, Barrio de la Estacion, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Bilbainas ★ 4.5

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, Mazuelo, ViuraFounded 1901Codorniu Raventos groupBarrio de la Estacion

A historic Haro bodega founded in 1901, now part of the Codorniu Raventos group, known for its Vina Pomal label and the La Vicalanda Reserva and Gran Reserva.

Tasting: Tours of the historic cellars with tastings of the Vina Pomal range and the La Vicalanda Gran Reserva; by reservation.

Calle La Estacion 3, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Vinedos del Contino ★ 4.6

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Graciano, Garnacha, MazueloFounded 1973CVNE (Real de Asua family)Rioja Alavesa

Rioja's pioneering chateau-concept estate, founded in 1973 at Laserna on a bend of the Ebro and part of CVNE, making single-estate reds including a varietal Graciano.

Tasting: Tours of the single-estate property at Laserna with its 16th-century cellars, with tastings of the estate and single-vineyard reds; by reservation.

Finca San Gregorio, Laserna, 01321 Laguardia, Alava, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Luis Canas ★ 4.5

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Graciano, Garnacha, ViuraFounded 1970Luis Canas family (Juan Luis Canas)Rioja Alavesa

A family bodega at Villabuena de Alava in Rioja Alavesa, founded in 1970, drawing on old-vine parcels for its Reserva de Familia and top cuvee Hiru Racimos.

Tasting: Guided tours and tastings at Villabuena de Alava, including the Hiru Racimos and the old-vine Reserva de Familia; by reservation.

Carretera Samaniego 10, 01307 Villabuena de Alava, Alava, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Vivanco ★ 4.5

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Graciano, Garnacha, Mazuelo, Maturana TintaFounded 1915Vivanco familyBriones

A family winery and foundation at Briones tracing to 1915, pairing its Rioja range with one of the world's largest wine museums, the Vivanco Museum of Wine Culture.

Tasting: Winery tours, tastings and the large Vivanco Museum of Wine Culture and gardens of Bacchus at Briones; book through the estate.

Carretera Nacional 232, 26330 Briones, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Sierra Cantabria ★ 4.6

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, ViuraFounded 1957Eguren familySan Vicente de la Sonsierra

The flagship of the Eguren family at San Vicente de la Sonsierra, founded in 1957, making the Sierra Cantabria range plus the prestige Amancio and El Bosque.

Tasting: Tours and tastings at San Vicente de la Sonsierra of the Sierra Cantabria, Amancio and Coleccion Privada reds; by reservation.

Amorebieta 3, 26338 San Vicente de la Sonsierra, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodega Contador ★ 4.7

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Graciano, GarnachaFounded 1996Benjamin RomeoSan Vicente de la Sonsierra

The cult San Vicente de la Sonsierra project of winemaker Benjamin Romeo, founded in 1996, whose garage-born Contador earned 100-point recognition and reset Rioja's modern ceiling.

Tasting: Tours of the cave cellar beneath San Vicente with tastings of Contador, La Cueva del Contador and La Vina de Andres Romeo; by reservation.

Carretera Logrono, 26338 San Vicente de la Sonsierra, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Campo Viejo ★ 4.2

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, MazueloFounded 1959Vinarchy (Australian Wine Holdco / Accolade Wines, since 2025; formerly Pernod Ricard)Rioja Alta

One of the best-selling Rioja brands, founded in 1959 and owned since 2025 by Vinarchy (the Australian Wine Holdco / Accolade Wines group, having previously belonged to Pernod Ricard), with a large visitor-friendly winery near Logrono producing Crianza, Reserva and Gran Reserva.

Tasting: Tours of the large sustainable winery near Logrono with tastings across the Campo Viejo range; book through the estate.

Camino de Lapuebla 50, 26006 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Baron de Ley ★ 4.3

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, GracianoFounded 1985Baron de Ley S.A.Rioja Oriental

An estate built around a 16th-century former Benedictine monastery on the eastern edge of Rioja, founded in 1985 and known for its Finca Monasterio and Reserva reds.

Tasting: Tours of the 16th-century former monastery estate with tastings of the Finca Monasterio and Gran Reserva; by reservation.

Carretera Mendavia-Lodosa km 5.5, 31587 Mendavia, Navarra, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Ramon Bilbao ★ 4.4

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Graciano, Garnacha, ViuraFounded 1924Zamora CompanyHaro

A Haro house founded in 1924, now part of the Zamora Company, producing a wide Rioja range led by the Mirto and Lalomba single-vineyard wines.

Tasting: Guided tours of the Haro winery with barrel and bottle halls, tastings and a wine bar; by reservation.

Avenida Santo Domingo de la Calzada 34, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Baigorri ★ 4.5

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, MazueloFounded 2002Jesus Baigorri familyRioja Alavesa

An architectural Rioja Alavesa winery at Samaniego opened in 2002, built as a glass cube over seven underground levels using gravity-flow winemaking, with a panoramic restaurant.

Tasting: Tours of the seven-level gravity-flow glass cube cellar at Samaniego with tastings and a restaurant; by reservation.

Carretera Vitoria-Logrono km 53, 01307 Samaniego, Alava, Spain → Book →

Vina Real ★ 4.5

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, MazueloFounded 2004CVNE (Real de Asua family)Rioja Alavesa

CVNE's Rioja Alavesa cellar near Laguardia, a striking circular building opened in 2004, home to the Vina Real range and the single-estate Pagos de Vina Real.

Tasting: Tours of the circular barrel-shaped Philippe Mazieres cellar near Laguardia with tastings of Vina Real and Pagos de Vina Real; by reservation.

Carretera Logrono-Laguardia km 4.8, 01300 Laguardia, Alava, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Franco-Espanolas ★ 4.3

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Viura, MazueloFounded 1890Eguizabal familyLogrono

The only historic bodega in the urban centre of Logrono, founded in 1890 as a French-Spanish venture after phylloxera, known for the Bordon reds and the off-dry Diamante white.

Tasting: Tours of the historic city-centre winery in Logrono with tastings of the Bordon and Diamante ranges; book through the wine-tourism site.

Calle Cabo Noval 2, 26009 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Montecillo ★ 4.2

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Graciano, GarnachaFounded 1874Osborne groupRioja Alta

One of the oldest Rioja houses, founded in 1874 and now owned by the Osborne group, based at Fuenmayor and known for traditional Reserva and Gran Reserva reds.

Tasting: Tours of the Fuenmayor winery with tastings of the Montecillo Reserva and Gran Reserva; by reservation.

Carretera Navarrete-Fuenmayor km 2, 26360 Fuenmayor, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Ontanon ★ 4.3

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Graciano, Maturana Tinta, Tempranillo BlancoFounded 1985Perez Marin familyLogrono

A family winery linking a Logrono cellar with old-vine vineyards at Quel in Rioja Oriental, known for art-themed tours and a focus on Garnacha and heritage grapes such as Maturana.

Tasting: Tours combining wine and art at the Logrono winery, with vineyards in Quel in Rioja Oriental; tastings of the Ontanon and Vetiver wines by reservation.

Avenida de Aragon 3, 26006 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Valdemar ★ 4.3

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Maturana Blanca, Tempranillo BlancoFounded 1889Martinez Bujanda familyRioja Alavesa

A Martinez Bujanda family bodega at Oyon dating to 1889, noted for reviving rare Rioja varieties such as Maturana Blanca and for its Inspiracion Valdemar range.

Tasting: Tours and tastings at Oyon, including pioneering work with recovered varieties such as Maturana Blanca; by reservation.

Camino Viejo de Logrono 24, 01320 Oyon, Alava, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Lecea ★ 4.2

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, ViuraFounded 1962Lecea familyRioja Alta

A grower bodega in Rioja Alta at San Asensio keeping the tradition of underground rock-cut calado cellars and foot-treading, offering hands-on harvest experiences alongside its reds.

Tasting: Visits to traditional rock-cut calado cellar-caves at San Asensio with tastings and the annual grape foot-treading experience; by reservation.

Calle Las Cuevas 246, Barrio de las Bodegas, 26340 San Asensio, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Olarra ★ 4.2

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, Mazuelo, GracianoFounded 1973Grupo OlarraRioja Alta

A Logrono winery founded in 1973, famous for its Y-shaped modernist building called the cathedral of Rioja, producing the Cerro Anon and Anares Reserva and Gran Reserva.

Tasting: Tours of the distinctive Y-shaped winery near Logrono, nicknamed the cathedral of Rioja, with tastings of the Anares and Cerro Anon ranges; by reservation.

Poligono de Cantabria, 26006 Logrono, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Tritium ★ 4.1

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Garnacha, GracianoFounded 2004Tritium familyRioja Alta

A small Rioja Alta bodega at Badaran in the Najerilla valley, producing estate Tempranillo-led reds and offering visits and tastings in a quieter corner of the region.

Tasting: Tours and tastings at Badaran in the Najerilla valley of Rioja Alta, including barrel-aged Tempranillo; by reservation.

Carretera de Cordovin, 26310 Badaran, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

Dinastia Vivanco Vineyards ★ 4.3

DOCa RiojaTempranillo, Graciano, Garnacha, Maturana Tinta, MazueloFounded 1915Vivanco familyBriones

The Vivanco family's vineyard and brand arm at Briones, planting a living collection of Rioja and international varieties alongside its core Tempranillo and Graciano reds.

Tasting: Vineyard walks and varietal-focused tastings at the Vivanco estate in Briones, showcasing the family's collection of recovered Rioja grape varieties; by reservation.

Carretera Nacional 232, 26330 Briones, La Rioja, Spain → Book →

CVNE ★ 4.6

Tempranillo, Viura, Garnacha, GracianoAppointment onlyWalk-ins welcomeBarrio de la Estacion, Haro

Founded 1879 in Haro, CVNE pours across an open-air wine bar plus guided tours of the Eiffel-designed barrel hall and the Imperial cellar.

Barrio de la Estacion s/n, 26200 Haro, La Rioja, Spain →

Bohedal Bodega Boutique ★ 4.4

Tempranillo, Viura, GarnachaAppointment onlyCuzcurrita de Rio Tiron, Rioja Alta

Family wine-tourism pioneer in Cuzcurrita pouring barrel samples that contrast oak origins, ending with a blending tasting and crianza.

Cuzcurrita de Rio Tiron, 26214 La Rioja, Spain →

Bodega Tentenublo Wines ★ 4.6

Rioja DOCaTempranillo, Viura, Garnacha, MalvasiaFounded 2010Roberto OlivanNaturalRioja Alavesa

Roberto Olivan's low-intervention, old-vine project near Vinaspre, making vivid field-blend Rioja with spontaneous ferments and minimal sulfites.

Tip: Look for the village-and-vineyard bottlings rather than the entry wine; the Escondido and single-site cuvees show what old-vine Vinaspre can do.

Camino del Soto 22, 01307 Vinaspre, Alava, Spain →

Bodegas Bhilar ★ 4.6

Rioja DOCaTempranillo, Graciano, Viura, GarnachaFounded 1999David Sampedro Gil and Melanie HickmanDemeter CertifiedECOCERTRioja Alavesa

A Demeter-certified biodynamic estate at Elvillar in Rioja Alavesa, ploughed by horse, making site-driven Phincas wines from organic old vines.

Tip: The estate was certified biodynamic by Demeter in 2021; the Phincas range is the one to taste for the single-vineyard story.

Carretera Elvillar 22, 01300 Elvillar, Alava, Spain →

Olivier Riviere Vinos ★ 4.5

Rioja DOCaTempranillo, Garnacha, Viura, GracianoFounded 2006Olivier RiviereBiodynamic PracticingRioja Oriental and Rioja Alta

A French-born grower making terroir-led, organically farmed Rioja across Rioja Oriental and Alta, with a Burgundy-inspired village-and-site hierarchy.

Tip: His outlook is terroir-first rather than oak-tier; the village and lieu-dit cuvees reward seeking out over the entry wines.

Calle Breton de los Herreros 16, 26500 Calahorra, La Rioja, Spain →

Bodegas Honorio Rubio ★ 4.4

Rioja DOCaViura, Tempranillo, GarnachaFounded 1985Rubio familyNaturalNajerilla valley, Rioja Alta

A Cordovin family winery reviving Rioja clarete and skin-contact whites with spontaneous fermentation and minimal added sulfites.

Tip: Seek out the macerado white and the clarete; they are a window into Rioja styles that the big houses largely abandoned.

Calle Mayor 12, 26323 Cordovin, La Rioja, Spain →

Pedro Balda ★ 4.4

Rioja DOCaTempranillo, GarnachaFounded 2008Pedro BaldaNaturalSan Vicente de la Sonsierra

A pioneering natural-wine grower in San Vicente de la Sonsierra making unfiltered, no-added-sulfite Rioja from old plots.

Tip: Majuelo de la Nava is the wine to find; production is tiny and these go to natural-wine specialists, not supermarkets.

26338 San Vicente de la Sonsierra, La Rioja, Spain →

Bodegas Lacus ★ 4.3

Rioja DOCaGarnacha, Tempranillo, ViuraFounded 2007Luis ArnedoECOCERTNaturalRioja Oriental

A small organic family cellar in Rioja Oriental making fresh, low-oak Garnacha and Tempranillo from Aldeanueva de Ebro.

Tip: Rioja Oriental Garnacha is the calling card here; the wines show the warmer, fruitier side of the region with restraint.

Calle Cervantes 18, 26559 Aldeanueva de Ebro, La Rioja, Spain →

CVNE Cune Crianza ★ 4.3

Rioja DOCaTempranillo, Garnacha, MazueloEUR 9-12 retail

The benchmark everyday Rioja: a year in oak gives gentle vanilla over bright Tempranillo cherry. Endlessly available and reliably under twelve euros, it is the bottle that taught the world what classic Crianza tastes like.

Tip: If you can find the Vina Real Crianza from the same house, it is a slightly plusher sibling at a similar price.

Bodegas Muga Reserva ★ 4.5

Rioja DOCaTempranillo, Garnacha, Mazuelo, GracianoEUR 18-24 retail

Made the old way with oak fermentation, egg-white fining and barrel ageing, Muga Reserva delivers genuine Haro-house pedigree for around twenty euros. Silky red fruit, cedar and a fine, savoury length well above its price.

Tip: The Reserva is the value pick; the same cellar's pricier Prado Enea and Torre Muga are for special occasions.

Marques de Murrieta Reserva ★ 4.4

Rioja DOCaTempranillo, Garnacha, Mazuelo, GracianoEUR 18-24 retail

From the estate descended from Rioja's first commercial bodega, a poised, traditional Reserva of red fruit, spice and fine tannin that punches well above its sub-twenty-five-euro price.

Tip: This is the gateway to the house; the flagship Castillo Ygay Gran Reserva is a serious step up in price and ageing.

Bodegas Lacus Inedito ★ 4.2

Rioja DOCaGarnachaEUR 12-16 retail

An organically farmed Rioja Oriental Garnacha from a tiny family cellar, fresh and unadorned in the new-wave style. Proof that low-intervention Rioja can be had well under twenty euros.

Tip: Lacus is a Rioja Oriental name worth seeking out for honest, terroir-driven wines at fair prices.
