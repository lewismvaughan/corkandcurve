Estates to visit in Priorat

Clos Mogador ★ 4.9

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Syrah, Cabernet Sauvignon, Garnacha Blanca, MacabeoFounded 1979Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDGratallops

Founded in 1979 by Rene Barbier, Clos Mogador is one of the five estates that revived DOQ Priorat with the celebrated 1989 harvest. The estate's Manyetes plot holds Vinya Classificada status in the DOQ Priorat classification.

Tasting: Visits by appointment; contact the estate directly.

Signature wines: clos-mogador, manyetes, clos-nelin

Cami Manyetes, s/n, 43737 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Alvaro Palacios ★ 5.0

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1989Gratallops

Alvaro Palacios established his Priorat estate in 1989 as one of the five founding producers of modern Priorat. His L'Ermita vineyard in Bellmunt del Priorat holds Gran Vinya Classificada status, the highest tier of the DOQ Priorat classification.

Tasting: Visits strictly by appointment.

Signature wines: lermita, finca-dofi, gratallops-vi-de-vila, camins-del-priorat, manyetes

Afores s/n, 43738 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Clos de l'Obac / Costers del Siurana ★ 4.8

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Merlot, Tempranillo, Cariñena, Garnacha Blanca, Macabeo, Xarel-lo, MuscatFounded 1989Winemaker Mariona JarqueGratallops

Founded in 1989 by Carles Pastrana as one of the five original Priorat pioneers. Winemaker Mariona Jarque leads production. Their fixed-coupage approach uses consistent vineyard parcels and blend percentages each vintage.

Tasting: Guided visits by arrangement; contact visits@obac.es.

Signature wines: clos-de-lobac, miserere, kyrie

Founded by Josep Lluis Perez in 1981 and now led by his daughter Sara Perez, Mas Martinet is one of the five founding estates of modern Priorat. The estate produces ecologically farmed wines from steep llicorella slate plots.

Cellers de Scala Dei ★ 4.7

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1878Escaladei

Established in 1878 at the site of the 12th-century Carthusian monastery of Escaladei, the origin of organised wine cultivation in Priorat. Specialises in Garnacha from high-altitude mountain vineyards near Montsant.

Tasting: Guided visits of winery and heritage site; book through the estate website.

Signature wines: scala-dei-cartoixa, scala-dei-prior, scala-dei-masdeu

Vineyards in Priorat, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Priorat for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Priorat is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Priorat estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Priorat cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Priorat tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Priorat?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Priorat rewards trust.
