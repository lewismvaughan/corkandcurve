Garnacha Blanca in Priorat

23 editor-picked estates growing Garnacha Blanca in Priorat.

Where to taste Garnacha Blanca in Priorat: the estates that grow it, how the region expresses the grape, and how to book a visit.

Garnacha Blanca estates in Priorat

Clos Mogador ★ 4.9

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Syrah, Cabernet Sauvignon, Garnacha Blanca, MacabeoFounded 1979Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDGratallops

Founded in 1979 by Rene Barbier, Clos Mogador is one of the five estates that revived DOQ Priorat with the celebrated 1989 harvest. The estate's Manyetes plot holds Vinya Classificada status in the DOQ Priorat classification.

Tasting: Visits by appointment; contact the estate directly.

Signature wines: clos-mogador, manyetes, clos-nelin

Cami Manyetes, s/n, 43737 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Alvaro Palacios ★ 5.0

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1989Gratallops

Alvaro Palacios established his Priorat estate in 1989 as one of the five founding producers of modern Priorat. His L'Ermita vineyard in Bellmunt del Priorat holds Gran Vinya Classificada status, the highest tier of the DOQ Priorat classification.

Tasting: Visits strictly by appointment.

Signature wines: lermita, finca-dofi, gratallops-vi-de-vila, camins-del-priorat, manyetes

Afores s/n, 43738 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Clos de l'Obac / Costers del Siurana ★ 4.8

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Merlot, Tempranillo, Cariñena, Garnacha Blanca, Macabeo, Xarel-lo, MuscatFounded 1989Winemaker Mariona JarqueGratallops

Founded in 1989 by Carles Pastrana as one of the five original Priorat pioneers. Winemaker Mariona Jarque leads production. Their fixed-coupage approach uses consistent vineyard parcels and blend percentages each vintage.

Tasting: Guided visits by arrangement; contact visits@obac.es.

Signature wines: clos-de-lobac, miserere, kyrie

Cami Manyetes, 43737 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Cellers de Scala Dei ★ 4.7

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1878Escaladei

Established in 1878 at the site of the 12th-century Carthusian monastery of Escaladei, the origin of organised wine cultivation in Priorat. Specialises in Garnacha from high-altitude mountain vineyards near Montsant.

Tasting: Guided visits of winery and heritage site; book through the estate website.

Signature wines: scala-dei-cartoixa, scala-dei-prior, scala-dei-masdeu

Rambla Cartoixa, S/N, 43379 Escaladei, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Terroir al Limit ★ 4.9

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Garnacha Blanca, Pedro XimenezFounded 2002Winemaker Dominik HuberBiodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalTorroja del Priorat

Founded in 2002 by Dominik Huber, Terroir al Limit champions minimal-intervention winemaking and biodynamic viticulture across steep single-vineyard plots in Torroja del Priorat and surrounding villages.

Tasting: Visits by appointment at their cellar in Torroja del Priorat.

Signature wines: les-manyes, les-tosses, pedra-de-guix, arbossar, dits-del-terra

Carrer Major, 43737 Torroja del Priorat, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Finca Mas d'en Gil ★ 4.7

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Garnacha Blanca, MacabeoFounded 1998Rovira Carbonell familyWinemaker Marta Rovira CarbonellCAAEBellmunt del Priorat

The Rovira Carbonell family acquired this 125-hectare estate in 1998 and introduced CAAE-certified organic farming under Marta Rovira Carbonell. Based in Bellmunt del Priorat within the Bellmunt Vi de Vila appellation.

Tasting: Mon-Fri 10:30, 12:00, 15:30; Sat 10:30, 12:00; advance booking required. Email visita@masdengil.com.

Signature wines: coma-vella, clos-fonta, bellmunt-mas-den-gil

Ctra. de Falset a Bellmunt, km 4, 43738 Bellmunt del Priorat, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Vinicola del Priorat ★ 4.3

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Garnacha Blanca, MacabeoFounded 1917Gratallops

Established in 1917, Vinicola del Priorat is the only cooperative managing the entire process from vine to commercialisation across Gratallops, El Lloar, La Vilella Alta and La Vilella Baixa. Produces the Onix range from over 200 hectares of old-vine parcels.

Tasting: Wine and olive oil shop on site; cooperative welcomes visitors.

Signature wines: onix-classic, onix-evolucio, onix-fusio

C/ Piro, s/n, 43737 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Marco Abella ★ 4.5

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Garnacha BlancaFounded 2005David Marco and Olivia BayesPorrera

The Marco family has cultivated vineyards in Porrera since the 15th century; David Marco and Olivia Bayes built the modern winery in 2005. Produces seven red and two white cuvees in DOQ Priorat.

Tasting: Enoturisme experiences available; contact enoturisme@marcoabella.com.

Signature wines: mas-mallola, loidana, olbia

Carretera Porrera a Cornudella del Montsant, Km. 0.7, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Buil and Gine ★ 4.4

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Cabernet Sauvignon, Syrah, Merlot, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1996Buil and Gine familiesGratallops

Founded in 1996 by the Buil and Gine families, with roots six generations deep in Priorat. The first bodega in the region purposely built for wine tourism, featuring a hotel and restaurant Amics on the estate.

Tasting: First winery in Priorat built for tourism; wine experiences, 4-star hotel and restaurant Amics on site.

Signature wines: gine-gine, joan-gine, pleret

Carretera Gratallops-Vilella Baixa Km 11.5, 43737 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Celler Cal Pla ★ 4.5

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Syrah, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1814Joan SangenisWinemaker Joan SangenisPorrera

The seventh generation of the Sangenis family at Porrera, with estate history dating to 1814. The modern Celler Cal Pla facility, built in 2017, vinifies Garnacha and Cariñena from the family's Mas d'en Compte vineyard.

Tasting: Visits by appointment at the Porrera cellar.

Signature wines: celler-cal-pla-planots, celler-cal-pla-nou-naix

Prat de la Riba, 1, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Mas Igneus ★ 4.4

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Garnacha BlancaFounded 1997ORGANIC CERTIFIEDGratallops

A pioneer of organic viticulture in Priorat since 1997, Mas Igneus operates a modern winery in Gratallops and produces a range from the entry-level FA 112 to the premium Costers de l'Ermita Carignan.

Tasting: Visits open 11:00 to 15:00; contact info@masigneus.com.

Signature wines: mas-igneus-fa-112, mas-igneus-ig

Ctra T-710 km 11.1, 43737 Gratallops, Tarragona, Spain → Book →

Costers del Priorat ★ 4.4

DOQ PrioratGarnacha, Cariñena, Syrah, Cabernet Sauvignon, Garnacha BlancaBellmunt del Priorat

Produces eight wines from vineyards across the DOQ Priorat, including parcels planted in 1934 in Bellmunt, Torroja and other villages. The Memories ranci draws on a solera from 1927.

Tasting: Visits by arrangement; contact via website.

Signature wines: costers-del-priorat-pissarres, costers-del-priorat-carinyena
