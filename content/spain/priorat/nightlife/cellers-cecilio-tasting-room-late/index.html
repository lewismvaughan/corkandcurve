Cellers Cecilio Tasting Room ★ 4.3

The cellar door and tasting room of Cellers Cecilio, the oldest family winery in Gratallops, is the village's de-facto wine bar in the summer season, pouring Garnacha and Carinena from the family's century-old licorella plots.

Wine bar in Priorat · Gratallops Verified May 29, 2026

The list: Gratallops Vi de Vila wines from the oldest family winery in the village, sold by the glass and bottle

Tip: Cellers Cecilio is open in the evenings during summer season. The village of Gratallops has no other dedicated wine bar; the cellar door serves this function.

Location

12 by the glassGratallops

A small tasting room and wine bar in Gratallops operating in the evenings as a listening-bar atmosphere for Priorat wine flights. The stone-vaulted interior and candlelit evenings create the most intimate wine-tasting setting in the village.

The list: Curated flights of Gratallops and DOQ Priorat wines, served in a intimate stone-cellar setting with ambient music in the evening

Bar el Portal de Porrera ★ 4.2

10 by the glassPorrera

The village bar in Porrera, the historic Carinena village of the DOQ, serves wines from local producers including Vall Llach and Cims de Porrera by the glass on weekend evenings.

The list: Porrera Vi de Vila wines from local producers, served by the glass with Catalan snacks

Tip: Porrera's village bar is the place to drink Carinena-dominant wines from the local vintage at cellar-door prices.

Carrer Major s/n, 43739 Porrera, Tarragona, Spain →
