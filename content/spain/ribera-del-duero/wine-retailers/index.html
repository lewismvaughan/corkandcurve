Wine Retailers in Ribera del Duero

VinosRibera.com Gourmet and Tasting Centre ★ 4.4

EUR 6-500+Fuentespina

Self-described largest representative centre of DO Ribera del Duero wines and gastronomic products. Over 100 wineries from the denomination represented; climate-controlled cellar; tasting area; gourmet food products.

Ctra. N-1 Km. 153.5, 09471 Fuentespina, Burgos, Spain →

Enoteca Agropal ★ 4.3

EUR 5-150Penafiel

Wine retail shop in a renovated 1953 grain silo on the Pesquera road in Penafiel. DO Ribera del Duero and other Castilla y Leon designations; adjacent gourmet supermarket with Iberian products, cheeses and regional specialities.

Carretera Pesquera 34, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Vinoteca y Artesania Zaguan ★ 4.2

EUR 5-200Penafiel

Wine shop near the Plaza del Coso in central Penafiel. Over 450 references from DO Ribera del Duero, Rueda, Cigales, Toro and Tierra de Castilla y Leon; wine accessories and traditional regional food products.

Plaza Espana s/n, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodega Historica Don Carlos Wine Shop ★ 4.3

EUR 8-80Aranda de Duero historic centre

Wine retail attached to the Bodega Historica Don Carlos underground cellar tour venue in the centre of Aranda de Duero. Wide variety of small-producer Ribera del Duero wines available to purchase; accessible retail after the underground cave tour.

C/ Isilla s/n, 09400 Aranda de Duero, Burgos, Spain →

Bodegas Felix Callejo Estate Shop ★ 4.4

EUR 12-80Sotillo de la Ribera

Cellar-door retail at Bodegas Felix Callejo in Sotillo de la Ribera. Full range of Callejo estate wines including Flores de Callejo, Callejo Crianza, Reserva and the single-parcel Majuelos de Callejo; also available for online order.

Avenida del Cid Km. 16, 09441 Sotillo de la Ribera, Burgos, Spain →

Bodegas Emilio Moro Estate Shop ★ 4.5

EUR 12-100Pesquera de Duero

Cellar-door retail at Bodegas Emilio Moro on the Golden Mile. Full estate range from La Revelia joven to La Felisa single-vineyard; available to purchase after winery visits or directly at the estate shop.

Ctra. Penafiel-Vitoria s/n, 47315 Pesquera de Duero, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodegas Protos Gourmet Shop ★ 4.3

EUR 8-120Penafiel

Estate shop in the Agora of the Richard Rogers-designed Bodegas Protos complex in Penafiel. Full Protos range including limited releases; wine accessories, local food products and the Protos cookbook.

C/ Bodegas Protos 24-28, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain →

Bodegas Portia Gourmet Shop ★ 4.2

EUR 9-60Gumiel de Izan

Gourmet shop within the Norman Foster Bodegas Portia complex in Gumiel de Izan. Full Portia range including Triennia, Summa, Crianza and Roble; wine accessories and Ribera del Duero artisan products.

Antigua Carretera Nacional 1 Km. 170, 09370 Gumiel de Izan, Burgos, Spain →

Consejo Regulador Ribera del Duero Tienda ★ 4.2

EUR 10-80Roa de Duero

The regulatory council of DO Ribera del Duero in Roa de Duero, located in the 16th-century Hospital de San Juan Bautista building. The council office maintains an information and promotional retail point for the denomination; direct source for DO registry information.

C/ Hospital 6, 09300 Roa de Duero, Burgos, Spain →

Bodegas Arzuaga Navarro Estate Shop ★ 4.4

EUR 10-150Golden Mile

Estate retail shop at Hotel and Spa Arzuaga on the Golden Mile. Full Arzuaga portfolio from young Roble to aged Gran Reserva and single-vineyard Finca La Planta; wine and food gifts from the estate.

Carretera N-122 Km 325, 47350 Quintanilla de Onesimo, Valladolid, Spain →

Wine Retailers in Ribera del Duero, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Ribera del Duero for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Ribera del Duero is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Ribera del Duero estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Ribera del Duero cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Ribera del Duero tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Ribera del Duero?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Ribera del Duero rewards trust.
