Wine Hotels in Ribera del Duero

Abadia Retuerta LeDomaine ★ 4.9

EUR 600-2000 per nightSardon de Duero

Set within a restored 12th-century Augustinian abbey surrounded by vineyards and woodland in Sardon de Duero. The 30 rooms blend original Romanesque architecture with 21st-century detail. Michelin-starred Refectorio restaurant in the original dining hall; Santuario Wellness spa with grape-therapy treatments. The estate produces pago-classified wines outside the DO Ribera del Duero under the Abadia Retuerta label.

The list: Cellar visits and pago wine tastings; library vintage selection in the cellar lounge; harvest participation September-October; pairing dinners with Refectorio team

Monasterio de Santa Maria, 122 Km. 332.5, 47340 Sardon de Duero, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Castilla Termal Monasterio de Valbuena ★ 4.7

EUR 200-600 per nightValbuena de Duero

Converted 12th-century Cistercian monastery adjacent to the Ribera del Duero Golden Mile, declared a National Monument. 79 rooms around original cloisters with stone walls and Gothic arches. Thermal spa of 2,000+ sq m; El Converso restaurant with tasting menus; the hotel's own Converso Tempranillo is an internationally awarded limited production wine. Monks Wine Cellar for tastings.

The list: Converso Tempranillo produced on-estate; DO Ribera del Duero tastings in the Monks Wine Cellar; guided winery visits to nearby estates; Converso Experience tasting package

San Bernardo s/n, 47359 Valbuena de Duero, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Hotel and Spa Arzuaga ★ 4.6

EUR 193-400 per nightGolden Mile

Wine-estate hotel at Bodegas Arzuaga Navarro on the Golden Mile, surrounded by vineyards with views of the Duero valley. 96 rooms with spa featuring vinotherapy treatments including wine wraps, grape exfoliant and marc massages. Michelin-starred Taller by Amaya Arzuaga on site; adjacent winery offers estate tours and tastings.

The list: Bodegas Arzuaga Navarro estate tours and vertical tastings; wine bar open to hotel guests; harvest participation; vineyard walks with the winemaking team

Carretera N-122 Km 325, 47350 Quintanilla de Onesimo, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

AZZ Penafiel Las Claras Hotel and Spa ★ 4.3

EUR 80-200 per nightPenafiel

17th-century Clarisse convent in the heart of Penafiel, 7 minutes walk from Bodegas Protos and a short walk from Penafiel Castle and the Provincial Wine Museum. 64 rooms; the Conde Lucanor restaurant in the former chapel serves creative Castilian cuisine; winery access package available for nearby estates.

The list: Wine tasting packages with nearby DO Ribera del Duero producers; Conde Lucanor restaurant wine list focuses on Ribera del Duero; cellar visit arrangements

Plaza Adolfo Munoz Alonso 1, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

LaVida Vino Spa Hotel ★ 4.3

EUR 90-200 per nightTorre de Penafiel

Boutique rural hotel in Torre de Penafiel, incorporating the restored 17th-century communal wine press of the village. 17 rooms; vinotherapy spa with jet baths in red wine, grape marc exfoliation and Winemaker's wrap treatments; Guyot restaurant serving regional wine-country cuisine. Located in the Ribera del Duero wine zone.

The list: Vinotherapy spa treatments; Ribera del Duero wine list at the Guyot restaurant; guided estate visits to nearby wineries by arrangement

Plaza Mayor 1, Torre de Penafiel, 47300 Penafiel, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Hotel Finca Torremilanos ★ 4.3

EUR 100-200 per nightAranda de Duero

Estate hotel at Finca Torremilanos in Aranda de Duero, one of the founding wineries of the DO Ribera del Duero. 37 rooms with vineyard views; Torremilanos restaurant on site serving traditional lechazo and creative dishes; biodynamic estate winery tours and tastings. Rates from EUR 100 per night.

The list: Biodynamic estate winery tours and Torremilanos wine tastings; winery releases and vertical dinners for guests; estate walk through the vineyards

Nacional 122 Km. 274, 09400 Aranda de Duero, Burgos, Spain → Book →

Hotel Rural Emina ★ 4.1

EUR 80-150 per nightValbuena de Duero

Rural hotel in Valbuena de Duero a short drive from the Bodega Emina winery (check-in at the bodega). 17 rooms with free WiFi; buffet breakfast included. Located on the Golden Mile corridor near the Cistercian Valbuena monastery; Bodega Emina wine tourism programme and estate shop within easy access.

The list: Bodega Emina estate wine tourism and tasting programme; guests check in via the winery; wine and art installations at the bodega

Calle de la Acena 8, 47359 Valbuena de Duero, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Comenge Casa Rural ★ 4.4

EUR 90-180 per nightCuriel de Duero

Small rural accommodation on the Comenge organic winery estate at the foot of Curiel de Duero castle. Six rooms; winery visit and tasting included in stay; panoramic restaurant with castle views. Picnic-in-the-vineyard and garden barbecue available to guests. A quiet and personal wine estate stay in the heart of the region.

The list: Full estate organic wine programme; winery tours and tasting for guests; picnic and garden experiences; blending session available by request

Camino del Castillo s/n, 47346 Curiel del Duero, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Legaris Rural Stay ★ 4.2

EUR 90-150 per nightCuriel de Duero

Guest rooms within the Bodegas Legaris estate in Curiel de Duero. Small number of rooms enables a personal estate experience with access to all winery tour formats including the Barrel to Senses programme. Set among vineyards and medieval castles of the Curiel de Duero valley.

The list: Full access to Legaris winery tour programme including Classic and Barrel to Senses experiences; vertical flight of Legaris range during stay

Ctra. Penafiel-Encinas de Esgueva Km. 2,5, Curiel de Duero, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Nabal Rural Accommodation ★ 4.2

EUR 70-130 per nightGumiel de Izan

Rustic rural accommodation at the Nabal family winery in Gumiel de Izan, with vineyard valley views. Rooms overlook the estate; morning tastings with the winemaking family included; covered porch wine bar open throughout the stay. Easy access from the A1 motorway.

The list: Morning estate wine tastings; wine bar access; Gumiel de Izan wine village walking access; personalised family-led introduction to the estate's old-vine Tempranillo

A1 Highway exit 168, Valle de Nabal, 09370 Gumiel de Izan, Burgos, Spain → Book →

Bodegas Felix Callejo Winery Accommodation ★ 4.2

EUR 80-150 per nightSotillo de la Ribera

Small rural accommodation at Bodegas Felix Callejo in Sotillo de la Ribera, allowing in-depth immersion in a family-run single-vineyard Tempranillo estate. Morning winery access and private family-led tastings; estate is producing old-vine parcels from the Sotillo terroir.

The list: Private family-led winery access; old-vine single-parcel tasting programme; harvest participation in October

Avenida del Cid Km. 16, 09441 Sotillo de la Ribera, Burgos, Spain → Book →

Finca Villacreces Rural Accommodation ★ 4.3

EUR 100-180 per nightGolden Mile

Rural accommodation on the 115-hectare Finca Villacreces estate in organic viticulture since 2017. Set between the Duero river and bicentennial pine forest adjacent to Vega Sicilia. E-bike estate tour, vineyard walk and post-ride tasting are available to guests; picnic-in-the-vineyard option.

The list: E-bike estate tour; organic vineyard walk; Pruno and Finca Villacreces tastings; picnic in the pines option

Carretera Soria (N-122) Km. 322, 47350 Quintanilla de Onesimo, Valladolid, Spain → Book →

Wine Hotels in Ribera del Duero, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Ribera del Duero for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Ribera del Duero is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Ribera del Duero estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Ribera del Duero cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Ribera del Duero tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Ribera del Duero?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Ribera del Duero rewards trust.
