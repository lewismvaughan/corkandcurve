Matallana (Telmo Rodríguez) ★ 4.6

Telmo Rodríguez's Ribera del Duero project producing Matallana from old-vine Tinto Fino parcels in the Valladolid sector, applying his low-intervention philosophy shared across his Spanish estate portfolio.

Vega Real ★ 4.3

DO Ribera del DueroTinto FinoValbuena de Duero

DO Ribera del DueroTinto FinoLa Horra
