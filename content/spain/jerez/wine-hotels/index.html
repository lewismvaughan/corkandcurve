Wine Hotels in Jerez & Sherry

Hotel Bodega Tio Pepe ★ 4.9

EUR 280 to EUR 600 per nightJerez centro

Opened in 2020 inside the Gonzalez Byass bodega complex, Hotel Bodega Tio Pepe is an immersive sherry-hotel concept. Rooms are set in restored winery buildings within the working cellar grounds; guests have direct access to the La Concha tasting room and evening bodega walks.

The list: Daily bodega walks before sunset; Tio Pepe aperitif included at check-in; VORS private tasting for hotel guests; in-house sherry sommelier

Calle Manuel Maria Gonzalez 12, 11403 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain →

Hotel Casa Palacio Maria Luisa ★ 4.6

EUR 120 to EUR 280 per nightJerez centro

An 18th-century palace in central Jerez converted into a boutique hotel. The Maria Luisa offers curated sherry programmes through its concierge; guests are guided to local tabancos and bodegas with personalised tasting routes across the Marco de Jerez.

The list: Concierge-arranged bodega visits; sherry aperitif on arrival; tabanco walking-tour packages; wine-pairing dinners on request

Calle Torneria 22, 11402 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain → Book →

Hotel Las Cortes de Cadiz ★ 4.5

EUR 110 to EUR 250 per nightCadiz ciudad

A restored 19th-century building in Cadiz's old city, the Las Cortes hotel serves as a wine-tourism base for visits across the Marco de Jerez triangle. The hotel bar stocks an extensive sherry list and the concierge runs structured Jerez day trips.

The list: Hotel bar with 40-label Jerez DO sherry list; guided day excursions to Jerez, Sanlucar and El Puerto; sherry and local gin aperitif programme

Calle San Francisco 9, 11004 Cadiz, Spain → Book →

Posada de Palacio ★ 4.4

EUR 75 to EUR 150 per nightSanlucar de Barrameda centro

A historic posada in the heart of Sanlucar de Barrameda, positioned within walking distance of the main Manzanilla bodegas. The Posada de Palacio provides direct access to Hidalgo, Barbadillo and La Guita cellar visits and the riverside seafood bars of the Bajo de Guia.

The list: Manzanilla tasting on arrival; bodega-visit booking service; Bajo de Guia seafood and sherry evening walking route

Calle Caballeros 11, 11540 Sanlucar de Barrameda, Spain → Book →

Hotel Duques de Medinaceli ★ 4.5

EUR 150 to EUR 350 per nightEl Puerto de Santa Maria

Set in the 17th-century Palacio Medinaceli in El Puerto de Santa Maria, this hotel is the base for exploring Osborne bodega and Aponiente restaurant from the Atlantic coast corner of the sherry triangle. The hotel bar carries an Osborne sherry list.

The list: In-house Osborne sherry list; concierge booking for Aponiente and Osborne bodega visits; day excursions to Jerez and Sanlucar

Plaza de los Jazmines 2, 11500 El Puerto de Santa Maria, Spain → Book →

Hotel Palacio Garvey ★ 4.5

EUR 130 to EUR 300 per nightJerez centro

Housed in the restored palace of the Garvey sherry family, one of Jerez's oldest sherry dynasties. The hotel retains original architectural features from the 18th-century bodega era and offers a personal connection to the history of the Marco de Jerez sherry trade.

The list: Historical sherry trade orientation on arrival; guided walk to adjacent tabancos and bodegas; bodega booking service for nearby Lustau and Tradicion

Calle Torneria 24, 11402 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain → Book →

Hacienda El Recreo ★ 4.3

EUR 100 to EUR 200 per nightJerez viticultural zone

A traditional Andalusian hacienda set in albariza vineyard land outside Jerez. Guests are surrounded by the white chalk soils of the Jerez DO; the property arranges private vineyard walks and tasting visits to nearby bodegas in the Jerez heartland.

The list: Vineyard walks on the surrounding albariza terroir; sherry-on-arrival from adjacent bodegas; private tasting visit bookings to Tradicion, Maestro Sierra and Valdespino

Carretera Jerez-Medina Sidonia km 7, 11406 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain → Book →

Hotel Sherry Park ★ 4.2

EUR 80 to EUR 180 per nightJerez norte

A large 4-star hotel in the Jerez wine district, positioned between the major bodegas and the Royal Andalusian School of Equestrian Art. The Hotel Sherry Park hosts wine-focused package stays incorporating bodega visits and flamenco shows.

The list: Hotel bar with 25-label Jerez DO list; sherry-tour package bookings; flamenco and bodega combination evenings for groups

Avenida Alvaro Domecq 11bis, 11405 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain → Book →

Hotel Argantonio ★ 4.3

EUR 70 to EUR 160 per nightCadiz ciudad

A boutique hotel in the Andalusian-Moorish heart of Cadiz old city, within easy reach of the Atlantic ferry to El Puerto de Santa Maria. The Argantonio concierge offers structured sherry-tourism routes across the Marco de Jerez as a two-day-from-Cadiz option.

The list: Sherry welcome drink; tabanco and bodega route planning; half-day Jerez sherry triangle excursion bookings

Calle Argantonio 3, 11004 Cadiz, Spain → Book →

Hotel Jerez and Spa ★ 4.6

EUR 200 to EUR 500 per nightJerez norte

The five-star Hotel Jerez is the principal luxury accommodation in the Jerez wine district, with a sherry-focused bar and a regular calendar of bodega-visit packages. The hotel is the meeting point for premium wine-tourism groups visiting the major Jerez houses.

The list: In-house sherry bar with 50+ Jerez DO labels; bodega-excursion concierge service; private tutored tasting dinners in the hotel's event suite

Avenida Alvaro Domecq 35, 11405 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain → Book →

Hotel Dona Blanca ★ 4.3

EUR 90 to EUR 200 per nightEl Puerto de Santa Maria

A 4-star hotel in El Puerto de Santa Maria, serving as the Atlantic-coast base for sherry tourism and visits to the Osborne bodega and the Aponiente restaurant. The hotel bar is stocked with Osborne sherry expressions and local Cadiz wines.

The list: Osborne sherry welcome; Aponiente reservation assistance; Jerez sherry-triangle day-tour bookings

Calle Mora 1, 11500 El Puerto de Santa Maria, Spain → Book →

Solar de los Timoteos ★ 4.4

EUR 110 to EUR 250 per nightJerez centro

A restored 18th-century family palace in the historic centre of Jerez, steps from the major tabancos and bodega entrances. The Solar de los Timoteos offers personalised sherry-tourism services including private tabanco visits at opening hours and concierge-arranged bodega tours.

The list: Sherry aperitif on arrival; private tabanco walking route; concierge-arranged bodega and VORS tasting visits

Calle Moral 1, 11403 Jerez de la Frontera, Spain → Book →

Wine Hotels in Jerez & Sherry, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Jerez & Sherry for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Jerez & Sherry is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Jerez & Sherry estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Jerez & Sherry cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Jerez & Sherry tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Jerez & Sherry?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Jerez & Sherry rewards trust.
