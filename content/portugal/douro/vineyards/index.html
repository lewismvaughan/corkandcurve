Estates to visit in Douro Valley

Taylor's Port ★ 4.8

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Barroca, Tinto CaoFounded 1692The Fladgate Partnership (Yeatman and Bearsley families)Vila Nova de Gaia

Founded in 1692, Taylor's is one of the oldest Port houses and a Fladgate Partnership flagship, famed for long-aged tawnies and structured Vintage Port aged at its Gaia cellars.

Tasting: Audio-guided cellar tour in 13 languages plus tastings; restaurant Barao Fladgate on site.

Signature wines: taylors-vintage-port, taylors-20-year-old-tawny

Rua do Choupelo 250, 4400-088 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Fonseca Porto ★ 4.7

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Barroca, Tinta Roriz, Tinto CaoFounded 1815The Fladgate PartnershipVila Nova de Gaia

A Fladgate Partnership house founded in 1815, Fonseca is known for richly fruited Vintage Port and the popular Bin 27 reserve, with cellars in Gaia and the Quinta do Panascal estate.

Tasting: Cellar visits and tastings in Gaia; Quinta do Panascal visitor estate in the Douro.

Signature wines: fonseca-vintage-port, fonseca-bin-27

Rua do Choupelo 84, 4400-187 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Graham's Port ★ 4.8

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Barroca, Tinta Roriz, Tinto CaoFounded 1820Symington Family EstatesVila Nova de Gaia

A Symington house founded in 1820, Graham's ages its Port at the hilltop 1890 Lodge in Gaia and is known for richly sweet Vintage Port and the Six Grapes reserve.

Tasting: Guided tours of the 1890 Lodge with tiered tastings; Vinum restaurant on site.

Signature wines: grahams-vintage-port, grahams-six-grapes

Rua Rei Ramiro 514, 4400-281 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Dow's Port ★ 4.7

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Barroca, SousaoFounded 1798Symington Family EstatesPinhao

A Symington house known for drier, structured Vintage Port, Dow's welcomes visitors at Quinta do Bomfim on the river at Pinhao, the heart of its Cima Corgo holdings.

Tasting: Dow's visitor centre at Quinta do Bomfim in Pinhao with tiered Port and Douro tastings.

Signature wines: dows-vintage-port

Quinta do Bomfim, 5085-060 Pinhao, Alijo, Portugal → Book →

Warre's Port ★ 4.6

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1670Symington Family EstatesVila Nova de Gaia

The oldest British Port house, founded in 1670 and now Symington-owned, Warre's makes firmly structured Vintage Port and the Otima lighter-styled aged tawnies.

Tasting: Historic Symington Port house; tastings via the family's Gaia visitor centres.

Signature wines: warres-vintage-port, warres-otima-10

Travessa Barao de Forrester, 4400-034 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Cockburn's Port ★ 4.5

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1815Symington Family EstatesVila Nova de Gaia

Founded in 1815 and Symington-owned since 2010, Cockburn's keeps the largest wooden Port cellar in Gaia and is best known for its Special Reserve Ruby.

Tasting: Guided tour of the largest wooden Port lodge in Gaia, with working coopers and tastings.

Signature wines: cockburns-special-reserve

Rua Serpa Pinto 346, 4400-307 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Sandeman ★ 4.5

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1790SograpeVila Nova de Gaia

Founded in 1790 and owned by Sogrape, Sandeman is one of the best-known Port and sherry houses, instantly recognised by its caped Don logo, with a flagship cellar in Gaia.

Tasting: Cellar tours led by guides in the Sandeman Don cape, with multiple tasting tiers.

Signature wines: sandeman-vintage-port, sandeman-20-year-old-tawny

Largo Miguel Bombarda 47, 4400-222 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Seixo (Sandeman) ★ 4.4

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1989SograpeTabuaco

Sandeman's Douro estate and visitor centre at Valenca do Douro in the Cima Corgo, with a modern winery and panoramic tasting terrace above the river.

Tasting: Sandeman's Cima Corgo visitor centre with terrace tastings overlooking the river.

Valenca do Douro, 5120-495 Tabuaco, Portugal → Book →

Ferreira ★ 4.6

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1751SograpeVila Nova de Gaia

Portugal's most historic native-owned Port house, founded in 1751 and now Sogrape-owned, Ferreira is bound up with the legendary Dona Antonia Adelaide Ferreira and the Douro's still-wine pioneer Barca Velha.

Tasting: Cellar tour and tasting in Gaia telling the story of Dona Antonia Ferreira.

Signature wines: ferreira-vintage-port, ferreira-dona-antonia-reserva

Avenida Ramos Pinto 70, 4400-082 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Calem ★ 4.3

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1859Kopke Group (formerly Sogevinus Fine Wines)Vila Nova de Gaia

Founded in 1859 and part of the Kopke Group (formerly Sogevinus), Calem runs one of Gaia's busiest riverside cellars, pairing tastings with an interactive Port museum and evening fado.

Tasting: Riverside cellar with a Port and Douro interpretive museum, tastings and fado nights.

Avenida Diogo Leite 344, 4400-111 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Kopke ★ 4.6

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1638Kopke Group (formerly Sogevinus Fine Wines)Vila Nova de Gaia

The oldest Port house, founded in 1638 and the namesake brand of the Kopke Group (formerly Sogevinus), Kopke is celebrated for its dated Colheita tawnies, poured by the glass at Casa Kopke on the Gaia riverfront.

Tasting: Casa Kopke tasting room with flights of aged Colheita tawnies by the glass.

Signature wines: kopke-colheita-tawny

Avenida Diogo Leite 312, 4400-111 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Burmester ★ 4.3

DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1750Kopke Group (formerly Sogevinus Fine Wines)Vila Nova de Gaia

Founded in 1750 and part of the Kopke Group (formerly Sogevinus), Burmester ages its Ports beside the Luis I bridge in Gaia and is known for its long-aged tawny styles.

Tasting: Cellar tour beside the Luis I bridge with tawny-focused tastings.

Largo Ponte Luiz I, 4400-111 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Niepoort ★ 4.7

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Amarela, Sousao, RabigatoFounded 1842Niepoort family (Dirk Niepoort)Vila Nova de Gaia

A family-owned house founded in 1842 and led by Dirk Niepoort, equally renowned for classic Vintage Port and a benchmark range of DOC Douro reds and whites such as Redoma and Charme.

Tasting: Family Port house; cellar visits by reservation, with Douro DOC and Port flights.

Signature wines: niepoort-vintage-port, niepoort-redoma-tinto

Rua Candido dos Reis 598, 4400-071 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Real Companhia Velha ★ 4.4

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, SousaoFounded 1756Silva Reis familyVila Nova de Gaia

Founded by royal charter in 1756, Real Companhia Velha (Royal Oporto) is Portugal's oldest wine company, with vast Gaia cellars and an expanding range of DOC Douro wines.

Tasting: Multilingual guided tour of the historic Gaia cellars ending in a Port tasting.

Rua Azevedo Magalhaes 314, 4430-952 Vila Nova de Gaia, Portugal → Book →

Croft (Quinta da Roeda) ★ 4.5

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1588The Fladgate PartnershipPinhao

One of the oldest Port houses, founded in 1588 and part of the Fladgate Partnership, Croft welcomes visitors at its Quinta da Roeda estate near Pinhao and created the original rose Port, Croft Pink.

Tasting: Vineyard tours, tastings and harvest treading at the Quinta da Roeda estate near Pinhao.

Signature wines: croft-vintage-port, croft-pink

Quinta da Roeda, 5085-016 Pinhao, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Noval ★ 4.8

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinto Cao, Sousao145 haFounded 1715AXA MillesimesPinhao

A single-quinta house above Pinhao owned by AXA Millesimes, Quinta do Noval is famed for its tiny ungrafted Nacional vineyard, source of one of the world's rarest and most expensive Vintage Ports.

Tasting: Estate visits and tastings at the Pinhao quinta, including the celebrated Nacional vineyard.

Signature wines: quinta-do-noval-nacional, quinta-do-noval-vintage-port

Vale de Mendiz, 5085-110 Pinhao, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Crasto ★ 4.8

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinto Cao, Viosinho74 haFounded 1615Roquette familySabrosa

A Roquette-family estate on the right bank between Regua and Pinhao, Quinta do Crasto is a leader of the Douro still-wine renaissance, famed for its old-vine field-blend reds.

Tasting: Tours, tastings, lunches and boat trips overlooking the river, by reservation.

Signature wines: quinta-do-crasto-vinha-maria-teresa, quinta-do-crasto-reserva-old-vines

Gouvinhas, 5060-063 Sabrosa, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Vale Meao ★ 4.8

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Barroca, Tinto Cao62 haFounded 1877Olazabal family (Francisco Olazabal)Douro Superior

Founded in 1877 by Dona Antonia Ferreira and run by her descendant Francisco Olazabal, this Douro Superior estate makes one of Portugal's most sought-after reds plus the Meandro second wine.

Tasting: Estate visits by appointment at the Douro Superior quinta.

Signature wines: quinta-do-vale-meao-tinto, meandro-do-vale-meao

Quinta do Vale Meao, 5150-501 Vila Nova de Foz Coa, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Vallado ★ 4.6

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Sousao, RabigatoFounded 1716Ferreira and Cabral familiesPeso da Regua

One of the oldest quintas in the Douro, dating to 1716 and owned by descendants of Dona Antonia Ferreira, Vallado pairs a design wine hotel at Regua with old-vine reds and the Adelaide top cuvee.

Tasting: Wine hotel with tours, tastings and a winery visit beside the Corgo river at Regua.

Signature wines: quinta-do-vallado-adelaide, quinta-do-vallado-reserva

Vilarinho dos Freires, 5050-364 Peso da Regua, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Vesuvio ★ 4.7

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Barroca, Tinta Amarela, Alicante Bouschet133 haFounded 1827Symington Family EstatesDouro Superior

A grand Douro Superior estate bought by the Symington family in 1989, Quinta do Vesuvio still treads its grapes by foot in granite lagares and makes a single-quinta Vintage Port every year it declares.

Tasting: Private full-day estate experience with foot-treading lagares, by reservation.

Signature wines: quinta-do-vesuvio-vintage-port

Lugar do Vesuvio, 5155-638 Numao, Vila Nova de Foz Coa, Portugal → Book →

Quinta Nova de Nossa Senhora do Carmo ★ 4.6

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinto CaoFounded 1764Amorim familyCima Corgo

An Amorim-family estate in the Cima Corgo at Covas do Douro, Quinta Nova is a pioneer of Douro wine tourism, combining a Relais & Chateaux winery hotel with a serious DOC Douro range.

Tasting: Relais & Chateaux winery hotel with tastings, vineyard walks and the Terracu's restaurant.

Signature wines: quinta-nova-grande-reserva, quinta-nova-referencia

Quinta Nova de Nossa Senhora do Carmo, 5085-222 Covas do Douro, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Portal ★ 4.4

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Moscatel Galego BrancoFounded 1994Mansilha familySabrosa

A Mansilha-family estate in the Pinhao river valley near Sabrosa, Quinta do Portal makes DOC Douro reds, Port and a rare Moscatel do Douro, with a striking architect-designed barrel cellar.

Tasting: Visits, tastings and stays at the estate, with a Siza Vieira-designed cellar.

Signature wines: quinta-do-portal-grande-reserva, portal-moscatel-do-douro

EN 323, 5060-020 Celeiros do Douro, Sabrosa, Portugal → Book →

Quinta de la Rosa ★ 4.6

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Sousao, ViosinhoFounded 1815Bergqvist familyPinhao

A Bergqvist-family estate on the river just below Pinhao, Quinta de la Rosa is a single-quinta producer of both Port and DOC Douro wines, with a wine hotel and restaurant on site.

Tasting: Riverside wine hotel near Pinhao with tours, tastings and the Cozinha da Clara restaurant.

Signature wines: quinta-de-la-rosa-tinto, quinta-de-la-rosa-vintage-port

Quinta de la Rosa, 5085-241 Pinhao, Portugal → Book →

Wine & Soul ★ 4.6

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Sousao, Tinta AmarelaFounded 2001Sandra Tavares da Silva and Jorge Serodio BorgesPinhao

A small, acclaimed Vale de Mendiz project from winemaking couple Sandra Tavares da Silva and Jorge Serodio Borges, Wine & Soul makes the old-vine Pintas red, Guru white and a 5G tawny Port.

Tasting: Boutique producer at Vale de Mendiz; tastings by appointment.

Signature wines: wine-and-soul-pintas, wine-and-soul-guru

Avenida Julio de Freitas, Vale de Mendiz 186, 5085-101 Pinhao, Portugal → Book →

Prats & Symington (Chryseia) ★ 4.7

DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1998Prats and Symington familiesCima Corgo

A 1998 partnership of the Symingtons of the Douro and Bruno Prats of Bordeaux, Prats & Symington makes the elegant Chryseia and its Post Scriptum second wine from Quinta de Roriz and Quinta da Perdiz.

Tasting: Douro DOC project of the Symington and Prats families; wines shown via Symington visits.

Signature wines: chryseia, post-scriptum-de-chryseia

Quinta de Roriz, Ervedosa do Douro, Sao Joao da Pesqueira, Cima Corgo, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Bomfim ★ 4.7

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Barroca, SousaoFounded 1896Symington Family EstatesPinhao

The Symington family's home quinta on the riverbank at Pinhao, source of Dow's finest Vintage Ports and an award-winning visitor centre at the heart of the Cima Corgo.

Tasting: Symington visitor centre on the river at Pinhao with Port and Douro DOC tasting tiers.

Quinta do Bomfim, 5085-060 Pinhao, Alijo, Portugal → Book →

Quinta da Pacheca ★ 4.5

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Viosinho, ArintoFounded 1738Maria Pacheco Serpa group (Pacheca)Lamego

An 18th-century estate at Cambres near Lamego on the south bank, Quinta da Pacheca was one of the first Douro quintas to bottle under its own name and now runs the Wine House Hotel with its landmark barrel rooms.

Tasting: Wine House Hotel with tours, tastings, harvest treading and wine-barrel rooms.

Signature wines: quinta-da-pacheca-reserva, pacheca-vintage-port

Rua Relogio do Sol 261, 5100-424 Cambres, Lamego, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Tedo ★ 4.4

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1992Bouchard familyECOCERTBaixo Corgo

An 18th-century estate at the confluence of the Tedo and Douro run by the Bouchard family, Quinta do Tedo farms organically and makes Port, DOC Douro reds and olive oil with river-view tastings.

Tasting: Organic estate at Folgosa with cellar tours, tastings, picnics and rooms.

Signature wines: quinta-do-tedo-vintage-port

Folgosa 5110-207, Douro Valley, Armamar, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Panascal (Fonseca) ★ 4.4

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Barroca, Tinta Roriz, Tinto CaoFounded 1992The Fladgate PartnershipTabuaco

Fonseca's source estate in the Tavora valley at Valenca do Douro, Quinta do Panascal opened to visitors in 1992 and offers an audio-guided walk through its terraced vineyards and traditional lagares.

Tasting: Self-guided audio tour of the vineyards and lagares with tastings, one of the Douro's first visitor estates.

Quinta do Panascal, 5120-496 Valenca do Douro, Tabuaco, Portugal → Book →

Quinta do Pego ★ 4.3

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1991Quinta do PegoTabuaco

A boutique wine hotel and estate perched above the Douro at Valenca do Douro, Quinta do Pego makes award-winning DOC Douro Grande Reserva reds and Vintage Port with daily tastings.

Tasting: Country wine hotel above the river with daily tastings of estate Douro and Port wines.

Signature wines: quinta-do-pego-grande-reserva

EN 222, Valenca do Douro, 5120-493 Tabuaco, Portugal → Book →

Quevedo ★ 4.3

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta Barroca, RabigatoFounded 1991Quevedo family (Oscar and Claudia Quevedo)Douro Superior

A five-generation family producer based at Quinta Senhora do Rosario near Sao Joao da Pesqueira, Quevedo makes approachable Port and DOC Douro wines and welcomes visitors at both winery and Gaia lodge.

Tasting: Family winery and vineyards at Sao Joao da Pesqueira plus a Gaia lodge; tastings by appointment.

Signature wines: quevedo-vintage-port

Vallegre (Quinta da Vista Alegre) ★ 4.1

DOC Porto and DOC DouroTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinta BarrocaFounded 1950Sampaio family (Vallegre, Vinhos do Porto)Cima Corgo

A family Port producer based at Quinta da Vista Alegre near Pinhao, Vallegre bottles Port and DOC Douro wines from its Cima Corgo and Douro Superior vineyards.

Tasting: Family Port company with the Quinta da Vista Alegre estate near Pinhao.

Signature wines: vallegre-vintage-port

Quinta da Vista Alegre, 5085-212 Pinhao, Sabrosa, Portugal → Book →

Domingos Alves de Sousa ★ 4.5

DOC Douro and DOC PortoTouriga Nacional, Touriga Franca, Tinta Roriz, Tinto Cao, SousaoFounded 1987Alves de Sousa familyBaixo Corgo

A five-generation Baixo Corgo family producer led from Quinta da Gaivosa at Santa Marta de Penaguiao, Alves de Sousa is a DOC Douro specialist known for the Gaivosa and old-vine Abandonado reds.

Tasting: Family estate at Santa Marta de Penaguiao with tours and tastings, booking advised.

Quinta da Romaneira ★ 4.5

A large historic estate between Pinhao and Alijo at Cotas, Quinta da Romaneira pairs a five-star hotel with DOC Douro reds and Port across one of the region's most scenic stretches of river.

Tasting: 400-hectare estate with a luxury hotel, restaurant, tastings and river activities at Cotas.

Signature wines: quinta-da-romaneira-tinto

Cotas, 5070-252 Alijo, Portugal → Book →

Quinta da Avessada ★ 4.2

A family estate on the Favaios plateau with an interactive wine museum, Quinta da Avessada showcases the sweet Moscatel de Favaios as well as Douro reds and Port through immersive tastings.

Vineyards in Douro Valley, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Douro Valley for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Douro Valley is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Douro Valley estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Douro Valley cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Douro Valley tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Douro Valley?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Douro Valley rewards trust.
