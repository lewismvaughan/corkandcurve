Estates to visit in Tokaj

Royal Tokaji ★ 4.8

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályFounded 1990Mád

Founded in 1990 by Hugh Johnson and partners, Royal Tokaji pioneered the post-1989 private revival of single-vineyard Tokaj Aszú from Mád's historic dűlők.

Tasting: Cellar visits and tastings by appointment at the Mád winery; contact via website.

Signature wines: royal-tokaji-mezes-maly-aszu-6-puttonyos, royal-tokaji-nyulaszo-aszu-6-puttonyos, royal-tokaji-szt-tamas-aszu-6-puttonyos, royal-tokaji-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: The Mézes Mály single-vineyard Aszú is the estate's flagship sweet expression; book the cellar visit through the estate website.

Rákóczi utca 35, 3909 Mád, Hungary → Book →

Disznókő ★ 4.7

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga Muskotály, Zéta104 haFounded 1992AXA MillésimesMezőzombor

Acquired in 1992 by AXA Millésimes, Disznókő is the largest single contiguous vineyard estate in Tokaj, producing Aszú and the rare Eszencia from 104 hectares at Mezőzombor.

Tasting: Guided cellar tours and tastings by appointment; guesthouse on site for overnight stays.

Signature wines: disznoko-aszu-6-puttonyos, disznoko-aszu-5-puttonyos, disznoko-eszencia, disznoko-inspiracio-furmint

Tip: Book the cellar tour well in advance; the estate's guesthouse is an atmospheric overnight option in the region.

3931 Mezőzombor, Külterület hrsz.0202, Hungary → Book →

Tokaj Oremus ★ 4.7

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályFounded 1993Tempos Vega SiciliaTolcsva

Oremus was acquired by Tempos Vega Sicilia in 1993 and is based in Tolcsva, where ancient 13th-century cellars underpin a modern winery producing both dry Furmint and Tokaji Aszú.

Tasting: Cellar tours and tastings by appointment at Tolcsva.

Signature wines: oremus-mandolas-furmint, oremus-aszu-5-puttonyos, oremus-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: The Mandolás dry Furmint is the estate's flagship white and a key reference for the dry Furmint revival.

Bajcsy-Zsilinszky Endre utca 45, 3934 Tolcsva, Hungary → Book →

Szepsy ★ 4.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályMád

The Szepsy family estate in Mád is the original force behind the single-vineyard Tokaj revival; meticulous work in the historic dűlők of Mád defines the dry Furmint movement.

Tasting: Visits by appointment only.

Signature wines: szepsy-furmint, szepsy-uradja-furmint, szepsy-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: Visits are strictly by prior arrangement; plan months ahead for harvest-season appointments.

Batthyány utca 59, 3909 Mád, Hungary → Book →

Holdvölgy ★ 4.5

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályMád

Set in central Mád in a 500-year-old cellar running almost 2 km underground, Holdvölgy produces precise single-vineyard dry Furmint and Aszú wines from the premier dűlők of the Mád basin.

Tasting: Open Sun-Thu 10:00-15:00, Fri-Sat 10:00-17:00; online booking via website.

Signature wines: holdvolgy-notabene-furmint, holdvolgy-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: The underground cellar tour is atmospheric; book the tasting package in advance.

Árpád utca 13, 3909 Mád, Hungary → Book →

Patricius Winery ★ 4.3

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga Muskotály, Zéta, Kövérszőlő68 haFounded 2000Bodrogkisfalud

Founded in 2000 in Bodrogkisfalud, Patricius cultivates 68 hectares of traditional Tokaj varieties including the rare Kövérszőlő, producing the full range from dry Furmint to Aszú.

Tasting: Monday to Friday 08:00-16:00, year-round; advance booking recommended.

Signature wines: patricius-furmint-dry, patricius-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: One of the few estates that bottles Kövérszőlő as a varietal; a rare Tokaj style worth asking about during any visit.

Várhegy dűlő, 3917 Bodrogkisfalud, Hungary → Book →

Béres Vineyards and Winery ★ 4.4

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga Muskotály45 haErdőbénye

Béres Vineyards and Winery occupies 45 hectares in the Bénye Basin at the geographical centre of Tokaj, producing single-vineyard dry whites and Aszú from terraced volcanic soils.

Tasting: Winery visits and tastings by appointment.

Signature wines: beres-locse-furmint, beres-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: The Lőcse single-vineyard Furmint is the estate's flagship expression of the volcanic rhyolite tuff terroir.

Bénye-völgy, 3932 Erdőbénye, Hungary → Book →

Kikelet Pince ★ 4.4

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályFounded 2002Winemaker Stéphanie BereczTarcal

Founded in 2002 by Loire-trained winemaker Stéphanie Berecz and her husband Zsolt in Tarcal, Kikelet crafts mineral dry whites and late-harvest wines from volcanic-rock cellars carved into the village bedrock.

Tasting: Winery and cellar visits by appointment in Tarcal.

Signature wines: kikelet-furmint, kikelet-harslevelu, kikelet-late-harvest

Tip: Stéphanie Berecz was awarded Winemaker of Winemakers by her peers in 2014; the natural-rock cellar environment is a highlight of any visit.

Könyves Kálmán utca 62, 3915 Tarcal, Hungary → Book →

Barta Winery ★ 4.4

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűMád

Barta Winery in Mád crafts single-vineyard Furmint from the historic Öreg Király dűlő and structured Aszú from meticulously tended volcanic hillside plots above the village.

Tasting: Wine shop and cellar tastings at Rákóczi utca 83, Mád; book via email or phone.

Signature wines: barta-oreg-kiraly-furmint, barta-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: The Rákóczi utca wine shop doubles as the tasting room; the Öreg Király (Old King) Furmint is the estate's signature white.

Rákóczi utca 83, 3909 Mád, Hungary → Book →

Sauska Tokaj ★ 4.3

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályRátka

The Sauska family's Tokaj winery, with a new Rátka facility opened in 2023, produces sparkling Furmint, dry whites and Aszú alongside the estate's Villány reds under the same family label.

Tasting: Winery tastings and visits at the Rátka facility, opened November 2023.

Signature wines: sauska-furmint, sauska-tokaj-aszu

Tip: The traditional-method sparkling Furmint is an unusual and rewarding expression of the grape's high natural acidity.

2722 hrsz, 3939 Rátka, Hungary → Book →

Tokaj Nobilis ★ 4.4

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű7 haFounded 2000Winemaker Sarolta BárdosBodrogkeresztúr

Tokaj Nobilis was founded in 2000 in Bodrogkeresztúr by winemaker Sarolta Bárdos and her husband Péter Molnár; the 7-hectare estate focuses on single-vineyard expressions and also offers guesthouse accommodation.

Tasting: Winery visits, tastings and accommodation in Bodrogkeresztúr; booking via website.

Signature wines: tokaj-nobilis-barakonyi-furmint, tokaj-nobilis-aszu

Tip: Sarolta Bárdos is one of the region's most compelling advocates for dry Furmint; the Barakonyi plot delivers notably saline minerality.

Bodrogkeresztúr, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Pendits ★ 4.4

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályBiodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalAbaújszántó

Pendits in Abaújszántó became Hungary's first Demeter-certified biodynamic winery in 2011 and the region's first certified organic estate in 2008, producing low-intervention Furmint and rare Aszú Essencia.

Tasting: Winery visits by appointment in Abaújszántó.

Signature wines: pendits-furmint, pendits-aszu-essencia

Tip: The first Hungarian winery to achieve both Demeter biodynamic and organic certification; a reference point for natural-leaning Tokaj.

Béke utca 111, 3881 Abaújszántó, Hungary → Book →

Bott Pince ★ 4.3

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű6 haFounded 2005ORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalBodrogkisfalud

Bott Pince is a 6-hectare family estate founded in 2005, farming six single vineyards across Tokaj with no herbicides, no enzymes and spontaneous fermentation from native yeasts.

Tasting: Visits by appointment only; contact via email.

Signature wines: bott-csontos-furmint, bott-teleki-furmint

Tip: With only 6 hectares split among six named plots, each bottling is genuinely single-site; the Csontos from Erdőbénye-Olaszliszka slopes is the most characteristic.

Bodrogkisfalud, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Erzsébet Pince ★ 4.2

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályFounded 1989Winemaker Erzsébet PrácserTokaj

Founded in 1989 on Tokaj's historic wine merchants' street by Erzsébet Prácser, one of the region's first female winemakers, this family cellar produces traditional dry and sweet Tokaj styles.

Tasting: Cellar tastings in the heart of Tokaj town; booking via website.

Signature wines: erzsebet-furmint-dry, erzsebet-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: The cellar sits on Bem Street, the historic wine trading street of Tokaj town; the family's personal history spans the transition from state to private winemaking.

Bem József utca 16, 3910 Tokaj, Hungary → Book →

Hímesudvar ★ 4.0

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga Muskotály3 haFounded 1983Tokaj

Established in 1983 in a 500-year-old building on Bem Street in Tokaj town, Hímesudvar produces small-batch Furmint, Hárslevelű and Sárga Muskotály from 3.5 hectares of estate vines.

Tasting: Cellar visits in Tokaj town centre; five-minute walk from the main square.

Signature wines: himesudvar-furmint, himesudvar-harslevelu

Tip: The historic building once served as a hunting lodge for King János Szapolyai; the cellar is among the oldest continually operated in the region.

Bem József utca 2, 3910 Tokaj, Hungary → Book →

Gizella Pince ★ 4.3

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályTokaj

Gizella Pince produces wines from 11 different Tokaj vineyards; Furmint dominates at 62% of plantings, including the historically documented Szil-völgy reconstruction project dating back to 1612.

Tasting: Winery visits by appointment in Tokaj town.

Signature wines: gizella-furmint-harslevelu, gizella-szil-volgy-furmint

Tip: The reconstruction of the historically documented Szil-völgy vineyard site produces very limited quantities worth seeking out at the cellar.

Dienes Pál utca 78, 3910 Tokaj, Hungary → Book →

Grand Tokaj ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga Muskotály, ZétaFounded 1948Hungarian StateTolcsva

Grand Tokaj, formerly Tokaj Kereskedőház, is the state-owned large-format producer, rebranded in 2016, producing the full range of Tokaj styles at commercial scale and widely distributed internationally.

Tasting: Winery visits and group tastings; contact via website.

Signature wines: grand-tokaj-aszu-5-puttonyos, grand-tokaj-furmint

Tip: Grand Tokaj's wines are widely available internationally and a practical entry point for exploring the region's principal styles.

Tolcsva, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Füleky Pince ★ 4.0

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályBodrogkeresztúr

Füleky Pince is a family estate in Bodrogkeresztúr at the Bodrog-Tisza confluence, a village whose morning mists reliably stimulate noble rot; the estate produces Furmint and Aszú from these fog-prone slopes.

Tasting: Cellar visits and tastings in Bodrogkeresztúr by appointment.

Signature wines: fuleky-furmint, fuleky-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Bodrogkeresztúr's autumn fogs produce some of the region's most consistently botrytised grapes; the village is a good base for understanding the geographical origins of Tokaji Aszú.

Bodrogkeresztúr, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Demeter Zoltán ★ 4.3

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűWinemaker Zoltán DemeterMád

Zoltán Demeter is a boutique Mád producer making concentrated single-vineyard Furmint and precise Aszú from tiny parcels on the volcanic slopes above the village.

Tasting: Visits by prior appointment only.

Signature wines: demeter-zoltan-furmint, demeter-zoltan-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: Production is very small; wines are primarily available through specialist importers and local Mád tasting rooms.

Mád, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Zsirai Pince ★ 4.1

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályTarcal

Zsirai Pince in Tarcal grows vines on the southern slopes of Kopasz-hegy including plots adjacent to the historic Mézes Mály dűlő, one of Tokaj's first-class classified vineyards, producing full-bodied dry Furmint and Aszú.

Tasting: Winery visits by appointment in Tarcal.

Signature wines: zsirai-mezes-maly-furmint, zsirai-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: Tarcal is the southernmost major wine village in Tokaj; its maximum sun exposure produces noticeably richer fruit than the cooler northern villages.

Tarcal, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Balassa Pince ★ 4.0

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályTolcsva

Balassa Pince is based in Tolcsva, home village of Oremus, producing dry Furmint and textured Szamorodni from the rolling volcanic hillsides and ancient cellar tunnels of the Tolcsva valley.

Tasting: Cellar visits and tastings in Tolcsva by appointment.

Signature wines: balassa-furmint, balassa-szamorodni

Tip: Tolcsva's deep historic cellar network runs for hundreds of metres through volcanic tuff; an atmospheric location for any tasting.

Tolcsva, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Kovács Nimród Winery ★ 4.1

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga Muskotály, KabarWinemaker Nimród KovácsMezőzombor

Kovács Nimród Winery in Mezőzombor produces a range of Tokaj styles including the rare Kabar variety alongside Furmint and Hárslevelű under the winemaker's own label.

Tasting: Winery visits and tastings in Mezőzombor by appointment.

Signature wines: kovacs-nimrod-furmint, kovacs-nimrod-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: One of the few estates that bottles Kabar (Hárslevelű × Bouvier crossing) as a varietal wine; a rare Tokaj style worth seeking out.

Mezőzombor, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Almási Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályHercegkút

Almási Pince is a family estate in Hercegkút, a Swabian settlement whose famous cellar street with barrel-roofed cellars built into the hillside creates one of Tokaj's most unusual and photogenic village streetscapes.

Tasting: Family cellar in Hercegkút's Swabian cellar street; visits by appointment.

Signature wines: almasi-pince-furmint-dry, almasi-pince-harslevelu

Tip: Hercegkút's Swabian cellar street is a short walk from the main road; the uniform barrel-vaulted cellar doors built into the hill are a memorable architectural sight unlike anything else in Tokaj.

Hercegkút, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Vincze Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűErdőbénye

Vincze Pince is a family estate in the elevated forest-village of Erdőbénye, producing racy dry Furmint and late-harvest wine from cool-climate volcanic plots at higher elevations within the appellation.

Tasting: Family estate; visits by appointment.

Signature wines: vincze-pince-furmint-dry, vincze-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Erdőbénye's altitude and forest cover produce Furmint with unusually high natural acidity; compare with a Mád or Tarcal bottling to appreciate the contrasting terroir.

Erdőbénye, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Fekete Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűSárospatak

Fekete Pince in Sárospatak produces Furmint and Aszú from plots in the northern sector of the Tokaj appellation, where cooler temperatures extend the growing season and produce wines of heightened natural acidity.

Tasting: Cellar tastings in Sárospatak; visits by arrangement.

Signature wines: fekete-pince-furmint-dry, fekete-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Sárospatak's 16th-century Renaissance castle complex is within walking distance of the cellar; combine the tasting with a visit to the historic castle and Calvinist college.

Sárospatak, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Harsányi Pince ★ 3.8

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűSátoraljaújhely

Harsányi Pince is a family producer in Sátoraljaújhely, the northernmost town of the Tokaj appellation bordering Slovakia, growing Furmint and Hárslevelű on sheltered south-facing terraces in the appellation's coolest zone.

Tasting: Family winery in Sátoraljaújhely; visits by prior arrangement.

Signature wines: harsanyi-pince-furmint-dry, harsanyi-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Sátoraljaújhely is the most northerly village of the appellation; wines from here show a more restrained, higher-acid profile than those from the sunnier Mád basin.

Sátoraljaújhely, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Katona Pince ★ 3.8

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályAbaújszántó

Katona Pince is a family producer in Abaújszántó, the southernmost wine village of Tokaj-Hegyalja, producing Furmint, Sárga Muskotály and Aszú from sheltered south-facing volcanic slopes.

Tasting: Cellar visits in Abaújszántó by appointment.

Signature wines: katona-pince-furmint-dry, katona-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Abaújszántó sits at the southern gateway to the appellation; its wines show warmer, riper fruit character than the central Mád villages.

Abaújszántó, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Bognár Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályTokaj

Bognár Pince is a traditional family estate in Tokaj town producing dry Furmint and both styles of Szamorodni alongside Aszú, representing the full range of styles historically associated with the eponymous wine capital.

Tasting: Family cellar in Tokaj town; visits by prior arrangement.

Signature wines: bognar-pince-furmint-dry, bognar-pince-szamorodni-edes

Tip: Dry Szamorodni (száraz) is one of Tokaj's most underrated styles; aged examples develop sherry-like oxidative complexity and pair well with aged cheese.

Tokaj, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Papp Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályTarcal

Papp Pince is a family producer in Tarcal growing Furmint, Hárslevelű and Sárga Muskotály on south-facing volcanic terraces that define this village's fuller, more concentrated style of wine.

Tasting: Family winery; visits by prior arrangement in Tarcal.

Signature wines: papp-pince-furmint-dry, papp-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Tarcal's Mézes Mály (Honey Meadow) is among the historically classified first-class dűlők; ask which plots the family tends during any visit.

Tarcal, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Jäger Pince ★ 4.0

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályMád

Jäger Pince is a boutique family estate in Mád focusing on single-parcel Furmint and small-volume Aszú from the village's historic volcanic clay and rhyolite tuff soils.

Tasting: Cellar visits by appointment in Mád.

Signature wines: jager-pince-furmint-dry, jager-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: In a village of many cellars, Jäger Pince offers an unhurried, personal tasting experience.

Mád, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Hétényi Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályTokaj

Hétényi Pince is a small family estate in Tokaj town producing dry Furmint, Sárga Muskotály and late-harvest wine from plots across the PDO, continuing a multigenerational family tradition in the wine capital.

Tasting: Family cellar tastings in Tokaj town; contact in advance.

Signature wines: hetenyi-pince-furmint-dry, hetenyi-pince-late-harvest

Tip: Tokaj town has several family cellars along its historic wine streets; combining two or three tasting visits in a single afternoon on foot is entirely feasible.

Tokaj, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Erdei Pince ★ 3.8

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályErdőbénye

Erdei Pince is a boutique estate in the hillside village of Erdőbénye set among forested ridges, producing mineral Furmint and fragrant Sárga Muskotály from sheltered volcanic plots at altitude.

Tasting: Family winery; visits by appointment.

Signature wines: erdei-pince-furmint-dry, erdei-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Erdőbénye's elevated, forested position gives its wines a distinctive cool-climate lift compared to the valley-floor villages.

Erdőbénye, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Tokaj Hétszőlő ★ 4.1

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályMád

Tokaj Hétszőlő, now part of the Grand Tokaj family, takes its name from one of Mád's historically documented vineyard sites, producing Aszú from clay-volcanic soils documented since the 17th century.

Tasting: Part of the Grand Tokaj group; visits by arrangement via Grand Tokaj.

Signature wines: tokaj-hetszolos-furmint, tokaj-hetszolos-aszu

Tip: The Hétszőlő name (seven vineyards) appears in historical Tokaj documents from the early 1600s; the estate carries the weight of the region's long documented viticultural history.

Mád, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Dorógi Pince ★ 3.8

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűBodrogkisfalud

Dorógi Pince is a small family winery in Bodrogkisfalud producing dry Furmint and Aszú from clay-volcanic slopes bordering the Bodrog river, where autumn fog reliably creates botrytis conditions.

Tasting: Family winery; visits by appointment.

Signature wines: dorogi-pince-furmint-dry, dorogi-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: A classic small-producer experience in a village that receives far fewer visitors than Mád or Tokaj town.

Bodrogkisfalud, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Szepsy Jr Pince ★ 4.7

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűWinemaker István Szepsy Jr.Mád

István Szepsy Jr. continues the Szepsy family tradition at the Mád estate, producing single-vineyard Furmint and Aszú with the same rigorous approach to dűlő selection that defined the original Szepsy revival.

Tasting: Visits by appointment only in Mád.

Signature wines: szepsy-jr-furmint-nyulaszo, szepsy-jr-aszu-6-puttonyos

Tip: The father-and-son succession at Szepsy is one of the clearest examples of generational knowledge transfer in Tokaj winemaking.

Mád, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Molnár Pince ★ 3.8

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűOlaszliszka

Molnár Pince in Bodrogolaszi on the Bodrog riverbank maximises autumn fog exposure for reliable botrytis; the family makes dry Furmint and traditional sweet styles from Bodrog-riverside plots.

Tasting: Cellar visits by appointment in Bodrogolaszi.

Signature wines: molnar-pince-furmint-dry, molnar-pince-szamorodni-edes

Tip: The Bodrog riverbanks near Olaszliszka and Bodrogolaszi produce some of the most consistently botrytised grapes in the whole appellation.

Bodrogolaszi, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Lapis Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűOlaszliszka

Lapis Pince is a small family producer in Olaszliszka, a Bodrog-valley village whose riverine fog creates ideal botrytis conditions; the estate produces Furmint and Aszú in a traditional style.

Tasting: Family cellar visits by appointment in Olaszliszka.

Signature wines: lapis-pince-furmint-dry, lapis-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Olaszliszka sits directly on the Bodrog and experiences heavy morning mist in autumn; harvest-time visits are particularly atmospheric.

Olaszliszka, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Várhelyi Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, Hárslevelű, Sárga MuskotályTokaj

Várhelyi Pince is a family cellar in central Tokaj town producing dry Furmint and sweet Aszú from volcanic plots across the region, with multigenerational roots in the historic wine-street quarter.

Tasting: Cellar tastings in Tokaj town; contact in advance.

Signature wines: varhelyi-pince-furmint-dry, varhelyi-pince-aszu-5-puttonyos

Tip: Located in the historic Bem Street wine quarter of Tokaj; an atmospheric traditional cellar tasting.

Tokaj, Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén, Hungary → Book →

Winesor Pince ★ 3.9

Tokaj PDOFurmint, HárslevelűSárospatak

Winesor Pince is based in the historic town of Sárospatak, associated with Hungarian Calvinist culture and winemaking, producing dry Furmint and Aszú from the northern sector of the appellation.

Tasting: Winery visits and tastings by appointment in Sárospatak.

Malatinszky Tokaj produces mineral dry Furmint from Mád-area plots, complementing the same estate family known for reds from Villány; a rare dual-appellation Hungarian producer.

Vineyards in Tokaj, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Tokaj for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Tokaj is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Tokaj estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Tokaj cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Tokaj tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.
