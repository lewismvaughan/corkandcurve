Natural wine around the world

12 editor-picked natural producers across 4 regions.

A guide to natural wine worth seeking out, by region.

Natural wine in Mosel

Weingut Stein ★ 4.4

Mosel PradikatsweinRieslingStein family (Ulrich and Peter Stein)Winemaker Ulrich SteinNaturalMittelmosel

Ulli Stein's individualist estate around St. Aldegund and Alf, known for low-intervention, sometimes provocative Rieslings from steep, near-abandoned slate sites and a hilltop tasting house.

Tasting: Tastings at Haus Waldfrieden above Alf and at the Bullay estate; by arrangement.

Brautrockstrasse 40, 56859 Bullay, Germany → Book →

Natural wine in Ribera del Duero

Goyo García Viadero ★ 4.7

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorFounded 2001Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalLa Horra

A natural-wine pioneer in the Burgos sector working with old pre-phylloxera Tinto Fino and Albillo Mayor vines, producing low-intervention wines with minimal sulfur under the Villalbilla and other labels.

Villalbilla de Gumiel, 09314 Villalbilla de Gumiel, Burgos, Spain →

Bodegas Mas Que Vinos ★ 4.4

DO Ribera del DueroTinto Fino, Albillo MayorBiodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalValbuena de Duero

A biodynamic-practicing estate at Valbuena de Duero producing the Ercavio and Alma del Duero ranges from Tinto Fino and Albillo Mayor with a natural wine philosophy, minimal intervention and low added sulphur.

Calle Cabeza Gorda s/n, 47359 Valbuena de Duero, Valladolid, Spain →

Natural wine in Sicily

Marco De Bartoli ★ 4.8

Marsala DOC, Pantelleria DOCGrillo, ZibibboFounded 1978NaturalMarsala DOC

Marco De Bartoli founded this artisan Marsala house in 1978, working primarily with Grillo to revive traditional un-fortified and minimally fortified styles. The estate at Samperi is the spiritual home of authentic Marsala craft.

Tasting: Guided tours and tastings at the Samperi cellar near Marsala. Weekday visits by appointment. Contact by phone (+39 0923 962093) or email.

Signature wines: de-bartoli-vecchio-samperi, de-bartoli-marsala-superiore-oro, de-bartoli-bukkuram-sole-d-agosto

Contrada Samperi 292, 91025 Marsala (TP), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Azienda Vinicola Calabretta ★ 4.7

Etna DOCNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, CatarrattoFounded 1900Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalEtna DOC

Founded in 1900, Calabretta is one of Etna's oldest family wineries. The fourth generation farms 12.5 hectares of ungrafted old vines with copper and sulphur only, producing low-intervention wines from Nerello Mascalese that rank among the most authentic expressions of the north slope.

Tasting: Visits by appointment at the Randazzo estate. Contact via calabretta.net.

Signature wines: calabretta-gaio-gaio, calabretta-etna-rosso, calabretta-vigne-vecchie

Contrada Calderara, 95036 Randazzo (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Frank Cornelissen ★ 4.9

Etna DOC, IGP Terre SicilianeNerello Mascalese, Nerello Cappuccio, Catarratto, Minella, CarricanteFounded 2001Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalEtna DOC

Belgian natural-wine pioneer Frank Cornelissen established his estate on Etna's north slope in 2001, working without sulphur at all. His Magma cuvee from ungrafted pre-phylloxera Nerello vines above 1,000 metres is among Sicily's most sought-after cult wines.

Tasting: Five tasting experiences from the Classic (5 wines, EU40) to the Magma private (6 crus including Grand Vin Magma, EU1,400 for up to 6 people). E-bike vineyard tours available. Book via pr@frankcornelissen.it.

Signature wines: cornelissen-magma, cornelissen-munjebel-rosso, cornelissen-munjebel-bianco

Via Canonico Zumbo 1, Passopisciaro, 95012 Castiglione di Sicilia (CT), Sicily, Italy → Book →

COS ★ 4.9

Cerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG, Vittoria DOCNero d'Avola, Frappato, Grecanico, ZibibboFounded 1980Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalCerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG

Founded in 1980 by Giambattista Cilia, Cirino Strano and Giusto Occhipinti, COS pioneered biodynamic farming and amphora fermentation in Sicily. Their Cerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG from Nero d'Avola and Frappato is the defining expression of the appellation.

Tasting: Winery visits by reservation only. Contact via the COS website or telephone (+39 0932 876145).

Signature wines: cos-cerasuolo-di-vittoria, cos-pithos-rosso, cos-rami

SP3 Acate-Chiaramonte Km 14,300, 97019 Vittoria (RG), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Arianna Occhipinti ★ 4.9

Cerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG, Vittoria DOC, IGP Terre SicilianeFrappato, Nero d'AvolaFounded 2004Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalCerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG

Arianna Occhipinti started with one hectare along the SP68 road in 2004 and has grown to 40 hectares farmed organically and biodynamically. Her SP68 Rosso blending Frappato and Nero d'Avola is among Italy's most celebrated natural wines.

Tasting: Visits by reservation year-round, mornings or afternoons. Vineyard walk, cellar tour and wine and oil tasting with olives and bruschetta. Languages: Italian, English, French. Contact +39 0932 1865519.

Signature wines: occhipinti-sp68-rosso, occhipinti-sp68-bianco, occhipinti-siccagno

SP68 Vittoria-Pedalino Km 3,3, 97019 Vittoria (RG), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Marco De Bartoli - Bukkuram (Pantelleria) ★ 4.8

Passito di Pantelleria DOC, Moscato di Pantelleria DOCZibibboFounded 1978NaturalPantelleria DOC

Marco De Bartoli's Pantelleria estate at Bukkuram produces the Sole d'Agosto passito from Zibibbo vines trained in the alberello pantesco system. The wine is a benchmark of minimal-intervention Pantelleria passito style.

Tasting: Visits by appointment on Pantelleria. Contact via the Marco De Bartoli website.

Signature wines: de-bartoli-bukkuram-sole-d-agosto

Via S. Michele 41, 91017 Pantelleria (TP), Sicily, Italy → Book →

COS Pithos (amphora wines) ★ 4.8

IGP Terre SicilianeGrecanico, Nero d'Avola, Frappato, ZibibboFounded 1980Biodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalCerasuolo di Vittoria DOCG

COS Pithos wines are fermented and aged in terracotta amphorae, reviving pre-industrial Sicilian vinification. The Pithos Bianco from Grecanico fermented on skins and the Pithos Rosso from Nero d'Avola are among Italy's most influential natural wines.

Tasting: Amphora wines tasted alongside DOCG range at the COS estate. By reservation.

Signature wines: cos-pithos-bianco, cos-pithos-rosso

SP3 Acate-Chiaramonte Km 14,300, 97019 Vittoria (RG), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Serragghia (Pantelleria) ★ 4.7

Passito di Pantelleria DOCZibibboBiodynamic PracticingORGANIC CERTIFIEDNaturalPantelleria DOC

Gabrio Bini's Serragghia on Pantelleria is a cult natural-wine producer farming Zibibbo in the alberello pantesco system without sulphur. The Serragghia passito and natural Zibibbo whites are among Italy's most sought-after natural sweet and aromatic wines.

Tasting: Visits by appointment on Pantelleria. Contact via Gabrio Bini's Serragghia farm.

Signature wines: serragghia-passito-di-pantelleria

Contrada Khamma 132, 91017 Pantelleria (TP), Sicily, Italy → Book →

Natural wine in Veneto

Corte Sant'Alda ★ 4.8

Amarone della Valpolicella DOCGCorvina, Rondinella, MolinaraFounded 1986Marinella CameraniWinemaker Marinella CameraniDemeter CertifiedECOCERTNaturalValpolicella

Marinella Camerani's biodynamic estate in the Mezzane valley has been Demeter-certified since 2003. The Mithas Amarone and Soraigo Valpolicella are made with minimal intervention and long ageing in large oak.

Tasting: Estate visits and tastings by appointment at Mezzane di Sotto; biodynamic vineyard tours available.

Signature wines: corte-santalda-amarone-mithas, corte-santalda-soraigo

Via Capovilla 28, 37030 Mezzane di Sotto, Verona, Italy → Book →
