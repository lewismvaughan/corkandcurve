Wine Experiences in Wachau

Domäne Wachau Wine.Vineyard.Flight - Helicopter over UNESCO Vines ★ 4.9

Dürnstein

An aerial helicopter tour over the UNESCO World Heritage vineyard terraces of the Wachau, followed by a private tasting of Domäne Wachau's Smaragd Rieds at the Kellerschlössel. Reservations via the estate office.

Domäne Wachau Nr. 107, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria → Book →

Domäne Wachau Wine.Vineyard.Yacht - Private Danube Tasting ★ 4.8

Dürnstein

A private Danube cruise on the yacht 'Anatole' at 25 knots from Krems to Spitz, combining on-board tasting of Domäne Wachau's portfolio with views of the UNESCO vineyard terraces from the water. Reservations via the estate office.

Domäne Wachau Nr. 107, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria → Book →

Domäne Wachau Wine.Vineyard.Picnic ★ 4.7

Dürnstein

A self-guided Wachau vineyard picnic experience: collect a curated backpack of wines, regional snacks and a terrace blanket from the Vinothek and explore the Ried terraces between Dürnstein and Spitz at your own pace. Reservations via the estate office.

Domäne Wachau Nr. 107, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria → Book →

Nikolaihof Biodynamic Harvest & Cellar Experience ★ 4.9

Mautern

A hands-on harvest experience at Austria's oldest Demeter-certified biodynamic winery, guided by the Saahs family. Participants learn lunar-calendar viticulture, hand-harvest Grüner Veltliner or Riesling grapes, and taste wine from the Roman-foundation cellar with Christine Saahs' organic meal.

Nikolaigasse 3, 3512 Mautern an der Donau, Austria → Book →

Singerriedel Vineyard Hike - Spitz ★ 4.7

Spitz

A walking ascent through the Spitz vineyards to the Red Gate and the Singerriedel terrace, a named single-vineyard site above Spitz associated with Weingut Franz Hirtzberger. The hike offers panoramic views of the Danube bend and the entire UNESCO landscape.

Tourist Information Spitz, Mittergasse 3a, 3620 Spitz an der Donau, Austria → Book →

Steinfeder Night at Schloss Spitz - Wachauer Weinfrühling ★ 4.8

Spitz

The Steinfeder Night at Schloss Spitz is the flagship evening event of the annual Wachauer Weinfrühling: three wine bars set up in the castle grounds pour Vinea Wachau wines with live music and food until 01:00. A uniquely atmospheric late-evening Wachau wine experience.

Schloss Spitz, 3620 Spitz an der Donau, Austria → Book →

Steigenberger Torch-Lit Vineyard Hike ★ 4.5

Krems

An evening guided torch-lit hike through the Steigenberger Hotel's own hilltop vineyard above Krems, with views of the UNESCO Wachau valley illuminated at dusk. Wine pairings at the Smaragd bar conclude the experience.

Am Goldberg 2, 3500 Krems an der Donau, Austria → Book →

Domäne Wachau Wine.Vineyard.Safari - Jeep Vineyard Exploration ★ 4.7

Dürnstein

A naturalist-guided Jeep exploration of the steep UNESCO Wachau vineyard terraces, accessing areas unreachable on foot or bicycle. The tour combines geology, viticulture history and live tasting of single-vineyard wines with views across the Danube valley.

Domäne Wachau Nr. 107, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria → Book →

Domäne Wachau Wine.Vineyard.SUP - Stand-Up Paddleboard ★ 4.6

Dürnstein

A stand-up paddleboard session on the Danube between Dürnstein and Spitz, with the UNESCO vineyard terraces rising on both banks, followed by a winery tour and tasting at the Domäne Wachau Kellerschlössel. Combine river and vine in a single afternoon.

Domäne Wachau Nr. 107, 3601 Dürnstein, Austria → Book →

Gartenhotel Pfeffel Wine Weekend Package ★ 4.6

Dürnstein

A structured wine weekend package at Gartenhotel & Weingut Pfeffel that combines guided vineyard walks among Dürnstein's terraces, evening blind tastings of house-made brandies at the bar and spa treatments, with own-estate Wachau wines at every meal.

3601 Dürnstein, Wachau, Austria → Book →

Wine Experiences in Wachau, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Wachau for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Wachau is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Wachau estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Wachau cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Wachau tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Wachau?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Wachau rewards trust.
