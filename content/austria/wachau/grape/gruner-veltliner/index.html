Seven-generation Pichler estate in Oberloiben producing Smaragd Grüner Veltliner and Riesling from the Kellerberg and Loibenberg sites. 20 hectares planted half Grüner Veltliner, half Riesling.

Tasting: By appointment only; cellar visits and tastings for trade and press.

Nikolaihof Wachau ★ 4.9

Oldest documented winery in Austria (nearly 2,000 years of history) at Mautern on the south bank. Demeter biodynamic since 1971. Famous for the Vom Stein Riesling Smaragd and long-aged Hefeabzug Grüner Veltliner.

Tasting: Wine shop and tasting room at the estate; guided cellar tours available on request. Salon 77 restaurant on site.

Domäne Wachau ★ 4.6

Wachau DACGrüner Veltliner, Riesling440 haFounded 1938ORGANIC CERTIFIEDDürnstein

Wachau's largest estate, representing around 250 vintner families across 440 hectares. Founded 1938 as Winzergenossenschaft Wachau, renamed Domäne Wachau in 2008. Roman Horvath MW oversees the range alongside Heinz Frischengruber.

Tasting: Vinothek in Dürnstein open to visitors; tastings and direct sales. The Kellerschlössel Baroque cellar palace hosts tours.

Signature wines: domaene-wachau-achleiten-gruner-smaragd, domaene-wachau-kellerberg-riesling-smaragd, domaene-wachau-terrasen-federspiel-gruner
