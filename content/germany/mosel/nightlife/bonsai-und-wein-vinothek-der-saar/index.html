Bonsai & Wein - Vinothek der Saar ★ 4.3

Saarburg vinothek pouring around 20 top Saar growers including Egon Mueller and Geltz-Zilliken, with inexpensive self-pour tasting flights.

Wine bar in Mosel · Saarburg (Saar) Verified May 26, 2026

By the glass20
NeighbourhoodSaarburg (Saar)

The list: A Saarburg vinothek pouring around 20 of the Saar's best growers, from Egon Mueller-Scharzhof and Geltz-Zilliken to Van Volxem and Peter Lauer, in self-pour tasting flights

Tip: At a few euros for a three- or six-wine flight, it is the cheapest way to taste cult Saar names like Egon Mueller side by side.

Location

Address: Kunohof 20, 54439 Saarburg, Germany, Mosel
