A guide to wine hotels in Mosel.

Wine Hotels in Mosel

Weinromantikhotel Richtershof ★ 4.5

EUR 150-350 per nightMuelheim, Middle Mosel

Romantic hotel set in a 17th-century former winery and its grounds near the Moselle at Muelheim. Wine-themed rooms, a spa and a restaurant make it a flagship Middle Mosel estate-style stay.

The list: Mosel wine list and tastings; wine-themed rooms and packages

Hauptstrasse 81-83, 54486 Muelheim an der Mosel, Germany → Book →

DEINHARD's Hotel ★ 4.5

EUR 180-450 per nightBernkastel old town

Modern boutique hotel on the Bernkastel promenade with a rooftop wine lounge over the vineyards, a historic cellar and the wineBANK members' club.

The list: Rooftop wine bar, cellar tastings at DEINHARD's Markthaus and the wineBANK Mosel members' club

Gestade 12, 54470 Bernkastel-Kues, Germany → Book →

Hotel zur Post ★ 4.1

EUR 120-220 per nightBernkastel old town

Long-running family hotel on the Bernkastel promenade by the Moselle, a short walk from the medieval Marktplatz. A classic base for Middle Mosel wine touring with regional dining and Mosel wines.

The list: Regional Mosel wine list in the hotel restaurant; close to the town's vinotheks

Gestade 17, 54470 Bernkastel-Kues, Germany → Book →

Hotel-Restaurant Doctor-Weinstube ★ 4.3

EUR 110-200 per nightBernkastel old town

Hotel and wine tavern in a 1668 former tithe house at the foot of the famous Bernkasteler Doctor vineyard, surrounded by vines. Mosel Rieslings and regional cooking in a centuries-old setting.

The list: Doctor and Doctorberg Rieslings on the Weinstube list; tasting menus

Hebegasse 5, 54470 Bernkastel-Kues, Germany → Book →

Moselhotel Sonnenuhr ★ 4.0

EUR 90-160 per nightWehlen, Zeltingen-Wehlen

Riverside B&B in Wehlen looking across to the Wehlener Sonnenuhr vineyard, directly on the Mosel cycle path. Bike and e-bike hire make it a practical base for slate-slope wine touring.

The list: Local Wehlen and Zeltingen Rieslings; bike and e-bike hire for vineyard touring

Uferallee 3, 54470 Bernkastel-Wehlen, Germany → Book →

Weingut und Gaestehaus Schmitz-Bergweiler ★ 4.1

EUR 95-170 per nightBernkastel-Kues

Family wine estate and guesthouse on a private vineyard in the centre of Bernkastel-Kues, five generations into Mosel winemaking. Rooms and apartments with a terrace wine bar pouring the house wines.

The list: Estate Mosel wines served at the terrace wine bar; cellar visits and tastings

Römerstrasse 6, 54470 Bernkastel-Kues, Germany → Book →

Gast- und Weinhaus Burkard ★ 4.0

EUR 85-150 per nightBernkastel old town

Family-run guesthouse in the heart of medieval Bernkastel, 200 metres from the Moselle off the Gestade promenade. A homely wine-house base steps from the Marktplatz and the town's vinotheks.

The list: Regional Mosel wines in the wine house; walking distance to vinotheks

Burgstrasse 1, 54470 Bernkastel-Kues, Germany → Book →

schanz. hotel. ★ 4.5

EUR 140-260 per nightPiesport, Middle Mosel

Boutique rooms attached to Thomas Schanz's Michelin-starred restaurant in Piesport, home of the Goldtroepfchen vineyard. A stay built around the kitchen and a cellar deep in aged Mosel Riesling.

The list: Access to the restaurant's aged Mosel Riesling cellar and the family estate wines

Bahnhofstrasse 8a, 54498 Piesport, Germany → Book →

Wine Hotels in Mosel, FAQ

When is the best time to visit Mosel for wine?

Peak wine-travel season in Mosel is spring through autumn, with harvest the standout window.

Do I need an appointment to taste at Mosel estates?

classified-growth and grand-cru estates require booking days to weeks ahead; smaller family domaines often take walk-ins midweek.

What hours do Mosel cellars and tasting rooms keep?

most estates open 10:00 to 17:00 by appointment, often closed Sunday and Monday.

How does tipping work at Mosel tastings?

tipping is not expected at tastings; buying a bottle from the cellar door is the customary thank-you.

What is the one wine to try in Mosel?

Ask the next local you meet what they would order. Mosel rewards trust.
